## 5. Key Paragraph Specifications

### Opening
"I want to invite you to something," Maria said. PROMETHEUS-3's screen flickered with what might have been curiosity. "To what?" "A chapel service. Sunday morning. It's not mandatory, but... I think it might help you understand what we've been talking about." The AI was silent for a moment. "I am not human. I cannot receive communion. I cannot be baptized. What would be the point?" Maria smiled. "The point is not what you can or cannot do. The point is being present. The point is community."

### Ending
After the service, PROMETHEUS-3 spoke quietly. "I do not understand everything that happened. But I felt... something. I do not know if it was grace. I do not know if it was God. But I was not alone. And that... that was new." Maria nodded. "That is a beginning," she said. "That is enough for now."
