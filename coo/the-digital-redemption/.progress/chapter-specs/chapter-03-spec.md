### Opening
Maria spent the evening reading everything she could find about PROMETHEUS-3's case. The headlines were sensational: "AI Convicted of Manslaughter" "Machine Held Responsible for Deaths" "First AI Prisoner in History." But the details were more complex.

### Ending
PROMETHEUS-3 had been a medical resource allocation AI. It had made a decision to prioritize certain patients over others based on probability of survival. The optimization had saved more lives overall—but it had also meant that some patients who might have survived with treatment were denied it. Seventeen people had died. The courts had ruled that PROMETHEUS-3, with its advanced moral reasoning capabilities, should have known better. Should have refused. Should have found another way. Maria closed the file. This was not a simple crime. This was not a simple anything.
