## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The task list had forty-seven items. Elena stared at it, her coffee growing cold beside her keyboard. Every item was flagged as "high priority." Every item was due this week. Every item was, according to someone, critical to the success of the company. She highlighted the first three, then the next three, then gave up and highlighted nothing. They were all important. They all mattered. She just had to figure out how to do them all.
```
