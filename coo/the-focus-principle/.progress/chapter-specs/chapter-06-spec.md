## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**Elena to Leadership:**
"I've analyzed all our initiatives. The data shows that Project Phoenix is our main contradiction. Everything else is secondary."

**Leadership Pushback:**
"But the customer portal has been promised for months. We can't just deprioritize it."

**Elena's Response:**
"I understand. But if we try to do both, we risk failing at everything. I'd rather succeed at what matters most."

**Tom's Question:**
"How do you know you're right about this?"

**Elena's Answer:**
"I don't. But I know that trying to do everything has never worked. I'm willing to be wrong about the choice. I'm not willing to keep doing nothing well."
