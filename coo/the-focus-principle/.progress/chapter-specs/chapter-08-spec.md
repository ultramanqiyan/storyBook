## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**Other Team Lead:**
"My team is sitting idle because we're waiting on Phoenix. How long is this supposed to take?"

**Elena's Response:**
"I understand the frustration. But if we split focus now, we'll extend everyone's wait. Let me show you the timeline."

**Marcus's Teaching:**
"The main contradiction is like the keystone in an arch. Remove it, and the whole structure changes. But the other stones still matter. They just matter in order."

**Elena's Realization:**
"So secondary contradictions aren't things to ignore. They're things to address in sequence."
