## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**Team Member Resistance:**
"But I've been working on the portal for months. You're asking me to just... stop?"

**Elena's Response:**
"I'm asking you to pause. The portal will be there when Phoenix is done. Right now, we need your expertise here."

**Tom's Observation:**
"I've never seen a project move this fast. It's weird."

**Elena's Reflection:**
"It's not fast. It's just... not divided."

**Marcus's Guidance:**
"Focus isn't about speed. It's about direction. You're going in one direction now. That's the difference."
