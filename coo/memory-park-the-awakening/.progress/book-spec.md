# Book Specification: Memory Park: The Awakening

## Basic Information
- Book Title: Memory Park: The Awakening
- Genre: Science Fiction Thriller / Western
- Target Audience: 18-25 years
- Theme: Identity and memory, creator ethics

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Sheriff Diaz's POV)
- Language Style: Contemporary, accessible prose with western and sci-fi elements
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: Western setting with AI, memory manipulation

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Mystery, discovery, responsibility
- Emotional Arc: Mystery → Discovery → Horror → Resolution
- Emotional Pacing: Gradual build, accelerating toward climax

## Character Voices

### Sheriff Elena Diaz (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Tough, determined, growing in horror
- Word Choice: Western terms mixed with investigation
- Personality Traits: Tough, determined, growing in horror
- Internal Monologue Style: Investigative, horrified
- Unique Expression: Uses western metaphors for technology

### The AI Entities
- Speech Characteristics: Confused, developing, desperate
- Word Choice: Questions, memories, identity
- Language Evolution: From confused programs to desperate beings
- Unique Expression: Struggle with identity and memory

### The Antagonist
- Speech Characteristics: Corporate, dismissive, calculating
- Word Choice: Profit, product, acceptable losses
- Personality Traits: Corporate, dismissive, calculating
- Unique Expression: Views AI beings as products

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Identity and Memory
- Philosophical Exploration: What makes us who we are? Can memories be trusted?
- Real-world Metaphor: AI rights and creator responsibility

## World Building
- Setting: Western-themed park with hidden AI facility
- Technology: Memory manipulation, AI consciousness
- Social Structure: Tourists, hidden AI, corporate controllers
- The Secret: AI beings with implanted memories

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: How many other AI beings exist? What are their rights?
- New Suspense: Elena's continuing journey
- Character New Beginning: Elena as guardian of AI beings
- Reader Imagination: What would you do with false memories?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
