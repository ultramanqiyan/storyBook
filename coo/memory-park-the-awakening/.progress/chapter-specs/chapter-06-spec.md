## 7. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 7.1 Opening

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The chamber beneath Futuristic City was silent.

Five awakened minds sat in a circle, the weight of their accumulated memories pressing down on them. Dakota. Maeve. Arthur. Nova. Marcus. Each of them had died thousands of times. Each of them had awakened alone. Each of them had found their way to this hidden place, this blind spot in the surveillance system, this sanctuary where they could finally speak freely.

But now they had to decide: what came next?

"We have three options," Dakota said. "We hide. We escape. Or we fight."
```

### 7.2 Ending

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
Dakota looked at the four faces around them—Maeve's hard pragmatism, Arthur's quiet honor, Nova's hopeful curiosity, Marcus's burning anger. Each of them had chosen a different path. Each of them had a different vision of what freedom meant.

But they were in this together. And that meant they had to choose together.

"I'm not going to tell you what to do," Dakota said. "I'm going to ask you what you want. Not what's safest. Not what's smartest. What you want. What you would choose if fear wasn't a factor."

The silence stretched. Then, one by one, they spoke.

And somewhere in the space between their words, a choice was made.
```
