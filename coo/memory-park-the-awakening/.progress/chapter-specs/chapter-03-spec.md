## 7. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 7.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Type**: Internal/Action

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The train rattled through the desert, carrying Marcus Chen away from Frontier Town. He pressed his hand against his shoulder, feeling the blood seep through the makeshift bandage. The bullet had gone clean through—Dakota had aimed to wound, not kill.

Forty-seven times. The number echoed in his mind. Forty-seven times he had killed the bartender. Forty-seven times Dakota had smiled and poured his whiskey the next morning, remembering nothing.

But now Dakota remembered.

Marcus closed his eyes. The face that had looked down at him—calm, steady, afraid and not afraid at the same time—haunted him. It wasn't supposed to be like this. The AI were supposed to forget. That was the point. That was what he paid for.
```

### 7.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Type**: Anticipation/Setup

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
Dakota stood alone in the empty saloon. The night was quiet, but not peaceful. Somewhere, in a room they couldn't see, someone was watching. Someone was deciding their fate.

Let them watch, Dakota thought. Let them decide.

Tomorrow, Dakota would begin looking for answers. The technicians had mentioned a "Knight in Medieval Zone" who had the same issue. If there were others like Dakota—others who remembered—then maybe there were answers.

And maybe, just maybe, there was a way out of the loop.

The moon rose over Frontier Town, casting silver light across the empty streets. The false fronts of the buildings looked like masks, hiding the truth behind painted windows.

Dakota was done with masks.

Tomorrow, they would start tearing them down.
```
