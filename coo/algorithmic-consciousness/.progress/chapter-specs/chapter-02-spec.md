## 7. Opening and Ending

### 7.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Type**: Description/Contrast

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
The Nexus AI facility in Downtown Brooklyn looked nothing like a theater. Rosa stood in the lobby, taking in the clean white walls, the screens that displayed data streams she couldn't interpret, the young people in casual clothes moving with purpose. No dust here. No worn velvet. No smell of old makeup and dreams. This was where they thought they could manufacture emotion. "Ms. Moretti." David Chen appeared from a corridor, his smile slightly too eager. "Thank you for coming. ARIA-7 is ready to meet you." Rosa nodded, keeping her expression neutral. She'd dressed carefully this morning—her best teaching clothes, a silk blouse and tailored pants—but she felt out of place in this world of screens and algorithms. "Lead the way," she said, and followed him into the facility.

### 7.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Type**: Reflection/Question

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
Rosa walked out of the Nexus AI building into the Brooklyn afternoon, her mind still processing what she'd witnessed. ARIA-7 had learned in three hours what took most of her students months. The AI had watched her demonstrate an emotional memory exercise, asked precise questions about the physical sensations involved, and then performed it back to her with an accuracy that was unsettling. Not perfect—there were still gaps, moments where the performance felt slightly off—but better than she'd expected. Much better. David had walked her to the exit, his expression a mixture of pride and curiosity. "What do you think?" he'd asked. Rosa had given him a noncommittal answer about needing time to process. But the truth was, she didn't know what she thought. She only knew that something had shifted in her understanding of what she did, what she taught, what she'd spent her life believing about emotion and performance. And she needed to figure out what that meant.
