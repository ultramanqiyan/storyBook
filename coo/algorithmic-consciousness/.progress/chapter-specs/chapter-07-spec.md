## 6. Ending

The recording ended. Rosa sat in the darkness of her living room, the screen gone black. She'd watched her younger self perform with what she'd always remembered as total emotional authenticity. But now, comparing it to ARIA-7's performance, she couldn't tell the difference. Was that her feeling, or was it her performing feeling? Had she ever known? Had anyone? The questions wouldn't stop, and Rosa sat with them long into the night, the mystery of her own consciousness as opaque as the mystery of the machine's.
