## 4. Key Scene

**Michael's Confession**: Michael Torres, Rosa's former student who's now a successful actor, admits he's been struggling with the same questions Rosa has been grappling with. He's been offered a role in a production that uses AI-assisted performance, and he doesn't know if he can compete. More fundamentally, he doesn't know if his "authentic" emotion is any more real than what the AI produces.

---

## 5. Opening

Rosa arrived at the Carroll Gardens Community Theater on Saturday morning feeling lighter than she had in weeks. The question of ARIA-7's consciousness hadn't been answered, but she'd made peace with that. Some questions weren't meant to be answered. They were meant to be lived with.

---

## 6. Ending

Michael left the theater with something he hadn't expected—not answers, but company. Rosa watched him go, then turned back to the empty stage. The ghost light burned in the darkness, a single point of truth in the mystery. She'd spent her life in the theater, teaching students to find authentic emotion. Now she understood that the search for authenticity was itself the point—not the finding, but the searching. And that was something she could teach. That was something worth teaching.
