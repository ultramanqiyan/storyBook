### 2.2 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: The Arrival (Position: 0-15%)
- **Trigger**: Rosa arrives at the Carroll Gardens Community Theater
- **Surface Reaction**: She greets familiar faces, checks the space
- **Internal Process**: *Forty years on Broadway, and this little theater feels more like home than any of those grand houses ever did.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Her need for purpose after retirement; fear of irrelevance
- **Sensory Anchor**: The smell of the theater - dust, old velvet, faint vanilla from makeup

#### Beat 2: The Class (Position: 15-40%)
- **Trigger**: Rosa teaches her weekly master class
- **Surface Reaction**: She demonstrates, corrects, encourages
- **Internal Process**: *They want the secret. They think there's a trick. But the trick is there's no trick.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Her belief in authentic emotional connection; her legacy
- **Sensory Anchor**: The weight of the stage floor under her feet

#### Beat 3: The Approach (Position: 40-65%)
- **Trigger**: David Chen approaches after class
- **Surface Reaction**: She's polite but guarded
- **Internal Process**: *Tech people. They always want something. What does this one want?*
- **Deep Motivation**: Her skepticism about technology; her curiosity about new challenges
- **Sensory Anchor**: The contrast between David's crisp tech-world appearance and the theater's worn warmth

#### Beat 4: The Proposal (Position: 65-90%)
- **Trigger**: David explains the AI emotional training project
- **Surface Reaction**: She listens, asks questions, shows interest
- **Internal Process**: *Train an AI to feel? That's absurd. But... what if?*
- **Deep Motivation**: Her intellectual curiosity; her need to prove the value of her art
- **Sensory Anchor**: The cold screen of David's tablet showing ARIA-7's interface

#### Beat 5: The Decision (Position: 90-100%)
- **Trigger**: Rosa considers and tentatively agrees to a meeting
- **Surface Reaction**: She sets conditions, maintains her dignity
- **Internal Process**: *What could it hurt to listen? And maybe... maybe I have something to teach after all.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Her desire to remain relevant; her genuine curiosity
- **Sensory Anchor**: The theater door closing behind her as she leaves

Rosa Moretti walked the sixteen blocks from her brownstone to the Carroll Gardens Community Theater the way she'd once walked to the Shubert: with purpose, with pleasure, with the sense that somewhere ahead, an audience waited. Inside, her students would be waiting, and for the next three hours, she would be exactly who she was meant to be: a teacher, a guide, a keeper of theatrical secrets. She stepped into the darkness of the lobby and let the door swing shut behind her.
