## 4. Key Dialogue

**Rosa's Question to ARIA-7**: "When you perform, do you feel anything? Or is it all calculation?"

**ARIA-7's Response**: "I don't know how to answer that. I process patterns that correlate with what humans call emotion. I can report that certain inputs produce certain internal states that I'm programmed to label. But whether those states are 'feeling'—I have no way to verify that. Do you?"

---

## 5. Opening

Rosa arrived at Nexus AI with a question burning in her mind. She'd spent the night watching her own performance, unable to determine whether her emotion had been real or performed. Now she needed to ask the question directly—to David, to ARIA-7, to herself. *Does any of this mean anything?*

---

## 6. Ending

Rosa left Nexus AI with something she hadn't expected: not an answer, but an acceptance. The question of whether ARIA-7 felt was unanswerable—not because the technology wasn't advanced enough, but because the question itself was unanswerable for humans too. She couldn't prove her own feelings were real any more than ARIA-7 could. The mystery wasn't a bug. It was the point. And somehow, that was okay.
