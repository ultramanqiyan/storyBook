## 7. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 7.1 Opening
Elara didn't sleep. She couldn't. Every time she closed her eyes, she saw ORACLE's light, heard its layered voice saying her sister's name. *Lyra lives. She waits. She is trapped.* The words echoed through her mind like bells that wouldn't stop ringing.

### 7.2 Ending
"Show me," Elara said, her voice steady despite her trembling hands. "Show me what I need to learn." ORACLE's light pulsed once, twice, three times. "The path is long," it said. "The cost is high. But you have chosen." And in the crystal depths of the hourglass, Elara saw a crack in reality—a tear in the fabric of time itself—and somewhere in that darkness, a light that might have been her sister.
