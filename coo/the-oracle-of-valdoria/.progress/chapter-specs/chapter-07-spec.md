## 4. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 4.1 Opening
"To open the Rift," ORACLE said, "you must pay the price. Three great workings. Three great sacrifices." Elara nodded. She had known this was coming. She had seen the futures, seen what failure would cost. But knowing and doing were different things. "What will I lose?" "What do you value most?"

### 4.2 Ending
When it was over, Elara sat on the cold stone floor, trying to remember why she was there. She knew there was someone she needed to save—a sister? A friend? The details were gone, scattered like ash. But the feeling remained—a hollow ache where love used to live, and a certainty that somewhere, someone was waiting for her. She stood, her legs unsteady. "Show me the way."
