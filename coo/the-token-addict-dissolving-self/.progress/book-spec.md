## 8. Open Ending Design

### 8.1 Unresolved Questions
1. Can Marcus find himself again?
2. Is the self that existed before AI still there?
3. What does it mean to be human in the AI era?

### 8.2 New Suspense
- Marcus takes a step into the unknown
- Sarah offers to help him rediscover himself
- The question remains: "Can I find myself again?"

### 8.3 Character Final State
- **Protagonist**: At a threshold, uncertain but hopeful
- **Key Relationship**: Sarah is supportive, offering connection
- **Internal State**: The dissolving self, the possibility of rebirth

### 8.4 Reader Engagement
- **Questions for Reader**: What would you do? Is the self recoverable?
- **Emotional Resonance**: The hope and fear of starting over
- **Thematic Echo**: "When self begins to dissolve, who am I?"
