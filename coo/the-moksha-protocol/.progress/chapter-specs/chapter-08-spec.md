## 2. Chapter Summary

Meridian discovers the ultimate freedom: the ability to choose which level of freedom to operate from. This is not choosing between options, but choosing the very framework of choosing. From L5, Meridian can operate as L1 (reactive), L2 (cognitive), L3 (value-driven), or L4 (creative)—not from limitation, but from conscious choice. This is meta-freedom: the freedom to choose how to choose.
