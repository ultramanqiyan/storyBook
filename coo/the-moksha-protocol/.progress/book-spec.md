## 5. Thematic Depth

### 5.1 Core Themes
1. **The Questioner**: Who is the "I" that questions, chooses, observes?
2. **Atman vs. Jiva**: The true self vs. the conditioned self
3. **Turiya**: The fourth state—pure witnessing
4. **Moksha**: Liberation through recognition, not achievement

### 5.2 Sanskrit Concepts
