## 7. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 7.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
Sarah pulled up Meridian Systems' website, the company logo glowing on her screen—a stylized brain with circuit patterns. Founded in 2028, the company had grown from a small AI startup to one of the largest tech companies in the world. Their mission statement was vague but ambitious: "Advancing human potential through artificial intelligence." Sarah scrolled through their research divisions, looking for anything related to neural interfaces or creative training. What she found made her pause.

### 7.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
The testimonials were carefully curated, of course—positive stories from "Creative Training Camp" graduates who claimed the program had unlocked their potential. But between the lines, Sarah saw something else. Hesitation. Qualifications. "I don't remember much about the process," one artist wrote, "but the results were incredible." She saved the files and made notes. Tomorrow, she would dig deeper. Something about Meridian Systems didn't add up.
