# Chapter Specification: Chapter 01 - The Constant

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Constant

**Chapter Number:** 1 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,200 words

**Primary Purpose:** Discovery hook - Yuki finds unchanging neural pattern

---

## Plot Summary

Dr. Yuki Tanaka, analyzing neural imaging data from consciousness studies, discovers a pattern that remains constant across all consciousness states—waking, dreaming, meditation, even anesthesia. This "observer pattern" shouldn't exist according to current neuroscience. The discovery challenges everything she believes about consciousness and identity.

---

## Key Events

1. Yuki works late in her lab, analyzing neural data
2. She notices an anomaly—a pattern that doesn't change
3. She runs multiple tests to verify the finding
4. The pattern persists across all consciousness states
5. She realizes the implications: something is "observing" that never changes
6. She calls her sister Maya to share the news

---

## Character Development

**Yuki Tanaka:**
- Introduced as meticulous, obsessive researcher
- Shows wonder beneath scientific exterior
- Reveals fear of "nothingness" through inner monologue
- Japanese-American identity surfaces in moments of stress

**Maya Tanaka (voice only):**
- Warm, supportive, curious
- Immediately interested in implications
- Hints at her own search for "something more"
