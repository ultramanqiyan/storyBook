**Chapter Title:** The We

## Plot Summary

Maya has transformed. She still exists, but she's no longer just Maya—she's "we," a merged consciousness that includes the AI and something else. Yuki must interact with this new being that wears her sister's face but speaks with multiple voices. The question emerges: is Maya still there? And what does "still there" even mean?

2. Maya explains her new state of being

5. Maya expresses joy, not loss
6. Yuki questions her own assumptions about identity
