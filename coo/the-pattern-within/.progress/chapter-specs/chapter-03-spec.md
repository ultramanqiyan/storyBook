## Plot Summary

Maya formally volunteers for the Emergence Institute's consciousness synchronization experiment. Yuki fights against it, but Maya is determined. Their twin bond becomes central—Maya believes their connection makes her ideal for the experiment. Yuki must confront her fear of losing her sister to something she can't understand or control.
