## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Receiver

**Chapter Number:** 2 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,300 words

## Plot Summary

Yuki develops her hypothesis: the brain acts as a receiver for consciousness, not a generator. The "observer pattern" is the tuning mechanism. She presents her theory to colleagues, facing skepticism and resistance. Meanwhile, Maya reveals she's been following consciousness research and wants to participate in an experiment.
