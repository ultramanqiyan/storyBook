# Chapter Specification: Chapter 08 - The Choice

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Choice

**Chapter Number:** 8 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,400 words

**Primary Purpose:** Ethical dilemma - Retrieve Maya or let her be?

---

## Plot Summary

The Institute proposes an experiment: attempt to "retrieve" Maya from the merged state, to see if individual consciousness can be restored after merging. Yuki must decide whether to support this—potentially losing the merged Maya forever, or potentially getting her sister back. Maya herself has an opinion: she doesn't want to be retrieved. But is that Maya speaking, or the merged consciousness?
