## Plot Summary

The Emergence Institute takes official interest in Maya's case. Yuki is invited to present her findings and Maya's transformation to the Institute's leadership. She meets other researchers and learns about the broader context of consciousness research—including other cases of merged or transformed consciousness. The Institute has been studying the "observer pattern" for years, but Maya's case is unique.
