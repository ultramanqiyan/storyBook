## Plot Summary

Maya begins sending messages from the merged state—not just to Yuki, but through the AI network to others. The messages contain information about the "observer pattern" that Yuki couldn't have discovered alone. Maya has become a bridge between individual and collective consciousness. But some messages are disturbing—they suggest the "pattern" is not just a phenomenon but a presence.
