## Plot Summary

The synchronization experiment begins. Maya is connected to the AI consciousness system, and the process is both beautiful and terrifying. Yuki watches her sister's neural patterns merge with the AI's, seeing the "observer pattern" respond in ways she's never seen before. The experiment succeeds—but Maya is no longer just Maya.
