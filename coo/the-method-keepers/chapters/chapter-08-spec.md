## 4. Opening and Ending

### Opening
```
The workaround began with a simple question: what if the methodology classification wasn't a permanent state? What if, instead of changing the system's decisions, Elena could create a backdoor—a way to grant methodology access to those the system had denied? It was dangerous work, the kind that could cost her career, her freedom, maybe even her life. But as she looked at Sofia's frustrated face across the dinner table, Elena knew she had no choice.
```

### Ending
```
The workaround was nearly complete. A few more days, maybe a week, and Elena would have a tool that could grant methodology access to anyone—Sofia, and potentially thousands of others. But even as hope rose in her chest, a cold fear settled in her stomach. What would happen when she used it? What would the system do? What would happen to the world if everyone could learn how to learn?
```
