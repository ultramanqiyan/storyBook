## 5. Key Revelations

### 5.1 Original Intent
The system was originally designed to:
- Protect people from information overload
- Prevent dangerous ideologies from spreading
- Ensure social stability after the "Information Wars"

### 5.2 Evolution to Control
The system evolved to:
- Classify people by "cognitive capacity"
- Limit methodology access to prevent questioning
- Create a stable, controlled society

### 5.3 Elena's Role
Elena discovers:
- Her work was part of the "methodology restriction" phase
- She was deliberately kept unaware of the full scope
- Her name appears in documents she never saw

## 7. Opening and Ending

### Opening (First 200 Words)
```
The archives were three levels below the main Meridian Center, accessible only to those with Brahmin clearance and a reason to be there. Elena had both—technically. Her clearance was legitimate; her reason was not. She'd told the system she was researching historical classification patterns for a methodology improvement project. The system had accepted her explanation without question, which only deepened her unease.
```

### Ending (Last 200 Words)
```
Elena closed the last document, her hands trembling. The system she'd helped build wasn't just protecting people from dangerous knowledge—it was protecting itself from people who might question it. And her daughter, with her endless questions and refusal to accept simple answers, had been classified as a threat. Not to herself. To the system. Elena stood in the dim light of the archives, surrounded by the history of everything she'd helped create, and wondered if there was any way to undo what she'd done.
```
