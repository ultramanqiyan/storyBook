```

### Elena's Response
```
"I'm trying to fix something. I don't know if I can. I don't even know if I should."
```

### Final Internal Monologue
```
Elena stood at the threshold, the workaround ready, the consequences known. On one side: her daughter's future, the freedom to learn how to learn. On the other: social stability, the prevention of chaos, the system she had helped build. There was no right answer. There was only the choice she would make, and the world that would exist because of it.
```

## 5. Opening and Ending

### Opening
```
Three days had passed since Elena had discovered the consequences of her workaround. Three days of carrying the knowledge like a stone in her chest, of looking at Sofia and seeing not just her daughter but the cost of every choice she might make. ### Ending

Elena stood in her home office, the workaround glowing on her screen, her daughter's voice echoing in her mind. The threshold stretched before her—two worlds, two futures, and no way to know which was right. She reached toward the interface. Her fingers hovered over the activation key. And then—
