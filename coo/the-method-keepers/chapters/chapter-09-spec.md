## 4. Key Revelation

### The Consequence
When Elena runs simulations of widespread methodology liberation, she discovers:
- Social institutions would collapse
- People would question everything without tools to find answers
- Chaos would follow rapid cognitive liberation
- The system, for all its flaws, was maintaining stability

### The Moral Horror
Elena realizes:
- Liberation might be worse than oppression
- She has created a tool that could destroy society
- There is no clean solution
