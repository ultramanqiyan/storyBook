**Participants**: Elena Vasquez, Sofia Vasquez

**Surface Content**: Silent drive home

**Subtext**: Both processing the classification, neither wanting to speak first

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Maternal fear, need to explain
- Sofia: Confusion, growing anger

**Power Dynamics**: Equal, both vulnerable

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Brief exchange about the drive (attempt at normalcy)
2. Silence (both processing)
3. Sofia asks what happens now (fear)
4. Elena promises to explain (maternal reassurance)
5. Return to silence (unresolved)

#### Segment 2: Methodology Explanation

**Participants**: Elena Vasquez, Sofia Vasquez

**Surface Content**: Elena explaining the methodology stratification system

**Subtext**: Elena's pride vs. Sofia's growing frustration

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Professional pride, genuine belief
- Sofia: Frustration, feeling trapped

**Power Dynamics**: Elena has knowledge, Sofia has lived experience

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Elena activates system, begins explanation (professional)
2. Sofia listens, asks clarifying questions (trying to understand)
3. Elena elaborates on the elegance of the design (pride)
4. Sofia asks why she can't learn methodology (frustration)
5. Elena struggles to explain (doubt emerging)

#### Segment 3: Confrontation

**Participants**: Elena Vasquez, Sofia Vasquez

**Surface Content**: Sofia confronting Elena about the system

**Subtext**: Sofia's anger, Elena's guilt and helplessness

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Guilt, helplessness, maternal fear
- Sofia: Anger, betrayal, fear for her future

**Power Dynamics**: Sofia has moral ground, Elena has knowledge

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Sofia asks why she was classified this way (seeking answer)
2. Elena explains the algorithm (defensive)
3. Sofia challenges the logic (frustration)
4. Elena cannot provide satisfying answer (helplessness)
5. Sofia's accusation (crisis)
