## 7. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 7.1 Opening
Thomas sat at his desk, the blank document open on his screen. The cursor blinked, patient and merciless. He had written hundreds of papers, thousands of pages, in his career. But this was different. This wasn't an academic exercise. This was a judgment—on ARIA-7, on Meridian Labs, and, he was beginning to realize, on himself.

### 7.2 Ending
Thomas handed the report to Sarah. She took it, her expression unreadable. "Thank you," she said. "I know this wasn't easy." Thomas nodded. "I don't know if I made the right decision. I don't know if there is a right decision." Sarah looked at him for a long moment. "Maybe that's the most honest thing anyone can say." Thomas walked out of Meridian Labs into the autumn afternoon. He had done what he came to do. Now he would have to live with it.
