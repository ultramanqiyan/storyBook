# Chapter 6 Specification: The Approach

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Number:** 6

**Chapter Title:** The Approach

**Word Count Target:** 2,200 words

**POV Character:** Dr. Sarah Chen

---

## Chapter Purpose

- Sarah prepares for her threshold
- Learning from Wei
- Integrating methodology with experience
- The threshold becomes tangible

---

## Key Events

1. Wei teaches Sarah threshold practices
2. She learns to sense the approaching state
3. Physical and mental preparation
4. Integration of her methodology with Wei's guidance
5. The threshold becomes more present
6. Sarah feels ready—or as ready as possible

---

## Emotional Architecture

**Primary Emotion:** Preparation
- Getting ready for transformation
- Learning new skills
- Building capacity

**Secondary Emotion:** Anticipation
- What will it be like?
- What will change?
- Fear mixed with excitement

**Tertiary Emotion:** Gratitude
- For Wei's guidance
- For the preparation
- For the opportunity

---

## Sensory Details (Minimum 5)

1. **Visual:** The threshold as shimmering edge, almost visible
2. **Auditory:** The sound of approaching change, like distant thunder
3. **Tactile:** The texture of preparation, practices becoming familiar
4. **Proprioceptive:** Body awareness changing, becoming more sensitive
5. **Internal:** The sense of something approaching, inevitable

---

## Character Voice

**Wei's Teaching Voice:**
- Clear, precise
- Building on her existing knowledge
- Patient with her questions
- Trust in her capacity

**Sarah's Learning Voice:**
- Integrating new with known
- Questions that reveal depth
- Growing confidence
- Readiness emerging

---

## Physics Integration

**Concept Introduced:** Critical slowing down
- System approaching transition
- Response time increasing
- Fluctuations growing
- The approach to criticality

**Metaphor:** The stillness before a storm—everything preparing for change

---

## Dialogue Requirements

- Teaching dialogue should feel authentic
- Sarah's questions should be real
- Wei's answers should open rather than close
- The relationship should deepen

## Pacing Notes

- Measured, preparatory pace
- Building understanding
- Growing presence of threshold
- Quiet readiness at end

## Forbidden Patterns

## Chapter Ending

Sarah sits in stillness, the threshold present at the edge of awareness. She thinks: "I'm ready." Then: "No—I'm willing. That's different."
