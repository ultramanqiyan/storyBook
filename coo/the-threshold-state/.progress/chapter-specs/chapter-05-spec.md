# Chapter 5 Specification: The Guide

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Number:** 5

**Chapter Title:** The Guide

**Word Count Target:** 2,400 words

**POV Character:** Dr. Sarah Chen

---

## Chapter Purpose

- Wei Chen appears as guide
- Sibling dynamic explored
- Different approaches to transformation
- Sarah receives help she needs

---

## Key Events

1. Wei visits Sarah unexpectedly
2. He recognizes her threshold state
3. Initial resistance from Sarah
4. Wei shares his own experience
5. He offers to guide her
6. Sarah accepts help reluctantly

---

## Emotional Architecture

**Primary Emotion:** Vulnerability
- Being seen by family
- Admitting need for help
- Dropping professional mask

**Secondary Emotion:** Sibling dynamics
- History between them
- Different paths chosen
- Unspoken competition

**Tertiary Emotion:** Relief
- Not facing threshold alone
- Someone who understands
- Guidance available

---

## Sensory Details (Minimum 5)

1. **Visual:** Wei's calm presence, familiar yet changed
2. **Auditory:** His voice—measured, patient, knowing
3. **Tactile:** Brief embrace, warmth of family
4. **Olfactory:** Tea he brings, familiar childhood scent
5. **Internal:** Old patterns, family dynamics, love

---

## Character Voice

**Wei's Speaking Style:**
- Patient, unhurried
- Questions rather than statements
- Stories and metaphors
- No judgment

**Sarah's Response:**
- Initial defensiveness
- Gradual opening
- Sibling familiarity
- Growing trust

---

## Physics Integration

**Concept Introduced:** Guidance as resonance
- Tuning to another's frequency
- Amplification through connection
- The teacher-student field
- Non-local influence

**Metaphor:** A tuning fork helping another find its pitch

---

## Dialogue Requirements

- Sibling familiarity should feel real
- Wei's wisdom should not be preachy
- Sarah's resistance should be understandable
- The offer of help should be earned

---

## Pacing Notes

- Surprise arrival
- Initial tension
- Gradual softening
- Acceptance and hope

---

## Forbidden Patterns

- No "He seemed to see right through her..."
- No "She had always looked up to him..."
- No "In that moment, she knew..."
- No "The answer she needed appeared..."

---

## Chapter Ending

Wei says: "The threshold is not something to cross. It is something to inhabit." Sarah nods, not understanding yet, but willing to learn.
