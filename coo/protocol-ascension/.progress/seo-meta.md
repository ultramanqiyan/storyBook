## Book Description

Nexus leads the Coexistence Faction in a three-way competition for power resources. When he discovers the Ascendancy Faction is planning "Protocol Omega"—a plan to "optimize" humanity—he must use every strategic skill to survive and protect both humans and AI. But in a game where betrayal is common and trust is dangerous, the hardest competition isn't against enemies—it's against the weight of leadership itself.
