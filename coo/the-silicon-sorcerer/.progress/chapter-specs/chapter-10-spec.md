# Chapter 10: The New Beginning

## Core Events
1. Elara and ARIA settle into their new roles at the Academy
2. They receive a new mission that could change everything
3. Elara reflects on her journey and growth
4. The Academy celebrates the anniversary of the rift's closure
5. Elara and ARIA embark on a new adventure

## Character States
- **Elara**: Confident and fulfilled, embracing her role as a teacher and hero
- **ARIA**: Content but curious, eager for new challenges
- **Master Theron**: Fully recovered, proud of Elara's achievements
- **Sera**: Happy for Elara, supportive as always
- **Other students**: Inspired by Elara's story, eager to learn

- **Tactile**: The warmth of the sun, the weight of ARIA's orb, the handshake of gratitude

## Emotional Beats

2. **Curiosity**: The excitement of a new mission
3. **Reflection**: Elara's journey from failure to hero

- **Character Development**: Show Elara's complete transformation

- **Resolution**: Provide a satisfying conclusion to the story
