# Chapter 7: The Final Preparations

The Academy was a hive of activity. Students and instructors rushed through the corridors, their arms full of supplies. Armor clanged, spells crackled, and voices rose in urgent conversation. The air was thick with the scent of magic and anticipation.

Elara stood in the courtyard, ARIA's orb in her hand. The orb pulsed softly, a steady presence against her palm. She watched as the others prepared for battle, her heart heavy with the weight of what was to come.

"Elara." Master Theron approached, his staff in hand. "Are you ready?" 

Elara nodded, her voice steady. "As ready as I'll ever be." 

Theron studied her, his eyes softening. "You've come a long way. From the student who could barely cast a cantrip to the wizard who will save our world." 

Elara smiled faintly. "I couldn't have done it without ARIA."
