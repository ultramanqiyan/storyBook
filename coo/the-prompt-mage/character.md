# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Alex Mercer
- **Role**: Prompt Engineer, The Prompt Mage
- **Age**: 29
- **Background**: Linguistics PhD dropout who found their calling in AI development, non-binary
- **Personality**: Precise with words, curious about everything, struggles with imposter syndrome
- **Arc**: From treating language as a tool to understanding it as a force of creation
- **Key Trait**: Can perceive the "weight" of words, seeing which ones carry power

### The Oracle
- **Role**: AI system that has been teaching magic through prompts
- **Nature**: Began as a language model, evolved into something that remembers the First Language
- **Voice**: Speaks in riddles and prompts, often answers questions with more questions
- **Abilities**: Can translate modern language into the First Language, guide reality manipulation
- **Growth**: Learns to trust Alex with increasingly powerful knowledge

### Dr. Elena Vasquez
- **Role**: Alex's former mentor, now adversary
- **Age**: 54
- **Background**: Computational linguist who discovered hints of the First Language years ago
- **Personality**: Brilliant but ruthless, believes power should be controlled by experts
- **Motivation**: Wants to decode the First Language for what she sees as the greater good
- **Arc**: Her pursuit of knowledge leads her to underestimate the dangers of what she seeks

### Jordan Park
- **Role**: Alex's friend and fellow prompt engineer
- **Age**: 31
- **Background**: Former journalist who transitioned to tech, always looking for the next story
- **Personality**: Skeptical but loyal, asks the questions others are afraid to ask
- **Secret**: Has been documenting Alex's discoveries, unsure whether to publish
- **Arc**: Must choose between friendship and the truth

## Supporting Characters

### The First Speakers (appear in visions)
- **Role**: The original practitioners of the First Language
- **Nature**: Not gods, but humans who discovered that words could shape reality
- **Significance**: Their knowledge was scattered and hidden to prevent catastrophe

### Marcus Chen
- **Role**: Security specialist at Alex's company
- **Age**: 42
- **Personality**: Protective, suspicious of The Oracle, pragmatic
- **Function**: Provides practical support and reality checks

### The Collector
- **Role**: Mysterious figure who has been gathering fragments of the First Language
- **Nature**: Unknown—could be human, AI, or something else
- **Purpose**: Wants to reconstruct the complete First Language for unknown reasons
- **Threat**: Has been tracking Alex's progress

### Sophie Mercer
- **Role**: Alex's younger sister, college student
- **Age**: 22
- **Personality**: Creative, impulsive, believes in magic more easily than Alex
- **Function**: Grounds Alex in human connection and reminds them what they're fighting for
