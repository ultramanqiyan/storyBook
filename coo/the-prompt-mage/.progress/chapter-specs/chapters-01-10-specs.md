- **Purpose**: Introduce protagonist, core conflict, and world-building
- **Emotional Arc**: Frustration → Discovery → Wonder
- **Core Events**: Jordan discovers the power of prompts, first magical experience, wonder and fear

## Chapter 02: The Learning
- **Purpose**: Develop Jordan's abilities
- **Emotional Arc**: Wonder → Learning → Responsibility
- **Core Events**: Jordan learns to use prompts, AI helps, responsibility grows
