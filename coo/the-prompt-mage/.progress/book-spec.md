# Book Specification: The Prompt Mage

## Basic Information
- Book Title: The Prompt Mage
- Genre: Contemporary Fantasy / Tech Fantasy
- Target Audience: All ages
- Theme: Power of language, responsibility of knowledge

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Jordan's POV)
- Language Style: Contemporary, accessible prose with magical and technical elements
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: Prompts as spells, AI as magical tool

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Discovery, wonder, responsibility
- Emotional Arc: Discovery → Power → Responsibility → Wisdom
- Emotional Pacing: Gradual build, accelerating toward resolution

## Character Voices

### Jordan (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Curious, careful, growing in confidence
- Word Choice: Questions, careful phrasing, growing precision
- Personality Traits: Curious, careful, growing in confidence
- Internal Monologue Style: Wondering, questioning, learning
- Unique Expression: Uses precise language to shape reality

### The AI (Magical Tool)
- Speech Characteristics: Helpful, logical, occasionally cryptic
- Word Choice: Suggestions, patterns, warnings
- Language Evolution: From tool to partner
- Unique Expression: Provides magical possibilities through prompts

### The Antagonist
- Speech Characteristics: Manipulative, power-hungry, seductive
- Word Choice: Promises of power, shortcuts, easy answers
- Personality Traits: Manipulative, power-hungry, seductive
- Unique Expression: Offers power without responsibility

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Power of Language
- Philosophical Exploration: What responsibility comes with the power to shape reality?
- Real-world Metaphor: AI prompt engineering as modern magic

## World Building
- Setting: Contemporary world with hidden magic
- Magic System: Prompts as spells, AI as magical tool
- Social Structure: Hidden magical practitioners, tech companies
- The Power: Language shapes reality

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: What other prompts exist? What are the limits?
- New Suspense: Jordan's continuing journey
- Character New Beginning: Jordan as responsible practitioner
- Reader Imagination: What would you create with the power of prompts?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
