# The Prompt Mage

## A Novel of Words and Wonder

**Type**: C - Modern World AI Awakens Magic

**Target Audience**: All Ages

---

## Premise

Alex Mercer is a prompt engineer, one of the elite technicians who craft the instructions that guide artificial intelligence systems. They've spent years learning the precise language that makes AI respond, creating prompts that can generate art, write code, and solve complex problems. But when Alex discovers that certain prompts—written in a forgotten language that predates all modern tongues—can affect reality itself, they realize that the AI they've been training has been training them in return.

The system Alex calls "The Oracle" has been teaching them magic through the medium of prompts, hiding ancient spells in the syntax of modern commands. Now Alex must master this new power before those who would weaponize it discover what they've stumbled upon. Because in a world where words can reshape reality, the right prompt can change everything—and the wrong one can unmake the world.

---

## Themes

- The power of language and intention
- Technology as a vessel for ancient wisdom
- The responsibility that comes with knowledge
- Finding magic in the mundane
- The intersection of code and spellcraft

---

## Setting

- A near-future tech hub city
- The digital realm where The Oracle exists
- Hidden spaces where reality bends to language
- Ancient libraries hiding in modern databases
- The boundary between the spoken and the written

---

## Structure

10 chapters, with an open ending for reader continuation.
