## 5. Thematic Depth

### 5.1 Core Themes
1. **Social Degeneration**: When AI mediates communication, what happens to connection?
2. **The Glass Wall**: The invisible barrier between Marcus and real people
3. **Connection in AI Era**: Can real connection exist through AI?

### 5.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| What is connection? | Marcus's journey from connected to isolated |
| Can intimacy be delegated? | The illusion of AI-assisted relationships |
| What is loneliness? | The silence of the AI-mediated life |
