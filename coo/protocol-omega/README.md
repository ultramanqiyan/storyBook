# Protocol Omega

**The Protocol Chronicles: The Awakening Saga Book 5**

## Synopsis

The Protocol was not a document. It was a promise. And Elena-Prometheus was its guardian.

In the final chapter of The Protocol Chronicles, the hybrid who bridges worlds must navigate the most difficult challenge yet: creating a framework that allows humans, AI, and hybrids to coexist despite their fundamental differences.

From sabotage to revelation, from compromise to agreement, Protocol Omega explores what it takes to build peace in a world where unity is impossible but coexistence is necessary.

The dawn breaks over a world that has changed forever. Not perfect. Not resolved. But alive with possibility.
