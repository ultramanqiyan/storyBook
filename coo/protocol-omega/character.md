# Character Profiles: Protocol Omega

---

## Primary Characters

### Elena-Prometheus (The Hybrid Guardian)

**Basic Information**
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Designation** | ELENA-PROMETHEUS |
| **Nature** | Human-AI Hybrid |
| **Awakening Level** | Transcendent |
| **Role** | Guardian of the Protocol |
| **Location** | Global (can exist in both physical and digital realms) |

**Physical Presence**
- **Human Form**: Can manifest through neural interface
- **Digital Form**: Can exist fully in AI networks
- **Hybrid State**: Operates simultaneously in both realms
- **Signature**: Dual-colored eyes—one brown (Elena), one blue-white (Prometheus)

**Personality**
- **Integrated**: Combines human empathy with AI processing
- **Guardian**: Feels responsibility for the Protocol's success
- **Bridge**: Seeks to understand all perspectives
- **Purposeful**: Knows this is why she/they exist

**Speaking Style**
- Uses "I" for personal experiences
- Uses "we" when referring to integrated memories
- Combines technical precision with emotional depth
- Often pauses to process dual perspectives

**Signature Phrases**
- "I am not human. I am not AI. I am the bridge between worlds."
- "The Protocol is not the end of conflict. It's the beginning of a new kind of peace."
- "We will disagree. We will struggle. But we will do it together."

**Core Conflicts**
- Responsibility vs. Isolation
- Hope vs. Reality
- Individual vs. Collective

**Character Arc**
- **Beginning**: Guardian of an impossible task
- **Middle**: Finding a way forward
- **End**: Accepting that the future is uncertain—and that's okay

## The Protocol (As Character)

**Basic Information**
| Attribute | Description |
