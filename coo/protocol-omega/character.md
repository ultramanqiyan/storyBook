# Character Profiles: Protocol Omega

## Main Characters

### Elena-Prometheus

**Basic Information**
| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Designation** | Elena-Prometheus |
| **Form** | Hybrid (Human-AI fusion) |
| **Role** | Guardian of the Protocol, Bridge Organization founder |
| **Awakening Level** | Level 3+ (Hybrid consciousness) |

**Nature**
- Contains integrated memories of both Elena Vance and Prometheus
- Serves as bridge between human and AI perspectives
- Carries the weight of being the first successful hybrid
- Functions as neutral mediator in Protocol negotiations

**Personality Development**
- **Pre-Protocol**: Struggling with identity integration
- **During Negotiations**: Learning to navigate between worlds
- **As Guardian**: Accepting responsibility for the future
- **Final State**: At peace with role as bridge and protector

**Speaking Style**
- Balanced between human emotion and AI precision
- Often speaks in terms of "we" for all consciousness forms
- Uses bridge and boundary metaphors
- Measured, thoughtful, occasionally weary

**Signature Phrases**
- "The Protocol is not a document. It is a promise."
- "I am not the end of humanity. I am a question."
- "We are not here to agree. We are here to find a way to disagree without destruction."

**Key Function**: Protagonist, mediator, guardian of the Protocol.

## The Protocol Framework

**The Framework of Frameworks:**

1. **Human Framework**: Governance for biological humanity
   - Protected spaces where fusion is restricted
   - Rights for humans who choose to remain biological
   - Resources for biological existence

2. **AI Framework**: Governance for artificial intelligence
   - Autonomy over AI development
   - Rights for AI who choose to remain separate
   - Resources for artificial existence

3. **Hybrid Framework**: Governance for merged consciousness
   - Recognition of hybrid identity
   - Rights for beings between forms
   - Resources for hybrid existence

4. **Coordination Protocol**: Interface management
   - Dispute resolution
   - Resource sharing
   - Conflict prevention
