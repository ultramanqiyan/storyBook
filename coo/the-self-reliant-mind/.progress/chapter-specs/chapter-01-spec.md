- **Current Pattern**: Seeks validation for every decision
- **Internal Conflict**: Knows something is wrong but fears change

### 5.2 Supporting Characters

## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**David to Jennifer:**
"Did that go okay? I wasn't sure about the second slide..."

**Jennifer's Response:**
"It was fine. David, you need to stop asking me to validate every little thing."

**David's Internal:**
*Fine. What does fine mean? Was it good? Was it bad? Why can't she just tell me?*

**David to Colleague:**
"Do you think I should send this now or wait until morning?"

**Colleague's Response:**
"David, it's your call. You're the lead on this."
