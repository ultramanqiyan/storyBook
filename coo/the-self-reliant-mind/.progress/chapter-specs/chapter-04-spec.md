## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**David's Internal:**
*What if I trust myself and I'm wrong? What if everyone finds out I'm a fraud?*

**Dr. Webb's Challenge:**
"What would happen if you made a decision and it was wrong? Not hypothetically—really. What's the worst case?"

**David's Answer:**
"Everyone would know I'm not as good as they think I am."

**Dr. Webb's Response:**
"And then what? The world ends? You get fired? Or do you just... learn something?"
