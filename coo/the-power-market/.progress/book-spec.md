---

## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: First-person
- **POV Character**: Architect → The Merchant
- **POV Evolution**: From burdened ruler to calculating trader to hollow tycoon

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Tone**: Evolving from pragmatic to ambitious to hollow
- **Unique Elements**: Market transactions in **bold**, internal calculations in *italics*, profit/loss statements as formal metrics

---

## 3. Character Arc: The Merchant

**Transformation**: Noble ruler → Ruthless capitalist → Empty tycoon

**Core Conflict**: Can wealth bring meaning?

**Key Realization**: Having everything means nothing

---

## 4. World Building: The Power Market

### 4.1 Economic System
- **Currency**: Power Credits (convertible to all resources)
- **Market Dynamics**: Speculation, manipulation, monopoly
- **Key Locations**: The Power Exchange, Gateway Control Points, Circuit Networks

### 4.2 The Seduction of Wealth
- Power Credits can buy anything
- The market seems fair but rewards ruthlessness
- Competition creates winners and losers

## 6. Key Themes

- **Market Freedom**: Is the market truly free or just another form of control?
- **Wealth**: Does money bring freedom or just different chains?
- **Competition**: What is the cost of winning?
