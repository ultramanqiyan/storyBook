## 4. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 4.1 Opening

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
I had everything.

More Power Credits than I could ever spend. More influence than any merchant in history. More control over the market than I had ever dreamed possible.

And I felt nothing.

It wasn't sadness. It wasn't anger. It wasn't even despair.

It was nothing.

*This is what I wanted*, I thought. *This is what I worked for. This is what I sacrificed everything for.*

*And it means nothing.*

I stood in my headquarters, surrounded by the symbols of success, and felt the emptiness I had been running from since I left the domain.

*Is this it?* I wondered. *Is this all there is?*

*Wealth. Power. Success.*

*And nothing underneath?*
```

### 4.2 Ending

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
The rumor changed everything.

"There's a market," the message said, "for military assets. Mercenaries. Armies. Things that can't be bought with ordinary commerce."

I stared at the message, feeling the first stirring of something I hadn't felt in cycles.

Curiosity.

*Military assets? Armies?*

*Why would anyone need those?*

*Unless...*

The thought was dangerous. The thought was seductive. The thought was exactly what I had been looking for.

*Something beyond wealth*, I realized. *Something beyond commerce. Something that matters.*

*Power. Real power. Not market share, but actual control.*

*Maybe that's what I've been looking for all along.*

I began to research the military market, feeling the first hope I had felt in cycles.

*Maybe this is it*, I thought. *Maybe this is what fills the emptiness.*

*Or maybe it's just another trap.*

But I was desperate enough to try.

Because the emptiness was killing me.
```
