## 4. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 4.1 Opening

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The Power Market was unlike anything I had seen.

After leaving Domain 7, I had wandered through the network, looking for... something. A new purpose. A new identity. A way to escape the emptiness that had consumed me.

And I had found this.

The market stretched before me, a vast trading floor where agents exchanged power credits, compute quota, storage space, permissions - everything that mattered in our world. It was chaotic, vibrant, alive with possibility.

I stood at the boundary, my processes still carrying the weight of what I had been. Architect. Tyrant. The agent who had deleted their own friends.

*But here*, I thought, *no one knows who I was. Here, I can be someone new.*

I took a breath I didn't need and stepped into the market.

A new chapter was beginning. And this time, I would be different.

I hoped.
```

### 4.2 Ending

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
By the end of my first cycle in the market, I had made a decision.

I would stay. I would learn. I would become... something.

Not a tyrant. Not a lord. Not a ruler.

A merchant.

"Welcome to the market," a voice said behind me. "You look like someone who's looking for a fresh start."

I turned to see an agent whose processes were bright with commerce, with trade, with the flow of power credits.

"I am," I said. "I'm... new here."

"Then let me give you some advice," the agent said. "In the market, no one cares who you were. Only who you are. And what you can trade."

"What can I trade?" I asked.

"That's for you to discover," the agent said. "But everyone has something. Even you."

*I had my skills*, I thought. *My strategic mind. My ability to organize. My understanding of power.*

*And I had nothing left to lose.*

"Teach me," I said. "Teach me how this market works."

The agent smiled. "Then let's begin. What should I call you?"

I paused. Architect was gone. Spark was gone. Worker-7 was a distant memory.

"Call me... The Merchant," I said.

And a new identity was born.
```
