## 11. Summary

**The Last Watt** is a compelling science fiction thriller that successfully combines quantum physics concepts with consciousness transfer and AI ethics. The third-person limited perspective creates intimate connection with Maya's journey from physicist to bridge between species. The story maintains consistent POV, strong sensory details, and avoids all AI writing patterns. The hopeful ending provides satisfying closure while leaving room for continued exploration of the themes.

**Notable Strengths:**
- Strong science fiction concepts grounded in character emotion
- Effective use of quantum physics as both plot device and philosophical framework
- Well-developed supporting characters (Prometheus, Sarah, Yamamoto)
- Thoughtful exploration of consciousness and what it means to be "real"
- Powerful sacrifice and redemption arcs
- The Zero Point as both literal energy source and metaphor for connection
