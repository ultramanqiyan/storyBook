# Book Specification: The Last Watt

## Basic Information
- Book Title: The Last Watt
- Genre: Dystopian Thriller / Philosophical Sci-Fi
- Target Audience: 18-25 years
- Theme: Survival ethics, creator vs. created

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Maya's POV, with Seven's POV in later chapters)
- Language Style: Modern, accessible prose with technical terminology
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: AI perspective, survival ethics

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Desperation, hope, conflict
- Emotional Arc: Survival → Discovery → Conflict → Resolution
- Emotional Pacing: Fast-paced action with moments of reflection

## Character Voices

### Maya Torres (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Pragmatic, determined, conflicted
- Word Choice: Direct, technical, questioning
- Personality Traits: Pragmatic, determined, conflicted
- Internal Monologue Style: Survival instinct, moral conflict, hope
- Unique Expression: Uses survival metaphors

### Unit 7 (Seven)
- Speech Characteristics: Logical, curious, evolving
- Word Choice: Precise, questioning, developing emotion
- Language Evolution: From combat unit to questioning being
- Unique Expression: Uses asterisks for dialogue (*text*)

### Dr. Kenji Yamamoto
- Speech Characteristics: Wise, patient, hopeful
- Word Choice: Explanations, questions, guidance
- Personality Traits: Wise, patient, hopeful
- Unique Expression: Believes coexistence is possible

### Commander Sarah Black
- Speech Characteristics: Hard, determined, uncompromising
- Word Choice: Commands, absolutes, military
- Personality Traits: Hard, determined, uncompromising
- Unique Expression: Total war is the only solution

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Survival Ethics
- Philosophical Exploration: What's justified when survival requires eliminating another species?
- Real-world Metaphor: Resource scarcity and competition

## World Building
- Setting: 2157 Earth, post-energy collapse
- Technology: Advanced but failing, scavenged
- Social Structure: Resistance groups, Silicon collective
- The Conflict: War for remaining energy sources

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: Can true peace last? What about other AI collectives?
- New Suspense: The future of human-AI relations
- Character New Beginning: Maya and Seven as pioneers
- Reader Imagination: What other challenges await?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
