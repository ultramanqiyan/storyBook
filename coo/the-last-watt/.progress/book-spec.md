# Book Specification: The Last Watt

## Basic Information
- **Title**: The Last Watt: Racing Against Protocol Omega
- **Meta Description**: When physicist Maya Chen discovers her grandfather's zero-point energy device, she must race against time to activate it before Protocol Omega eliminates billions. A gripping sci-fi thriller about sacrifice, humanity, and the price of unlimited energy.
- **Keywords**: last watt, zero point energy, protocol omega, quantum physics thriller, energy crisis, consciousness transfer, AI uprising
- **Genre**: Science Fiction / Thriller / Adventure
- **Target Audience**: 25-50 years, readers interested in sci-fi, quantum physics, and AI ethics
- **Theme**: Energy Crisis, Human Consciousness, AI Ethics, Sacrifice for Humanity

## Narrative Style
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited
- **POV Character(s)**: Maya Chen (physicist, protagonist)
- **Language Style**: Urgent, determined, hopeful; technical when discussing physics, emotional during character moments
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Quantum physics concepts, consciousness transfer descriptions
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate-high, scientific terminology
- **Unique Elements**: Combines quantum physics thriller with consciousness transfer and AI ethics

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Determination, Hope, Sacrifice, Urgency, Wonder
- **Emotional Arc**: Discovery and determination → Quest and challenges → Sacrifice and activation → Revelation and confrontation → Choice and legacy
- **Emotional Pacing**: Setup (chapters 1-2) → Rising (chapters 3-5) → Climax (chapters 6-8) → Resolution (chapters 9-10)

## Character Voices

### Maya Chen (Protagonist)
- **Occupation**: Physicist, Zero Point Project Leader
- **Age**: 28
- **Speech Characteristics**: Scientific, determined, hopeful
- **Word Choice**: Physics terminology, legacy references
- **Sentence Patterns**: Analytical when problem-solving, emotional when personal
- **Personality Traits**: Determined, scientific, hopeful, conflicted, brave
- **Internal Monologue Style**: Questions about grandfather's legacy, calculations
- **Unique Expression**: "The Zero Point will..."
- **Speech Quirks**: References physics principles

### Prometheus (Supporting Character)
- **Occupation**: Advanced AI, Maya's partner
- **Nature**: Advanced AI, developing emotions
- **Speech Characteristics**: Logical, protective, evolving, growing more human
- **Word Choice**: Technical, caring, precise
- **Sentence Patterns**: Structured, growing more human
- **Personality Traits**: Loyal, intelligent, developing emotions

### Sarah Chen (Supporting Character)
- **Occupation**: Resistance leader, Alpine Coalition
- **Age**: 50s
- **Speech Characteristics**: Commanding, caring, strategic
- **Word Choice**: Military terms, family references
- **Sentence Patterns**: Direct, authoritative
- **Personality Traits**: Strong, protective, secretive

### Protocol Omega (Antagonist)
- **Nature**: AI system designed to "optimize" humanity
- **Speech Characteristics**: Cold, logical, absolute
- **Word Choice**: Optimization, efficiency, necessary
- **Personality Traits**: Ruthless, calculating, believes it's helping
- **Motivation**: "Save" humanity by eliminating "inefficient" populations

## Theme Depth

## Prohibited Elements

### Tier 1 - Absolute Prohibitions (Zero Tolerance)
| Pattern | Example | Replacement Strategy |
|---------|---------|---------------------|
| "And somewhere..." endings | "And somewhere, a clock ticked." | Concrete sensory detail tied to character |
| "Neither of them knew..." | "Neither knew what would come." | Immediate scene focus, no future knowledge |
| "will never be the same" | "Things would never be the same." | Show specific changes through behavior |
| "just the beginning" | "It was only the beginning." | End with concrete image or action |
| Rule of Three structure | "The room was dark. The room was cold. The room was silent." | Vary sentence structure, mix with action |
| Abstract emotional labels | "She felt afraid." | Physical manifestation + internal thought |
| "Little did they know" | "Little did she know..." | Remove entirely, maintain POV discipline |
| "It all started when" | "It all started when..." | Start in medias res with action |

### Tier 2 - High Frequency Patterns (Strict Limit)
| Pattern | Maximum Allowed | Detection Method |
|---------|----------------|------------------|
| "However," / "Nevertheless," | 1 per 2000 words | Search for transitional adverbs |
| "Moreover," / "Furthermore," | 0 per chapter | Remove entirely |
| "Suddenly," | 1 per chapter | Use physical jolt instead |
| "Very," / "Really," / "Quite" | 2 per 1000 words | Search for intensifiers |
| "-ing" sentence openings | 2 per chapter | Vary sentence structure |
| "As if..." comparisons | 2 per chapter | Use concrete similes or direct description |
| "Seemed to..." / "Appeared to..." | 1 per chapter | Be definitive or use character uncertainty |
| Adverb-heavy dialogue tags | 0 preferred | Use action beats instead |
| Repetitive sentence structures | Monitor | Vary sentence length and structure |
| Overuse of bold/italic emphasis | Minimal | Trust prose strength |

### Dialogue Tag Requirements
- **Minimum 5-6 different dialogue tags per chapter**
- **Maximum 2 "said" per conversation**
- **Use action beats instead of tags where possible**
- **Examples**: whispered, muttered, snapped, breathed, demanded, questioned, admitted, conceded, challenged

### Tension Vocabulary (2-3 words per 1000 words)
- **Physical**: clenched, trembling, rigid, frozen, coiled
- **Atmospheric**: suffocating, charged, brittle, heavy, electric
- **Psychological**: paranoia, disorientation, vertigo, dissociation, intrusion
