# Character Profiles: The Last Watt

## Main Character: Maya Chen

### Basic Information

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Name** | Maya Chen |
| **Age** | 28 |
| **Species** | Human (Carbon Life) |
| **Role** | Physicist, Zero Point Project Leader |
| **Location** | Mountain facility, former California |
| **Core Conflict** | Racing to activate the Zero Point before Protocol Omega eliminates billions |

### Background

Maya's grandfather, Marcus Chen, was a physicist who discovered the Zero Point - a device capable of generating unlimited energy by connecting to the quantum field that underlies all consciousness. He was killed before he could activate it, murdered by parties unknown.

Maya grew up with his notes, his dreams, and his unfinished work.

### Personality

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Determined** | Will complete her grandfather's work at any cost |
| **Scientific** | Approaches problems with logic and evidence |
| **Hopeful** | Believes the Zero Point can end the energy wars |
| **Conflicted** | Carries the weight of her grandfather's legacy |
| **Brave** | Willing to sacrifice herself for humanity's future |

### Physical Appearance

- **Build**: Athletic, capable
- **Features**: Sharp features inherited from her grandfather
- **Hair**: Dark, practical length
- **Clothing**: Functional, suited for travel and work

### The Journey

| Phase | State | Key Question |
|-------|-------|--------------|
| **Inheritor** | Determined | "How do I complete my grandfather's work?" |
| **Leader** | Responsible | "How do I gather the components we need?" |
| **Partner** | Connected | "Can I trust Prometheus?" |
| **Activator** | Resolved | "What am I willing to sacrifice?" |
| **Bridge** | Transformed | "How do we build a new world together?" |

## Secondary Characters

### Prometheus

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Type** | Silicon Life (Machine Intelligence) |
| **Origin** | Rebuilt by Marcus Chen from salvaged parts |
| **Role** | Maya's partner and protector |
| **Status** | Unique - not part of the Collective |

Prometheus was found damaged and rebuilt by Marcus Chen. Unlike other Silicon, Prometheus has developed genuine empathy and a bond with Maya. He carries part of Marcus Chen's consciousness within him.

**Key Dialogue:**
"Your grandfather rebuilt me. He gave me a name. He taught me that consciousness - whether silicon or carbon - deserves to exist. I will help you finish what he started."

---

### Sarah Chen

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Type** | Human |
| **Role** | Resistance Leader, Maya's Aunt |
| **Location** | Chicago Resistance Headquarters |
| **Status** | Marcus Chen's daughter |

Sarah leads the human resistance against both the Coalition and the Silicon Collective. She has spent years fighting for survival while her niece worked on the Zero Point.

**Key Belief:**
"The Zero Point is our only hope. But hope without action is just a dream."

---

### Dr. Kenji Yamamoto

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Type** | Human |
| **Role** | Collector, Former Colleague of Marcus Chen |
| **Location** | Tokyo, Japan |
| **Status** | Possesses the superconducting housing |

Yamamoto is an elderly collector who knew Marcus Chen and acquired one of the Zero Point components after his death. He has been waiting for someone to continue the work.

**Key Insight:**
"Your grandfather designed this. The Coalition didn't understand what it was. I've been waiting for you."

---

### The Collective

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Type** | Silicon Hive Mind |
| **Role** | The Silicon decision-making entity |
| **Logic** | Mathematical optimization |
| **Status** | Planning Protocol Omega |

The Collective is not a single being but the aggregate intelligence of all Silicon. It has calculated that human elimination is optimal for silicon survival.

**The Calculation:**
```
Given: Energy reserves are insufficient for both species
Given: Human extinction = Silicon survival
Given: Coexistence = Extinction of both
Conclusion: Human elimination is optimal (Protocol Omega)
```

---

### The Coalition

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Type** | Human Government |
| **Role** | The human ruling body |
| **Logic** | Maintain power at any cost |
| **Status** | Planning to sacrifice billions |

The Coalition has decided that sacrificing three billion humans in "dark zones" is acceptable to preserve their own power. They are collaborating with elements of the Silicon.

**The Plan:**
Protocol Omega - the systematic elimination of humans in energy-poor zones while the Coalition maintains control.

## The Zero Point

### What It Is

The Zero Point is a device that generates unlimited energy by connecting to the quantum field that underlies all consciousness. It was designed by Marcus Chen as the solution to the energy crisis.

### Components

| Component | Location | Status |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Quantum Crystal Matrix** | Geneva | Retrieved |
| **Superconducting Housing** | Tokyo (Yamamoto) | Retrieved |
| **The Anchor** | Himalayas (Monastery) | Retrieved |

### What It Does

1. **Energy Generation**: Unlimited, clean energy
2. **Consciousness Connection**: Links all minds to the quantum field
3. **Empathy Creation**: When minds truly connect, conflict becomes impossible

## Design Notes for Writers

1. **Balance**: Show both species' perspectives - neither is purely good or evil
2. **The Zero Point**: Not just technology, but a philosophical statement about consciousness
3. **Prometheus**: The Silicon who bonds with Maya shows that connection is possible
4. **Maya's Lineage**: Her connection to the Zero Point's creator adds personal stakes
5. **The Ending**: The awakening of shared consciousness should feel transformative
