## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: Third-person limited
- **POV Character(s)**: Sofia Reyes
- **POV Consistency**: Single POV throughout

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Artistic, sensory prose with philosophical depth
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, visual and tactile focus
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate - art terminology mixed with everyday language

## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **AI and Art**: Can AI create authentic beauty?
2. **Aesthetic Subjectivity**: Is beauty in the object or the experience?
3. **Authenticity**: What makes art "authentic"?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| Can AI create art? | Sofia uses AI tools in restoration and questions the results |
| Is beauty objective? | AI produces "perfect" restorations that feel different |
| What is authenticity? | The difference between original and AI-restored becomes unclear |
