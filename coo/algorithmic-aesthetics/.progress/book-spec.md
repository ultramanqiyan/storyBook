# Book Specification: Algorithmic Aesthetics

## Basic Information
- **Title**: Algorithmic Aesthetics: When Beauty Is Computed
- **Meta Description**: An antique restorer discovers AI can restore furniture better than she can. What happens when beauty becomes computable? A meditation on art and authenticity.
- **Keywords**: AI art, philosophy of aesthetics, art restoration, authenticity in art, beauty and technology
- **Genre**: Contemporary Fiction / Philosophical Fiction
- **Target Audience**: Adults 25-55, readers interested in philosophy of art and AI
- **Theme**: AI and Art, Aesthetic Subjectivity, Authenticity in Creation

## Narrative Style
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Sofia Reyes's POV)
- **Language Style**: Artistic, sensory prose with philosophical depth
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, visual and tactile focus
- **Unique Elements**: Craft terminology, Spanish expressions, restoration philosophy

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Contemplative, questioning, ultimately hopeful
- **Emotional Arc**: Confidence 竊?Doubt 竊?Crisis 竊?Understanding 竊?Integration
- **Emotional Pacing**: Slow build through philosophical exploration, emotional peaks at key revelations

## Character Voices

### Sofia Reyes (Protagonist)
- **Speech Characteristics**: Warm, precise, uses artistic metaphors
- **Word Choice**: Craft terminology, Spanish expressions, sensory vocabulary
- **Personality Traits**: Patient, detail-oriented, deeply connected to history and craft
- **Internal Monologue Style**: Reflective, questioning, processing through sensory detail
- **Unique Expression**: Describes furniture as having "memory" and "stories"
- **Development Focus**: From confident craftsperson to questioning philosopher to integrated practitioner

### Marcus Chen (Supporting)
- **Speech Characteristics**: Enthusiastic, technical, optimistic about technology
- **Word Choice**: Tech terminology, startup language, future-focused
- **Personality Traits**: Ambitious, genuinely curious, well-meaning but sometimes tone-deaf
- **Internal Monologue Style**: Not shown (not POV character)
- **Unique Expression**: Frames everything as "progress" and "potential"
- **Development Focus**: Learns to value human judgment alongside AI capabilities

### Margaret Chen (Supporting - Chapter 9)
- **Speech Characteristics**: Academic, measured, emotionally articulate
- **Word Choice**: Literary references, family history, emotional vocabulary
- **Personality Traits**: Thoughtful, connected to heritage, emotionally intelligent
- **Internal Monologue Style**: Not shown (not POV character)
- **Unique Expression**: Connects objects to family stories and generational memory
- **Development Focus**: Helps Sofia understand the meaning of preservation

## Theme Depth
- **Core Theme**: AI and Art - Can AI create authentic beauty?
- **Philosophical Exploration**: What makes something authentic? Is beauty objective or subjective? What is the relationship between perfection and meaning?
- **Real-world Metaphor**: The tension between technological efficiency and human meaning in creative work

## Prohibited Elements

### Tier 1 - Absolute Prohibitions (Zero Tolerance)
| Pattern | Example | Replacement Strategy |
|---------|---------|---------------------|
| "And somewhere..." endings | "And somewhere, a clock ticked." | Concrete sensory detail |
| "Neither of them knew..." | "Neither knew what would come." | Immediate scene focus |
| "It was as if..." | "It was as if time stood still." | Direct description |
| "Little did she know..." | "Little did she know..." | Remove entirely |
| "The truth was..." | "The truth was she had always..." | Direct statement |

### Tier 2 - High Frequency Patterns (Strict Limit)
| Pattern | Maximum Allowed | Detection Method |
|---------|----------------|------------------|
| "---" separator | 2 per chapter | Count occurrences |
| "However," / "Nevertheless," | 1 per 2000 words | Search for transitional adverbs |
| "Moreover," / "Furthermore," | 0 per chapter | Remove entirely |
| "She felt a..." | 3 per chapter | Vary emotional description |
| "She thought about..." | 5 per chapter | Use action or dialogue instead |

- **Minimum 5-6 different dialogue tags per chapter**
