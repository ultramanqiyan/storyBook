## 4. Opening

The salesperson from RestoreAI arrived on a Tuesday morning, carrying a tablet and a portable scanner. "This will change everything," she said. Sofia had heard that before - about new finishes, new tools, new techniques. But this was different. This was AI that could analyze a piece of furniture and determine exactly how it had looked originally - the exact shade of finish, the precise pattern of the upholstery, the original shape of damaged elements.

---

## 5. Ending

Sofia scanned the damaged table and watched as the AI produced its analysis. The original finish had been a specific shade of mahogany. The original hardware had been brass, not the iron that had replaced it. The original proportions had been slightly different. The AI knew all of this from analyzing the remaining original material. Sofia wasn't sure if she was impressed or disturbed.
