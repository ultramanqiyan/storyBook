## 4. Opening

Sofia decided to test the AI properly. She would restore a piece using her traditional methods, while the AI produced its own restoration plan. Then she would compare. The piece was a 1920s Art Deco cabinet with water damage and missing hardware. Sofia knew how she would restore it. The question was: how would the AI?

---

## 5. Ending

The two restorations sat side by side. Sofia's was good - she knew that. But the AI's was... different. More precise. More historically accurate. The finish matched the original exactly. The proportions were perfect. Sofia looked at her own work and saw, for the first time in years, the tiny imperfections she'd learned to accept as part of the craft. The AI had no such imperfections. And she couldn't decide if that was better or worse.
