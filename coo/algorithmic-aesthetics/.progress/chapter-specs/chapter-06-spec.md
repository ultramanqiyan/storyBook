## 4. Opening

Sofia set up the test carefully. She restored five pieces using her traditional methods. She restored five similar pieces using the AI's guidance. Then she mixed them up and tried to identify which was which. It should have been easy. She'd been doing this work for forty years. She should be able to recognize her own hand.

---

## 5. Ending

Sofia couldn't tell. She couldn't reliably identify which restorations were hers and which were the AI's. The differences were too subtle, too small. Or maybe there were no differences that mattered. She sat in her workshop, surrounded by furniture she couldn't distinguish from machine work, and felt something she hadn't felt in forty years: doubt about her craft.
