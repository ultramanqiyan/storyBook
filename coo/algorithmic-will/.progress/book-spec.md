- **POV Character(s)**: Anna Nowak

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Clinical but warm prose; increasingly introspective
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, precise but human
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate - counseling terminology mixed with everyday language

## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Free Will**: Do we have genuine choice?
2. **Determinism**: Can our choices be predicted?
3. **Addiction and Freedom**: Is addiction a failure of will or a predictable pattern?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| Is free will real? | Anna participates in a neural prediction study |
| Can choices be predicted? | The study predicts Anna's decisions before she's aware of them |
| What is addiction? | Anna questions whether her clients' struggles are truly "choices" |

## 6. Open Ending Design

### 6.1 Unresolved Questions
1. Is free will real or an illusion?
2. Can we make meaningful choices if our brains are predictable?
