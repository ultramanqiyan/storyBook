## 4. Opening

The lab was nothing like Anna had imagined. She'd expected something clinical - white walls, cold equipment. Instead, it looked almost cozy, with warm lighting and comfortable chairs. The researcher, Dr. Patel, explained what would happen. "We'll show you two images. You choose the one you prefer. Simple as that."

---

## 5. Ending

The test lasted two hours. Anna made hundreds of choices - left or right, this image or that, this word or that one. By the end, she was tired but curious. "When do I find out the results?" she asked. Dr. Patel smiled. "We'll have them analyzed by next week. I think you'll find them interesting."
