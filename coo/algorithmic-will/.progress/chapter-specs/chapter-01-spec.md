## 4. Opening

Anna Nowak sat across from her client, the way she had thousands of times before. Mark was trying to quit smoking - again - and Anna was helping him find the reason that would finally stick. "Why do you want to quit?" she asked, the question she always started with. The answer mattered more than any technique.

---

## 5. Ending

Anna drove home through Detroit's evening traffic, thinking about Mark's breakthrough. He'd finally admitted what she'd suspected for months: he didn't want to quit for his health. He wanted to quit because he was ashamed. The shame was the real addiction. The cigarettes were just the symptom. This was why she did this work - helping people find the truth beneath the behavior.
