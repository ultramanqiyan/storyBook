## 5. Key Paragraph Specifications

### Opening
The news broke before the board was ready. Someone had leaked information about the judgment algorithm, and the media was in a frenzy. Headlines proclaimed: "AI to Judge Citizens' Worth" "Algorithm Will Decide Who Gets Resources" "The End of Human Discretion?" James watched the coverage, knowing the reality was more complex than the headlines suggested.

### Ending
James gave interview after interview, trying to explain the nuances. The algorithm wasn't inherently evil. It could be a tool for fairness, for consistency, for removing human prejudice. But it could also encode bias at scale, hiding discrimination behind the veneer of mathematical objectivity. The question was not whether to use AI for judgment. The question was how to ensure that the judgment was fair. And that question had no easy answers.
