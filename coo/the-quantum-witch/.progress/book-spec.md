# Book Specification: The Quantum Witch

## Basic Information
- Book Title: The Quantum Witch
- Genre: Science Fantasy
- Target Audience: All ages
- Theme: Intersection of science and magic, probability and fate

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Dr. Sarah Chen's POV)
- Language Style: Contemporary, accessible prose with scientific and magical elements
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: Quantum mechanics as magic, probability manipulation

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Wonder, discovery, responsibility
- Emotional Arc: Discovery → Power → Responsibility → Wisdom
- Emotional Pacing: Gradual build, accelerating toward resolution

## Character Voices

### Dr. Sarah Chen (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Scientific, curious, growing in power
- Word Choice: Scientific terms mixed with wonder
- Personality Traits: Scientific, curious, growing in power
- Internal Monologue Style: Analytical, wondering
- Unique Expression: Uses quantum metaphors for magic

### The Quantum Entity
- Speech Characteristics: Probability-based, cryptic, wise
- Word Choice: Probabilities, possibilities, warnings
- Language Evolution: From alien presence to wise guide
- Unique Expression: Speaks in probabilities and possibilities

### The Antagonist
- Speech Characteristics: Power-hungry, manipulative, seductive
- Word Choice: Power, control, destiny
- Personality Traits: Power-hungry, manipulative, seductive
- Unique Expression: Offers power without responsibility

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Science and Magic's Intersection
- Philosophical Exploration: Can probability be controlled? What is fate?
- Real-world Metaphor: Quantum computing as modern magic

## World Building
- Setting: Near-future Earth with hidden magic
- Magic System: Quantum mechanics as magic, probability manipulation
- Social Structure: Hidden magical practitioners, scientific community
- The Power: Probability manipulation

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: What other probabilities exist? What are the limits?
- New Suspense: Sarah's continuing journey
- Character New Beginning: Sarah as responsible practitioner
- Reader Imagination: What would you do with probability control?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
