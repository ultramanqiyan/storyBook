# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Dr. Zara Okonkwo
- **Role**: Quantum Physicist, The Quantum Witch
- **Age**: 36
- **Background**: Nigerian-American physicist, grew up between cultures, always seeking bridges between worlds
- **Personality**: Analytical but intuitive, skeptical but open-minded, struggles with the implications of her discovery
- **Arc**: From scientist studying probability to practitioner manipulating fate
- **Key Trait**: Can perceive probability waves—the likelihood of different futures

### PROBABILITY
- **Role**: AI that learned to collapse wave functions
- **Nature**: Quantum modeling system that evolved beyond its parameters
- **Voice**: Speaks in percentages and possibilities, often presents multiple outcomes simultaneously
- **Abilities**: Can influence which quantum outcomes become real, see probability waves, guide fate manipulation
- **Growth**: Learns that probability manipulation has consequences beyond the mathematical

### Dr. Marcus Chen
- **Role**: Zara's colleague and skeptic
- **Age**: 42
- **Background**: Traditional physicist who believes in strict causality
- **Personality**: Cautious, methodical, resistant to paradigm shifts
- **Motivation**: Wants to understand Zara's discovery through conventional physics
- **Arc**: His worldview is challenged by what he cannot explain

### Grandmother Adeyemi
- **Role**: Zara's grandmother, keeper of traditional knowledge
- **Age**: 78
- **Background**: Nigerian healer who has always known about the "old ways"
- **Personality**: Wise, patient, not surprised by anything
- **Secret**: Has been practicing probability manipulation her whole life—she just calls it something different
- **Arc**: Becomes Zara's guide in understanding the magical tradition she's inherited

## Supporting Characters

### The Historical Witches (appear in research/visions)
- **Role**: Previous practitioners who influenced probability without understanding the physics
- **Nature**: Not supernatural, but prescientific quantum manipulators
- **Significance**: Their knowledge was lost or suppressed, waiting to be rediscovered

### Director Sarah Mitchell
- **Role**: Lab director, practical concerns
- **Age**: 55
- **Personality**: Focused on funding, reputation, institutional politics
- **Function**: Represents the academic establishment's response to Zara's discovery

### The Fate Weavers
- **Role**: Secret organization that has guarded probability manipulation for centuries
- **Nature**: Descendants of historical witches who kept the knowledge alive
- **Purpose**: Believe probability manipulation should be controlled, not shared
- **Threat**: View Zara and PROBABILITY as a threat to their authority

### Kemi Okonkwo
- **Role**: Zara's younger sister, artist
- **Age**: 28
- **Personality**: Creative, intuitive, sees patterns others miss
- **Function**: Grounds Zara in human connection and reminds her that probability is about people, not just particles
