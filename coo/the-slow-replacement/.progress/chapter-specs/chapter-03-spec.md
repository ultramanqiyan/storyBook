## Character States

### Marcus Chen
- **Mental State**: Obsessive, anxious, trying to regain control through numbers
- **Motivation**: Wants to prove his value, fears being "optimized" like David
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Creates spreadsheets, tracks metrics, works longer hours
  - Inner: *If I can just show the numbers, they'll see I'm valuable. I'm not like David. I'm better.*
  - Deep: Desperate need for control in an uncontrollable situation

### Sarah Chen
- **Mental State**: Worried, confused, hurt by Marcus's rejection
- **Motivation**: Wants to understand what's happening, wants to help
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: "You're working late again. The kids miss you."
  - Inner: *Why won't he talk to me? What's happening at work?*
  - Deep: Fear of losing connection with Marcus, feeling helpless

### David Park (via text/call)
- **Mental State**: Still angry, but starting to process
- **Motivation**: Wants to vent, wants Marcus to understand

  - Deep: Loss of self-worth, questioning his value
