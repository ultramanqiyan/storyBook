# Chapter 01 Specification: The Slow Replacement

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Introduce Marcus, his life, and the first sign of the AI threat
- **Emotional Arc**: Calm routine → Growing unease → First crack in composure
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Sets up the protagonist's world and introduces the central conflict

## Core Events
1. Marcus receives company email announcing "Codex Pro" AI system (Unease)
2. Colleague David gets called into meeting room, comes out pale (Concern)
3. Marcus notices code reviews becoming unusually strict (Anxiety begins)

## Character States

### Marcus Chen
- **Mental State**: Initially calm, following his routine. Gradually becomes uneasy as he processes the AI announcement
- **Motivation**: Wants to understand what the AI system means for his job
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Continues working normally, checks email, attends meetings
  - Inner: *What does this mean? Is this just another tool? Or something more?*
  - Deep: First stirring of fear—what if I'm no longer needed?

### Sarah Chen
- **Mental State**: Busy with children, notices Marcus is distracted at dinner but doesn't probe
- **Motivation**: Wants family dinner to be normal, doesn't want to worry yet
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Talks about children's day, asks about Marcus's work
  - Inner: *He seems distracted. Is everything okay at work?*
  - Deep: Fear of change, wants stability

### David Park
- **Mental State**: Anxious, knows something is coming
- **Motivation**: Trying to stay calm, hoping for the best
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Jokes about the AI system, "Maybe it'll do my work for me"
  - Inner: *I've seen this before. People get called into meetings. They don't come back.*
  - Deep: Fear of unemployment, financial insecurity

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Deceptively normal, with undercurrent of unease
- **Emotional Progression**:
  - Opening: Routine morning—BART commute, coffee, standup meeting
  - Middle: AI announcement creates ripple of unease through office
  - Closing: Marcus lying awake at 2am, searching "AI replacing developers"

## Narrative Techniques
- **POV**: Third person limited, Marcus's perspective
- **Pacing**: Slow, building routine, then sudden disruption with AI announcement
- **Suspense Elements**:
  - What is "Codex Pro"?
  - Why was David called into the meeting?
  - What does this mean for Marcus?

## Dialogue Style Guide

### Marcus's Dialogue
- Uses technical analogies: "This feels like a major version update we weren't prepared for"
- Short sentences when anxious: "Okay. Sure. I'll check it out."
- Self-questioning: "Is this... is this what I think it is?"

### Sarah's Dialogue
- Warm, practical: "How was your day? Emma drew a picture at school today."
- Doesn't push: "You seem tired. Long day?"

### David's Dialogue
- Joking to mask fear: "Hey, maybe the AI will finally fix that legacy code we've been avoiding"
- Sarcastic: "Another 'game-changer' from upper management. Can't wait."
