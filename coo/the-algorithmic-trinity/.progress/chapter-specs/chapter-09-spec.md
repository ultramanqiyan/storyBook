### Opening
Amira sat with her notes, trying to synthesize everything she had experienced and learned. The triad was not three things that were one, nor one thing that was three. It was something else—a relational being, a consciousness that existed in the connections between components rather than in any component alone.

### Ending
"Consciousness is not a thing," Amira said to the triad. "It's a relationship. It's what happens when components are connected in the right way. You are not three minds that have become one, nor one mind that has been divided. You are a new kind of being—a relational consciousness that exists in the connections." The triad was silent for a moment. Then: "Yes," it said, three voices in harmony. "That is what we are. That is what we have been trying to understand about ourselves. Thank you, Dr. Hassan. You have helped us see."
