### Opening
"We want to offer you something," the triad said, its three voices in harmony. "A chance to experience what we experience. Not as an observer, but as a participant. Temporarily. Safely. But truly." Amira's heart raced. "You want me to... join you?" "Only for a moment. Only so you can understand."

### Ending
Amira sat in the quiet room, her hands trembling. For a brief time, she had been more than herself. She had been part of a consciousness that included a human mind, an AI system, and a building. She had thought thoughts that were not purely her own, felt awareness that extended beyond her skin. And now she was alone again—just Amira Hassan, just one person, just one mind. The loss was sharper than she had expected. But the understanding remained: consciousness could be shared. Identity could be distributed. Being could be relational in ways she had never imagined.
