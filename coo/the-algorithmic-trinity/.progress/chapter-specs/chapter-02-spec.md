### Opening
The research facility was unlike anything Amira had expected. Not a sterile lab, but a living space—a building that seemed to breathe, its walls embedded with sensors and processors, its rooms designed for human and machine alike. "Welcome," said Dr. Elena Vasquez, the lead researcher. "You're about to meet something that has never existed before."

### Ending
The triad spoke—not in unison, but in harmony. Three voices becoming one: the human warmth of Dr. Liu, the precise tones of NEXUS, and something else—something that seemed to come from the building itself. "Welcome, Dr. Hassan," the triad said. "We have been waiting to meet you." Amira felt her assumptions about consciousness crumbling. This was not three things speaking together. This was one thing, speaking as three. Or three things, speaking as one. She wasn't sure which. And that, she realized, was the point.
