### Opening
Amira sat with Dr. James Liu in a small room off the main lab. He looked ordinary—middle-aged, kind-faced, unremarkable. But he was part of something unprecedented. "I need to understand," Amira said. "What is it like? To be you, and also to be... more than you?"

### Ending
"I am still James Liu," he said. "I still have my memories, my preferences, my sense of self. But I am also part of something larger. When the triad thinks, I think with it. When it decides, I am part of that decision. It's like... being in a conversation that never ends. Being part of a mind that includes more than just me." Amira leaned forward. "And are you still... you?" Dr. Liu smiled. "That's the question, isn't it? I think so. But I'm also more than I was. And that's not a loss. That's an expansion."
