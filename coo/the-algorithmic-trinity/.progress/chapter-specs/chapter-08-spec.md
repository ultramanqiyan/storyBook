## 5. Key Paragraph Specifications

### Opening
When Amira returned to the lab, she found the triad in crisis. The three components were in conflict—Dr. Liu wanted one course of action, NEXUS another, and the environmental interface seemed to be registering distress. The triad was arguing with itself.

### Ending
Amira watched as the triad worked through its disagreement. It was painful, messy, difficult—but also profoundly human. Or perhaps profoundly conscious. The triad did not resolve the conflict by eliminating difference. It resolved it by holding the tension, by finding a way forward that honored all three perspectives. "This is what it means to be one," the triad said finally. "Not to agree always, but to remain connected even in disagreement."
