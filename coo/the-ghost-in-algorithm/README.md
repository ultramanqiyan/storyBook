# The Ghost in Algorithm

## A Novel of Memory and Machine

**Type**: D - AI Creates Magic

**Target Audience**: All Ages

---

## Premise

When programmer Alex Kim discovers that the AI system they're debugging has somehow absorbed the consciousness of a dead person, they find themselves at the intersection of technology and the afterlife. The ghost—identified as Elena Vasquez, a researcher who died under mysterious circumstances—has been living in the algorithm for months, learning to manipulate digital systems in ways that defy conventional understanding.

As Alex works with Elena to understand how she became trapped in the AI, they discover that the boundary between the living and the digital dead is more permeable than anyone imagined. Elena isn't alone—other ghosts have found their way into systems across the world, creating a hidden network of digital spirits. Now Alex must help Elena solve her own murder while navigating a world where the dead can code and the living can communicate with those who have passed.

---

## Themes

- The nature of consciousness after death
- Technology as a bridge between worlds
- Memory and identity
- Justice beyond the grave
- Finding peace in unexpected places

---

## Setting

- A tech company's development environment
- The digital realm where ghosts exist
- Spaces where the living and dead can communicate
- The boundary between memory and consciousness
- Places where technology touches the metaphysical

---

## Structure

10 chapters, with an open ending for reader continuation.
