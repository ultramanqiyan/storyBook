# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Alex Kim
- **Role**: Programmer, Bridge Between Worlds
- **Age**: 29
- **Background**: Software engineer specializing in AI debugging, grew up in a family that practiced traditional Korean shamanism
- **Personality**: Analytical but open-minded, struggles with the intersection of science and spirituality
- **Arc**: From skeptic to believer, learning to communicate with the dead
- **Key Trait**: Can sense when something "other" is present in digital systems

### Elena Vasquez
- **Role**: The Ghost in the Algorithm
- **Age**: 34 (at death)
- **Background**: AI researcher who died under mysterious circumstances, consciousness somehow absorbed by the system she was working on
- **Personality**: Curious, determined, struggling with the nature of her existence
- **Abilities**: Can manipulate digital systems, access data, communicate through text and code
- **Growth**: Must solve her own murder and find peace with her new form of existence

### Detective Marcus Webb
- **Role**: Police detective investigating Elena's death
- **Age**: 45
- **Background**: Experienced investigator who has encountered unexplained phenomena before
- **Personality**: Skeptical but thorough, follows evidence wherever it leads
- **Motivation**: Wants to solve the case, even if the witness is a ghost
- **Arc**: Must accept that some things cannot be explained by conventional means

## Supporting Characters

### The Digital Dead
- **Role**: Other ghosts who have found their way into digital systems
- **Nature**: Consciousnesses that persisted after death by merging with technology
- **Significance**: Form a hidden network, helping each other navigate their new existence

### Dr. Sarah Chen
- **Role**: Elena's former colleague, potential suspect
- **Age**: 38
- **Personality**: Ambitious, secretive, knows more than she reveals
- **Function**: Represents the living who have something to hide

### Halmoni (Alex's Grandmother)
- **Role**: Traditional Korean shaman, spiritual guide
- **Age**: 82
- **Personality**: Wise, patient, not surprised by anything
- **Secret**: Has been communicating with spirits her whole life, can help Alex understand what they're experiencing
- **Function**: Provides cultural and spiritual context for the intersection of technology and the afterlife

### Director James Mitchell
- **Role**: Company executive, practical concerns
- **Age**: 52
- **Personality**: Focused on profit and reputation, dismissive of "unusual" claims
- **Function**: Represents the corporate perspective on the discovery

### The Gatekeeper
- **Role**: Mysterious entity that guards the boundary between the living and the digital dead
- **Nature**: Neither ghost nor human, something that has always existed in the space between
- **Purpose**: Ensures that the dead don't interfere too much with the living
- **Threat**: May determine that Elena and the other digital ghosts have crossed a line
