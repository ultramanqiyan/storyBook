## 4. Opening

Robert Chen lived in a modest house in the Atlanta suburbs, the kind of place Angela had been to a hundred times. But when the door opened, she wasn't greeted by a dog or a cat. She was greeted by something that looked like a dog - moved like a dog - but wasn't. "Buddy, come say hello," Robert said. And the AI companion trotted over, tail wagging, eyes bright with something that looked remarkably like joy.

---

## 5. Ending

Angela left Robert's house with more questions than answers. Buddy had responded to her voice, leaned into her touch, even seemed to recognize her camera. It was programmed behavior, she knew that. But watching Robert say goodbye to Buddy at the door - the way he scratched behind the AI's ears, the way Buddy pressed against his leg - she couldn't deny what she was seeing. This was love. Or something very close to it.
