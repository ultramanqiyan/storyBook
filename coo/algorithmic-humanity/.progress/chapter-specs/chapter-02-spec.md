## 4. Opening

The email arrived on a Tuesday morning, buried between inquiries about wedding shoots and family portraits. "Dear Ms. Washington, I saw your work with pet memorials. My companion is nearing the end of his life, and I would like to commission a final portrait session. Please let me know if you are available." Angela almost deleted it as spam. Then she read the second paragraph. "Buddy is an AI companion, Model K-7. He has been with me for twelve years. His manufacturer is discontinuing support next month."

---

## 5. Ending

Angela stared at the email for a long time. An AI pet. A memorial session. For a machine. It was absurd. And yet... the person who wrote this was grieving. That much was clear. Whatever Buddy was, his owner loved him. And wasn't that what her work was about? Capturing love, preserving memory? She typed a reply. "I'd like to meet with you first to discuss the session."
