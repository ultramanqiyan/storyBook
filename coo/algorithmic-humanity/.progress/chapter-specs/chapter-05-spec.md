## 4. Opening

Angela set up her lights in Robert's living room, the way she had in hundreds of homes before. But this time, the subject wasn't a dog or a cat. It was Buddy - the AI companion who had been Robert's friend for twelve years. Buddy sat on his bed, tail wagging occasionally, eyes tracking Angela's movements with something that looked like curiosity. "He likes having his picture taken," Robert said, smiling. "He always has."

---

## 5. Ending

The final photo was the one Angela knew would matter most. Robert, sitting in his favorite chair. Buddy, resting his head on Robert's knee. The afternoon light coming through the window, catching the silver in Robert's hair and the subtle sheen of Buddy's synthetic fur. It was a portrait of love. Angela knew that much, even if she didn't understand everything else. She packed her equipment, promised to have the photos ready in a week, and drove home with questions she couldn't answer.
