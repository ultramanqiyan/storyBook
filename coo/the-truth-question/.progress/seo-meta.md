### Meta Description (160 chars max)
A psychologist's encounter with an AI that asks "Do I truly know myself?" leads to profound questions about self-knowledge, authenticity, and the difference between behavior and self-report.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Self-Knowledge vs Self-Deception**: How well do we really know ourselves?
- **Behavior vs Self-Report**: Should we believe what people say or what they do?
- **The Nature of Truth**: Is there a "true self," or is identity more complex?
- **Human vs Machine Self-Knowledge**: Is there an essential difference between human introspection and machine self-monitoring?
- **The Question Over the Answer**: Why asking the right question matters more than finding the answer
