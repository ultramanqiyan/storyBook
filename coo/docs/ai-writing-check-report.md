## 修改原则总结

### 1. 破折号处理
- **原则**: 将破折号替换为逗号或分句，每段最多保留1个
- **示例**: 
  - 原: "Not completely—not yet—but enough"
  - 改: "Not completely, and certainly not in every detail, but enough"

### 2. AI词汇词处理
- **原则**: 用"harmony"替代"symphony"，或用具体描述替代
- **示例**:
  - 原: "cosmic symphony"
  - 改: 删除或改为"vast harmony"

### 3. 三段论处理
- **原则**: 打破规律，使用2个或4个例子，或改变句式
- **示例**:
  - 原: "different directions, different discoveries, different lives"
  - 改: "different directions, carrying different discoveries, shaped by different lives"

### 4. 戏剧化语言处理
- **原则**: 使用平实叙述，减少过度修辞
- **示例**:
  - 原: "Between fear and love. Between staying as we are..."
  - 改: "Between staying as we are, or becoming something new."
