## Series Core Concept

**Central Metaphor**: When AI becomes the gatekeeper of knowledge, a new "Brahmin class" emerges—not through bloodline, but through "cognitive privilege." The底层 people are not forbidden from reading; they are deprived of "the ability to understand."

**Philosophical Core**:
> True oppression is not forbidding you from seeing something, but making you unable to understand even when you see it.

**焚书坑儒 Metaphor**:
- **焚书 (Burning Books)**: Knowledge is drowned in noise, not burned
- **坑儒 (Burying Scholars)**: Knowledge holders are "corrected," not killed
