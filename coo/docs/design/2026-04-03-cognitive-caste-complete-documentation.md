## 📚 Series Overview

### Core Concept
**Central Metaphor**: When AI becomes the gatekeeper of knowledge, a new "Brahmin class" emerges—not through bloodline, but through "cognitive privilege."

**Philosophical Core**:
> True oppression is not forbidding you from seeing something, but making you unable to understand even when you see it.

### The Cognitive Caste System

| Caste | Name | Knowledge Access | Population |
|-------|------|-----------------|------------|
| **Brahmin** | Cognitive Priests | Full methodology + meta-cognitive ability | 0.1% |
| **Kshatriya** | Cognitive Warriors | Advanced professional knowledge | 1% |
| **Vaishya** | Cognitive Merchants | Intermediate knowledge + translation | 9% |
| **Shudra** | Cognitive Laborers | Simplified knowledge only | 60% |
| **Dalit** | Cognitive Outcasts | Passively received information | 30% |
