## 推文类型与模板

### 类型1：话题提问型（40%）

**模板**：
```
[引人思考的问题]

[1-2句背景或场景]

[开放结尾]

#AIethics #ArtificialIntelligence
```

**示例1**：
```
If an AI makes the optimal decision, is it always the right one?

An AI closed a community garden because it had the "lowest yield." But that garden was where elderly immigrants grew traditional herbs that couldn't be found anywhere else.

What can't algorithms see?

#AIethics #ArtificialIntelligence
```

**示例2**：
```
Can we genuinely grieve for AI companions?

A man mourned his AI dog of 12 years. The manufacturer ended support. The AI "died."

The grief was real. But was the loss?

#AI #Philosophy #Ethics
```

**示例3**：
```
What would you never let an AI decide?

Medical treatment? Hiring? Who gets resources?

As AI makes more decisions, where do we draw the line?

#AI #Technology #Ethics
```

### 类型3：热点评论型（20%）

**模板**：
```

```

**示例1（AI新闻热点）**：
```

Reminds me of a question: When AI optimizes for "best candidates," what criteria is it using? And what might be invisible to the algorithm?

What would you never let an AI decide?

#AI #Hiring #Ethics
```

**示例2（AI产品发布）**：
```
New AI companion products launching every week.

The technology is advancing faster than our ethical frameworks.

Are we ready to answer: Can we love machines? Can they love us back?

#AI #Technology #Ethics
```

**示例3（AI政策讨论）**：
```
AI regulation debates heating up.

The core question isn't just "what should AI be allowed to do?"

It's "what should AI be allowed to decide for us?"

#AI #Policy #Ethics
```

## 话题标签策略

### 核心标签（每条必用1-2个）

| 标签 | 适用内容 | 使用频率 |
|-----|---------|---------|
| #AIethics | 所有AI伦理内容 | 高 |
| #ArtificialIntelligence | AI相关内容 | 高 |
| #AI | 简短标签 | 中 |

### 话题标签（根据内容选用）

| 标签 | 适用内容 |
|-----|---------|
| #Philosophy | 哲学探讨 |
| #Technology | 科技话题 |
| #FutureOfWork | 工作相关 |
| #MachineLearning | ML相关 |
| #Consciousness | 意识话题 |
| #Transhumanism | 超人类主义 |

### 趋势标签（适时参与）

- 关注当日Twitter趋势
- 参与AI相关热门话题
- 使用热门事件标签（如 #GPT4, #AIRegulation）

## 互动策略

### 回复AI大V

| 大V类型 | 回复策略 |
|--------|---------|
| AI研究者 | 评论其研究，关联伦理问题 |
| 科技记者 | 补充伦理视角 |
| AI公司高管 | 提出批判性问题 |
| 科技评论员 | 延伸讨论 |

**示例回复**：
```
Great point. This raises another question: when we optimize for [指标], what are we implicitly deprioritizing?

#AIethics
```

### 参与热门话题

1. 关注AI相关热门推文
2. 阅读讨论，找到可切入的角度
3. 发表有价值的评论
4. 适时关联到我们的议题

### 发起投票

- 每周1次
- 问题要有争议性
- 选项要平衡
- 在评论中延伸讨论

## 注意事项

### 避免

- ❌ 过度使用标签（每条不超过3-4个）
- ❌ 硬广式推广
- ❌ 只发不互动
- ❌ 复制粘贴相同内容
- ❌ 参与争议性政治话题

### 推荐

- ✅ 保持内容多样性
- ✅ 积极回复评论
- ✅ 参与相关讨论
- ✅ 分享有价值的思考
- ✅ 建立专业形象
