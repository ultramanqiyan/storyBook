### 7.1 数据驱动叙事亮点

**系统性歧视的揭示：**
```
"Every person who left under pressure in three years was a woman or person of color. Every person promoted was a white man."
```
→ 数据驱动，揭示系统性问题

### 7.2 对抗场景亮点

**与Marcus的对峙：**
```
"Meritocracy isn't about equality of outcome. It's about equality of opportunity."

```
→ 对话真实，揭示"文化契合"的歧视本质

### 7.3 结局设计亮点
