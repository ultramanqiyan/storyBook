## 七、优秀实践记录

### 7.1 概念创新亮点

**需求黑洞理论：**
```
"Demand wasn't simply a function of income and price. It was a function of identity, community, and meaning. People bought things not just because they could afford them, but because those things helped them express who they were, connect with others, and participate in a shared culture."
```
→ 概念创新，深刻揭示AI时代经济困境

### 7.2 核心洞察亮点

**努力=意义：**
```
"We spent so much time making things easier, we forgot that difficulty has value. We eliminated effort, and we eliminated meaning along with it."
```
→ 核心洞察深刻有力

### 7.3 转变设计亮点

**从消费到创造：**
```
"After desire comes effort. After consumption comes creation. After isolation comes community."
```
→ 转变设计巧妙，主题升华
