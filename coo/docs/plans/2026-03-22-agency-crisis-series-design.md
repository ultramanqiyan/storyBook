# Five New Books Design: Agency Crisis Series

> **Design Version**: 1.0
> **Created**: 2026-03-22
> **Theme**: Agency Crisis - Who is Thinking? Who is Acting?
> **Language**: English (US)

---

## 1. Series Overview

### 1.1 Core Theme
**Agency Crisis**: When AI can autonomously plan, execute tools, and complete complex tasks, the human position as the sole agent is challenged.

### 1.2 Three Dimensions

| Dimension | Description | Books |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| **Cognitive Outsourcing Trap** | Humans outsource memory, judgment, and decision-making to AI, leading to cognitive atrophy | Book 1, Book 2 |
| **Self-Concept Blurring** | When AI writes papers, brainstorms, and sends emails, the fundamental question arises: which thoughts truly belong to "me"? | Book 3, Book 4 |
| **Intentionality Absence** | AI lacks true qualia consciousness and free will, cannot be a true aesthetic or moral subject | Book 5 |

### 1.3 Philosophical Questions Chain

```
Book 1: Memory Outsourced → Is thinking = pattern recognition?
    ↓
Book 2: Understanding Outsourced → Is understanding = knowing the answer?
    ↓
Book 3: Expression Outsourced → Is self = behavior pattern?
    ↓
Book 4: Relationship Outsourced → Is care = remember + respond?
    ↓
Book 5: Meaning Outsourced → Does meaning come from purpose fulfillment or purpose setting?
```

## 3. Common Quality Standards

### 3.1 Sensory Detail Requirements
- **Minimum per chapter**: 5 sensory details
- **Minimum categories per chapter**: 3 types
- **Distribution target**: Visual (40%), Others (60% combined)

### 3.2 Emotional Depth Model (Three Layers)
Every significant emotional moment must include:
- **Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**: External behavior, visible actions
- **Layer 2 - Internal Activity**: Thought process, inner dialogue
- **Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**: Underlying fears, desires, beliefs

### 3.3 Prohibited Elements
- AI writing patterns (vague location endings, cliché transitions, etc.)
- Abstract emotion statements ("He felt sad")
- Template dialogue ("What do you mean?" / "You know what I mean")
- Deus ex machina resolutions
- Perfect character consistency

### 3.4 Word Count Standards
- **Per Chapter**: 3,000-6,000 words
- **Per Book**: 40,000-60,000 words
- **Minimum Total**: 30,000 words

## 4. US Real-World Verification

### 4.1 Real Estate Agent (Book 1)
- **Reality**: Real estate agents' core work is showing houses, negotiation, and knowing neighborhoods
- **AI Tools**: Zillow Zestimate (limited accuracy, 5-10% error rate)
- **Core Value**: Knowing which school district is good, which neighborhood is appreciating

### 4.2 Insurance Agent (Book 2)
- **Reality**: Insurance agents face AI underwriting and claims processing challenges
- **Core Value**: Handling complex cases and maintaining client relationships
- **AI Limitation**: Standardized cases only, complex cases still need human judgment

### 4.3 B2B Sales Representative (Book 3)
- **Reality**: Sales reps use AI tools like Gong.io, Outreach, Salesforce Einstein
- **AI Capabilities**: Generate emails, analyze customer sentiment, predict close probability
- **Core Controversy**: Is AI-generated "personalized" email sincere or disguised?

### 4.4 Investment Advisor (Book 4)
- **Reality**: Robo-advisors (Betterment, Wealthfront) challenge traditional advisors
- **Core Value**: Understanding clients' life goals, not just investment advice
- **AI Limitation**: Cannot understand "why leave money to grandson"

### 4.5 Community Bookstore Owner (Book 5)
- **Reality**: Small retailers use AI tools (Square, Shopify AI features)
- **Core Value**: Community relationships and personal taste
- **AI Limitation**: Cannot understand "why this store is important to the community"

## 5. Legal Risk Mitigation

### 5.1 Trademark Risk
- **Solution**: Use fictional AI system names only
- **Fictional Names**: HomeMatch AI, ClaimSmart, SalesPilot, WealthGuide, StoreBrain

### 5.2 Professional Defamation Risk
- **Solution**: Protagonists are positive characters; emphasize systemic issues
- **Approach**: AI is a neutral tool; the problem is "how humans use it"

## 7. Success Criteria

### 7.1 Quality Metrics
- AI writing trace detection: 0 template phrases
- Sensory detail count: ≥5 per chapter, ≥3 types
- Emotional depth: All 3 layers present
- Word count: 3,000-6,000 words per chapter

### 7.2 Reader Engagement
- Philosophical questions resonate with Western readers
- Sensory details create immersive experience
- Open endings provoke continued reflection
