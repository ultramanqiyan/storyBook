## 7. Quality Assurance Process

### 7.1 Per-Chapter Quality Checklist

```
For each chapter, verify:

□ AI Writing Pattern Detection
  □ No "And somewhere..." endings
  □ No "Neither of them knew..." foreshadowing
  □ No "will never be the same" clichés
  □ No "just the beginning" false beginnings
  □ No Rule of Three structures
  □ No abstract emotion statements
  □ No template dialogue
  □ No repetitive sentence structures
  □ No overused conjunctions
  □ No generic descriptions

□ Sensory Detail Verification
  □ Minimum 5 sensory details
  □ Minimum 3 different types
  □ Visual details: ~40%
  □ Other senses: ~60%
  □ Each detail serves a function

□ Emotional Depth Check
  □ Layer 1 (Surface): Present and specific
  □ Layer 2 (Internal): Present and character-specific
  □ Layer 3 (Deep): Present and thematically connected

□ Character Voice Consistency
  □ Speech pattern matches character spec
  □ Vocabulary appropriate to character
  □ Inner monologue reflects character's unique perspective
  □ No voice confusion between characters

□ Narrative Quality
  □ POV consistent (third-person limited)
  □ Tense consistent (past)
  □ Scene transitions smooth
  □ Chapter opening hooks
  □ Chapter ending creates momentum

□ Thematic Integration
  □ Theme present but not didactic
  □ Philosophical questions emerge naturally
  □ No preaching or easy answers
  □ Reader left with questions, not conclusions

□ Legal Safety
  □ No real company names
  □ No real person references
  □ No copyrighted material
  □ No sensitive topic violations
```

### 7.2 Per-Book Quality Checklist

```
For each book, verify:

□ All 10 chapters pass individual checks
□ Emotional arc is coherent across chapters
□ Character development is believable
□ Theme development is consistent
□ No plot holes or inconsistencies
□ Chapter transitions are smooth
□ Opening chapter hooks effectively
□ Ending chapter satisfies while leaving questions
□ Book can stand alone
□ Book connects to series themes
□ All legal safety measures verified
```

### 7.3 Series-Wide Quality Checklist

```
For the complete series, verify:

□ All 5 books pass individual checks
□ Series emotional arc is coherent
□ Each book's theme connects to series themes
□ No contradictions between books
□ Each book stands alone
□ Series feels unified
□ Character diversity is maintained
□ Philosophical progression makes sense
□ All legal safety measures verified
```
