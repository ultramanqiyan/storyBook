## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.1 Position in Book
- **Overall Location**: Falling Action (80-90% of book)
- **Narrative Function**: Elena passes on the knowledge to her daughter Maya

**Primary Objective**: Elena teaches her daughter Maya one Welsh word - "Hiraeth" - and begins to pass on what she's saved.

**Secondary Objectives**:
