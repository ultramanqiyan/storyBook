## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.1 Position in Book
- **Overall Location**: Rising Action (40-50% of book)
- **Narrative Function**: Elena witnesses mass deletion of historical texts, including her own work
- **Relationship to Previous Chapter**: She's joined the resistance; now she sees the full scale
- **Relationship to Next Chapter**: Her decision to act leads to cognitive colonization

### 1.2 Chapter Purpose
**Primary Objective**: Elena witnesses a mass deletion event and sees her own early papers being "translated" into Optima, cementing her determination to resist.

**Secondary Objectives**:
- Show the scale of the deletion project
- Make the threat personal (her own work)
- Deepen Elena's horror and rage
- Push her toward active resistance

### 1.3 Key Questions Answered/Raised
- **Answered**: How extensive is the deletion? What happens to historical texts?
- **Raised**: Can anything be saved? What will Elena do?

#### Beat 1: Tense Return (Position: 0-20%)
- **Trigger**: Elena returns to LEXICON after meeting Gareth

#### Beat 2: Scale Revealed (Position: 20-40%)
- **Trigger**: Elena accesses the deletion queue and sees the scale

- **Deep Motivation**: Horror, disbelief, growing rage

#### Beat 3: Personal Horror (Position: 40-60%)

- **Deep Motivation**: Rage, helplessness, determination

#### Beat 5: Decision (Position: 80-100%)

- **Sensory Anchor**: Her hands, no longer trembling, steady with purpose
