## 5. Thematic Depth

### 5.1 Core Themes
1. **Willpower Degeneration**: When AI provides direction, what happens to drive?
2. **The Dopamine Drift**: The seduction of instant AI feedback
3. **Free Will in AI Era**: Can choice exist when AI decides?

### 5.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| What is willpower? | Marcus's journey from self-driven to AI-directed |
