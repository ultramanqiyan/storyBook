## 4. Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Assignment (400 words)
**Location**: Jennifer's office
**Time**: Morning

**Key Events**:
- Jennifer assigns Marcus a long-term strategic project
- "This requires sustained focus over three months"
- "I need you to drive this yourself"
- Marcus's confidence: "I can do this"
- The challenge: no AI direction for strategy

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Jennifer's office, project brief
- Auditory: Jennifer's voice
- Internal: Confidence, underlying doubt

**Character Voices**:
- Jennifer: "Marcus, I need you to lead this strategic initiative. It's a three-month project requiring sustained focus and self-direction."
- Marcus: "I can handle that."
- Jennifer: "This isn't about completing tasks. It's about driving a vision. Can you do that?"
- Marcus: "Yes. I'll drive it."
- Marcus (internal): "But can I? Can I sustain effort without FocusZone telling me what to do?"

### Scene 2: The Attempt (700 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: Following weeks

**Key Events**:
- Marcus tries to drive the project himself
- He starts strong but loses momentum
- Without AI direction, he can't sustain effort
- The drift: he forgets about the project
- FocusZone doesn't prioritize it (it's strategic, not task-based)
- The failure: weeks pass with no progress
- The realization: he can't sustain effort without AI

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Project brief, calendar, lack of progress
- Auditory: Silence, the absence of direction
- Internal: Frustration, the failure

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I need to work on the strategic project. I need to drive it."
- Marcus (internal): "But FocusZone isn't telling me to. It's not on my task list."
- Marcus (internal): "I'll get to it tomorrow. After my FocusZone tasks."
- Marcus (weeks later): "I haven't touched the project in weeks. What happened?"
- Marcus (internal): "I can't sustain effort without AI direction. I've lost that ability."

### Scene 3: The Failure (500 words)
**Location**: Jennifer's office
**Time**: Project check-in

**Key Events**:
- Jennifer asks for a project update
- Marcus has nothing to show
- "I got caught up in other priorities"
- Jennifer's disappointment
- "This was supposed to be YOUR priority"
- The exposure: Marcus can't drive himself

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Jennifer's face, the empty project
- Auditory: Jennifer's voice, Marcus's excuses
- Internal: Shame, the exposure

**Character Voices**:
- Jennifer: "Marcus, where are we on the strategic initiative? It's been six weeks."
- Marcus: "I... haven't made as much progress as I'd hoped."
- Jennifer: "How much progress?"
- Marcus: "I got caught up in other priorities. The day-to-day tasks..."
- Jennifer: "This WAS your priority. I asked you to drive this yourself."
- Marcus: "I know. I'm sorry. I'll do better."
- Jennifer: "I'm not sure you can, Marcus. I'm not sure you can drive anything yourself anymore."

### Scene 4: The Retreat (400 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: After the meeting

**Key Events**:
- Marcus retreats to FocusZone
- He adds the project to his task list
- Now AI will direct him
- The relief: he doesn't have to drive himself
- The shame: he can't drive himself

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: FocusZone interface, the project added
- Auditory: Keyboard sounds
- Internal: Relief, shame

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I'll add the project to FocusZone. Then it will tell me what to do."
- FocusZone: "New long-term project added. I'll break this into tasks for you."
- Marcus (internal): "There. Now I don't have to drive. I just follow."
- Marcus (internal): "But that's the problem. I can't drive. I can only follow."

### Scene 5: The Sarah Confrontation (500 words)
**Location**: Home, living room
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Marcus tells Sarah about the failure
- Her response: "This is what I was worried about"
- "You've lost the ability to sustain effort"
- Marcus's defense: "I'll use AI to help"
- Sarah's question: "What happens when AI can't help?"
- The question Marcus can't answer

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Living room, Sarah's face
- Auditory: Sarah's voice
- Internal: Shame, the question

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus: "I failed on the strategic project. I couldn't sustain the effort."
- Sarah: "This is what I was worried about. You've lost the ability to drive yourself."
- Marcus: "I'll use FocusZone to help. I'll break it into tasks."
- Sarah: "But that's not driving, Marcus. That's following."
- Marcus: "It's the same thing. I'll get it done."
- Sarah: "What happens when AI can't help? What happens when you need to sustain effort on your own?"
- Marcus: "I don't know. I don't know."
