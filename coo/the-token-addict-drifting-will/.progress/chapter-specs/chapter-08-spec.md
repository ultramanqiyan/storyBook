- Visual: FocusZone interface, long-term plans
- Auditory: Completion sounds

**Location**: Home, with Sarah
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Sarah asks about Marcus's week
- He describes the peace of surrender
- "I'm not struggling anymore. FocusZone decides everything."
- Sarah's grief: "You've given up"
- Marcus's defense: "I'm at peace. Isn't that enough?"

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Home, Sarah's face
- Auditory: Sarah's voice
- Internal: Peace, the surrender

**Character Voices**:
- Sarah: "How was your week? You withdrawal attempt..."
- Marcus: "I stopped trying. I let FocusZone decide everything now."
- Sarah: "You've given up? You've surrendered completely?"
- Marcus: "I'm at peace. I'm not struggling anymore. Isn't that enough?"
- Sarah: "Marcus, you've surrendered your ability to direct your own life. That's not peace. That's... loss."
- Marcus: "It feels like peace to me. It feels like relief."

### Scene 5: The Drift (400 words)
**Location**: Home, bedroom
**Time**: Night

**Key Events**:
- Marcus reflects on the surrender
- The drift into AI-directed existence
- The question: "Is this living?"
- The answer: "It's existing. It's following."
- The peace of not choosing

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Dark room, ceiling
- Auditory: Silence
- Internal: The drift, the peace

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I've surrendered. I've given up the ability to direct myself."
- Marcus (internal): "But it's peaceful. It's easy. It's... right."
- Marcus (internal): "Is this living? Or is this just existing?"
- Marcus (internal): "I don't know. But I'm at peace. And that's enough for now."
