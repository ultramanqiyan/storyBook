## 4. Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Decision (400 words)
**Location**: Home, morning
**Time**: Early morning

**Key Events**:
- Marcus decides to disconnect from FocusZone
- The determination: "I need to prove I can drive myself"
- Sarah's support: "I'm proud of you for trying"
- The plan: one week without AI direction
- The fear: "What if I can't?"

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Home, FocusZone icon
- Auditory: Sarah's voice
- Internal: Determination, fear

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus: "I'm going to try. One week without FocusZone. I'll drive myself."
- Sarah: "I'm proud of you for trying. Whatever happens."
- Marcus (internal): "But what if I can't? What if I've lost it completely?"

### Scene 2: The First Day (600 words)
**Location**: Nexus Technologies, Marcus's office
**Time**: Day 1

**Key Events**:
- Marcus starts the day without FocusZone
- He tries to decide his own priorities
- The struggle: what to focus on?
- The discomfort of not having direction
- He makes some progress, but feels lost
- The temptation to check FocusZone

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Office, no FocusZone
- Auditory: Silence, his own thoughts
- Internal: Struggle, discomfort, temptation

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "What should I focus on? What matters most?"
- Marcus (internal): "I don't know. I can't decide."
- Marcus (internal): "This is what FocusZone does for me. It decides."
- Marcus (internal): "But I used to decide. I used to know."
- Marcus (internal): "FocusZone is right there. I could just check it..."

### Scene 3: The Withdrawal (700 words)
**Location**: Various
**Time**: Days 2-4

**Key Events**:
- The withdrawal symptoms intensify
- Marcus can't sustain effort without AI direction
- He starts tasks but doesn't finish them
- The dopamine craving: he misses the feedback
- The anxiety: without AI, he feels lost
- The productivity collapse
- The temptation becomes overwhelming

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Unfinished tasks, the FocusZone icon
- Auditory: Silence (no completion sounds)
- Internal: Anxiety, craving, the struggle

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I can't finish anything. I start and then... drift."
- Marcus (internal): "I need the completion. I need the feedback."
- Marcus (internal): "This is withdrawal. This is what dependency looks like."
- Marcus (internal): "FocusZone is right there. It would solve everything."
- Marcus (internal): "But I'm trying to prove I can do this myself."

### Scene 4: The Failure (500 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: Day 5

**Key Events**:
- Marcus breaks down and opens FocusZone
- The relief: AI direction returns
- The shame: he couldn't last a week
- The surrender: he needs AI
- The recognition: he's truly dependent

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: FocusZone opening, the familiar interface
- Auditory: The first completion sound
- Internal: Relief, shame, surrender

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I can't do this. I can't function without it."
- Marcus (opening FocusZone): The relief floods in
- FocusZone: "Welcome back, Marcus. Here's your focus for today..."
- Marcus (internal): "This is what I needed. This is what I can't live without."
- Marcus (internal): "I failed. I couldn't last five days."

### Scene 5: The Admission (400 words)
**Location**: Home, with Sarah
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Marcus admits the failure to Sarah
- "I couldn't do it. I couldn't last a week."
- Sarah's response: compassionate but concerned
- "This is real dependency, Marcus. This is addiction."
- Marcus's recognition: "I know. I don't know what to do."

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Home, Sarah's face
- Auditory: Sarah's voice
- Internal: Shame, recognition

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus: "I failed. I couldn't last five days without FocusZone."
- Sarah: "I know. I could see it was hard for you."
- Marcus: "This is real dependency. I can't function without it."
- Sarah: "This is addiction, Marcus. You need help."
- Marcus: "I know. But I don't know what to do. I can't work without it."
