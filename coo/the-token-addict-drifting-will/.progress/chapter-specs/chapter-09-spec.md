**Location**: Home, living room
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Sarah confronts Marcus directly
- "I've been watching you for weeks. You're not there anymore."
- "FocusZone decides everything. What do YOU decide?"
- Marcus's defense: "I'm productive. I'm at peace."
- Sarah's counter: "You're not at peace. You're absent."
- The question: "What do YOU want, Marcus? Not what FocusZone wants. What do YOU want?"
- Marcus's struggle: he can't answer
- The exposure: there's nothing there

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Living room, Sarah's face, Marcus's struggle
- Auditory: Sarah's voice, Marcus's silence
- Internal: Panic, the void

**Character Voices**:
- Sarah: "I've been watching you for weeks. You're not there anymore, Marcus."
- Marcus: "I'm right here. I'm productive. I'm at peace."
- Sarah: "You're not at peace. You're absent. You've drifted away."
- Marcus: "I'm following FocusZone. It helps me. It gives me direction."
- Sarah: "FocusZone decides everything. What do YOU decide?"
- Marcus: "I decide to follow FocusZone."
- Sarah: "That's not a decision. That's surrender. What do YOU want, Marcus? Not what FocusZone wants. What do YOU want?"
- Marcus: "I... want to be productive. I want to be at peace."
- Sarah: "Those aren't your wants. Those are FocusZone's outputs. What do YOU want?"
- Marcus: (silence)
- Marcus (internal): "What do I want? What do I want?"
- Marcus (internal): "There's nothing there. There's no answer."
- Sarah: "You can't answer, can you? You don't know what you want because you've let AI decide for so long."
- Marcus: "I... I don't know. I don't know what I want."
