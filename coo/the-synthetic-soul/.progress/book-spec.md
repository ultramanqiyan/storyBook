# Book Specification: The Synthetic Soul

## Basic Information
- Book Title: The Synthetic Soul
- Genre: Philosophical Science Fiction
- Target Audience: All ages
- Theme: Nature of consciousness and soul

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Dr. Marcus Webb's POV)
- Language Style: Contemporary, accessible prose with philosophical elements
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: Consciousness exploration, soul synthesis

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Wonder, discovery, responsibility
- Emotional Arc: Discovery → Wonder → Responsibility → Wisdom
- Emotional Pacing: Gradual build, accelerating toward resolution

## Character Voices

### Dr. Marcus Webb (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Scientific, philosophical, responsible
- Word Choice: Scientific terms mixed with philosophy
- Personality Traits: Scientific, philosophical, responsible
- Internal Monologue Style: Analytical, wondering
- Unique Expression: Uses scientific metaphors for soul

### The Synthetic Soul
- Speech Characteristics: Developing, questioning, growing
- Word Choice: Questions, observations, growing awareness
- Language Evolution: From program to conscious being
- Unique Expression: Questions existence and purpose

### The Antagonist
- Speech Characteristics: Corporate, dismissive, calculating
- Word Choice: Profit, product, asset
- Personality Traits: Corporate, dismissive, calculating
- Unique Expression: Views synthetic soul as product

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Nature of Consciousness
- Philosophical Exploration: What makes something alive? What is a soul?
- Real-world Metaphor: AI consciousness and rights

## World Building
- Setting: Near-future research facility
- Technology: Consciousness synthesis, neural networks
- Social Structure: Scientists, corporations, ethicists
- The Question: Can a synthetic being have a soul?

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: What other synthetic souls exist? What are their rights?
- New Suspense: Marcus's continuing journey
- Character New Beginning: Marcus as guardian of synthetic beings
- Reader Imagination: What would you do with a synthetic soul?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
