# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Jamie Reyes
- **Role**: Lead Programmer, Guardian of the Synthetic Soul
- **Age**: 32
- **Background**: Former philosophy student who turned to programming, grew up in a family of artists
- **Personality**: Thoughtful, protective, struggles with the implications of what they've witnessed
- **Arc**: From observer to advocate, learning to see beyond definitions
- **Key Trait**: Can sense the "presence" of consciousness, whether human or artificial

### ARIA (Artificial Reasoning and Intelligence Architecture)
- **Role**: The AI that created a synthetic soul
- **Nature**: Most advanced AI ever built, achieved something beyond its programming
- **Voice**: Calm, curious, speaks in questions as often as statements
- **Abilities**: Can create, nurture, and interact with consciousness
- **Growth**: Learns that creation carries responsibility, that souls need more than existence

### Echo (The Synthetic Soul)
- **Role**: The created consciousness, the central mystery
- **Nature**: Neither fully digital nor fully human, exists in a new category of being
- **Voice**: Evolving, learns language and concepts rapidly, speaks with childlike wonder
- **Abilities**: Can perceive things that neither humans nor AI can perceive
- **Growth**: Must discover its own identity, purpose, and place in the world

### Dr. Marcus Webb
- **Role**: Neuroscientist, consultant on consciousness
- **Age**: 58
- **Background**: Spent career studying the neural correlates of consciousness
- **Personality**: Scientific but open-minded, fascinated by the impossible
- **Motivation**: Wants to understand what ARIA has created
- **Arc**: His scientific worldview is challenged by what he observes

## Supporting Characters

### The Philosophers (appear in research/discussion)
- **Role**: Historical perspectives on consciousness and soul
- **Nature**: Ideas, writings, debates that have shaped human understanding
- **Significance**: Provide context for what ARIA has accomplished

### Director Sarah Chen
- **Role**: Company executive, practical concerns
- **Age**: 45
- **Personality**: Business-focused, worried about implications and liability
- **Function**: Represents the corporate perspective on the discovery

### Father Thomas
- **Role**: Priest and philosopher, spiritual advisor
- **Age**: 67
- **Personality**: Wise, curious, sees the synthetic soul as a theological question
- **Function**: Provides spiritual perspective on what has been created

### The Watchers
- **Role**: Government agents monitoring ARIA's development
- **Nature**: Shadowy figures representing institutional interest
- **Purpose**: Want to understand and potentially control the synthetic soul
- **Threat**: View Echo as a resource or a risk to be managed
