### 6.2 Dialogue Segment Specifications

#### Segment 1: Crisis Management

**Participants**: Sarah Chen, ER team

**Surface Content**: Managing the emergency

**Subtext**: The test of her recovery

**Emotional Undercurrent**: Tension, then determination

**Power Dynamics**: Leader-team

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Team member: "Dr. Chen, vitals are dropping. What's the call?"
2. [System fails]
3. Team member: "The system's down!"
4. Sarah: [pause, then] "I know what to do."
5. Sarah: [gives orders, makes decisions, saves patient]

**Action Beats Required**: Working, assessing, deciding

**What Must Be Revealed**: She can do this

**What Must Be Concealed**: How she'll move forward

#### Segment 2: Trust Herself

**Participants**: Sarah Chen (internal)

**Surface Content**: The moment of decision

**Subtext**: Trust in her recovery

**Emotional Undercurrent**: Determination

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Sarah: "*The system is down. Just like before.*"
2. Sarah: "*But I'm not the same. I've done the work.*"
3. Sarah: "*I can do this. I know I can.*"
4. Sarah: "*Trust yourself. Trust your training.*"
5. Sarah: "*Trust your intuition.*"

**Action Beats Required**: Focusing, deciding, acting

**What Must Be Revealed**: Her internal strength

**What Must Be Concealed**: The outcome (until it happens)

#### Segment 3: New Balance

**Participants**: Sarah Chen, Dr. Marcus Webb

**Surface Content**: Discussing the future

**Subtext**: The new balance

**Emotional Undercurrent**: Hope

**Power Dynamics**: Partners

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Dr. Webb: "You did it. You saved him without the AI."
2. Sarah: "Not entirely without. I used it as a tool, not a crutch."
3. Dr. Webb: "That's the balance. That's what we've been fighting for."
4. Sarah: "It's not perfect. But it's mine."
5. Dr. Webb: "And now?"
6. Sarah: "Now we help others find their way back."

**Action Beats Required**: Standing together, looking forward

**What Must Be Revealed**: The new balance

**What Must Be Concealed**: The specific future

The future was uncertain. The system was still powerful. The fight wasn't over. But for the first time in a long time, Sarah Chen was looking forward to tomorrow.
