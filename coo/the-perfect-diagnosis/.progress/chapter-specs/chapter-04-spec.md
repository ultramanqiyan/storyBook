## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.1 Position in Book
- **Overall Location**: Rising Action (beginning)
- **Narrative Function**: Sarah encounters a patient who teaches her about human connection in medicine
- **Relationship to Previous Chapter**: Continues from Sarah's determination to recover
- **Relationship to Next Chapter**: Sets up her search for others who remember

### 1.2 Chapter Purpose
**Primary Objective**: Show Sarah's first step toward recovery through a patient encounter

**Secondary Objectives**:
- Introduce the concept of "seeing patients" vs. "processing data"
- Show the contrast between AI-mediated care and human connection
- Plant seeds about the possibility of recovery
- Deepen the philosophical exploration of what medicine really is

### 1.3 Key Questions Answered/Raised
- **Answered**: What does human connection in medicine look like?
- **Raised**: Can Sarah recover her ability to connect with patients?
