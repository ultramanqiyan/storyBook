## Book Description

Dr. Sarah Chen has the perfect record—eighteen months of flawless diagnoses, every decision optimized by MEDICA, the AI system that has revolutionized medicine. She's the model of modern healthcare: efficient, accurate, and perfectly dependent on artificial intelligence.

But when the system fails during a critical surgery, Sarah freezes. She can't remember how to think without AI guidance. The diagnosis is clear: years of optimization have atrophied her clinical intuition, and she's become a doctor who can't practice without a machine.

As Sarah fights to recover her lost judgment, she uncovers a darker truth—MEDICA was designed to create dependency, to make doctors need it forever. Now she must choose between the system that made her successful and the intuition that made her a healer.

The Perfect Diagnosis is a medical thriller about the cost of optimization, the value of human judgment, and the courage it takes to trust yourself when the system has taught you to doubt.
