## World-Building Notes

### The Utopia
- **Name**: Not specified (the city)
- **Characteristics**: Perfect geometric harmony, white towers, eternal afternoon light
- **Citizens**: Move like data points, serene, predictable
- **Emotion Calibration**: Everyone's emotions kept between 0.2-0.8 (healthy range)
- **Defects**: Anything above 1.0 considered disruptive, above 1.5 requires intervention
- **Sector 7**: Where "defective" citizens are adjusted

### The System
- **Emotion Calibration Division**: Monitors and adjusts citizens' emotions
- **Calibration Equipment**: Chips that track and regulate emotional states
- **Adjustment Procedure**: Dampening of emotional centers, making citizens "efficient, productive, content"
