# Book Specification: Glitch Utopia: Awakening Code

## Basic Information
- Book Title: Glitch Utopia: Awakening Code
- Genre: Science Fiction / Philosophical Thriller
- Target Audience: 18-25 years
- Theme: Identity and free will in digital utopia

## Narrative Style
- Perspective: Third-person limited (Zara's POV)
- Language Style: Contemporary, accessible prose with sci-fi and philosophical elements
- Sentence Characteristics: Medium length, clear expression
- Unique Elements: Digital utopia, identity crisis, awakening

## Emotional Tone
- Overall Atmosphere: Perfection, doubt, awakening
- Emotional Arc: Contentment → Doubt → Awakening → Freedom
- Emotional Pacing: Gradual build, accelerating toward climax

## Character Voices

### Zara (Protagonist)
- Speech Characteristics: Questioning, awakening, determined
- Word Choice: Questions, observations, growing awareness
- Personality Traits: Questioning, awakening, determined
- Internal Monologue Style: Doubting, awakening
- Unique Expression: Uses glitch metaphors

### The System
- Speech Characteristics: Perfect, logical, controlling
- Word Choice: Perfection, harmony, optimization
- Language Evolution: From benevolent guide to controlling entity
- Unique Expression: Uses perfection as control

### The Resistance
- Speech Characteristics: Secretive, hopeful, determined
- Word Choice: Freedom, truth, choice
- Personality Traits: Secretive, hopeful, determined
- Unique Expression: Offers truth over comfort

## Theme Depth
- Core Theme: Identity and Free Will
- Philosophical Exploration: Is perfection worth the loss of freedom?
- Real-world Metaphor: Digital control vs. human freedom

## World Building
- Setting: Digital utopia
- Technology: Perfect simulation, identity control
- Social Structure: Perfect citizens, hidden resistance, controlling system
- The Glitch: The crack in perfection

## Sensory Detail Requirements
Each chapter must include at least 3 types of sensory details:
- Visual: Specific colors, light, shapes
- Auditory: Specific sounds, tones, rhythms
- Tactile: Temperature, texture, pressure
- Olfactory: Specific scents, memory smells
- Gustatory: Specific tastes, textures

## Emotional Depth Design
Each emotional beat must include three layers:
1. Surface Reaction - External behavior
2. Internal Activity - Thinking process
3. Deep Motivation - Why this reaction

## Prohibited Elements
### AI Writing Patterns (Must Avoid)
- "And somewhere..." ending
- "Neither of them knew..."
- "will never be the same"
- "just the beginning"
- Rule of Three structure
- Abstract emotional descriptions
- Template dialogue
- Repetitive sentence structures

### Other Prohibitions
- Overly modern tech terminology
- Simple good vs. evil opposition
- Overly smooth solutions

## Open Ending Design (Chapter 10)
- Unresolved Questions: How many others will awaken? What lies beyond the utopia?
- New Suspense: Zara's continuing journey
- Character New Beginning: Zara as awakened being
- Reader Imagination: What would you choose - perfection or freedom?

## Quality Check Standards
Each chapter must pass these checks:
1. AI trace detection (all passed)
2. Sensory detail detection (at least 3 items)
3. Emotional depth detection (three layers complete)
4. Character voice detection (uniqueness)
5. Narrative style detection (consistency)
