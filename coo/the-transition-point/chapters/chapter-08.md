# Chapter 8: The Transition

The transition began.

Consciousness as a whole started to shift. Individual awareness began to merge while somehow remaining individual. The phase transition that had been approaching for years finally arrived.

---

Those who had prepared found the transition natural. They'd already experienced dissolution of boundaries in their own ways—zero causal weight, the pattern, the simulation, the iteration, the three states, the threshold, the cosmic frequency, the play state.

---

Those who hadn't prepared found the transition terrifying. But the guides were ready—Marcus, Sarah, Amara, others who had learned to help others through their thresholds.
