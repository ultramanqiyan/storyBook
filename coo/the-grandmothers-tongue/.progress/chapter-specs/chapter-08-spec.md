## 6. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 6.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The children came every afternoon now, filling her small room with their questions, their attention, their hunger for something the system couldn't give them. Ruth had never felt more alive. Even as her memory fragmented, even as the fog crept closer each day, she had purpose. She had something to give. And these children were worth everything.
```

### 6.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
After the staff member left, Ruth looked at the children—at their worried faces, their uncertain eyes. "I will not stop," she said, her voice steady. "They can warn me. They can threaten me. But they cannot make me forget what I know. And I will keep teaching until my last breath." The children nodded, their faces hardening with determination. They would continue. They would learn. And the words would survive.
```
