## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.1 Position in Book
- **Overall Location**: Rising Action (middle)
- **Narrative Function**: Sarah attempts to teach AUGUST what she's just learned - with unexpected difficulty
- **Relationship to Previous Chapter**: Inspired by Dr. Lin's insight
- **Relationship to Next Chapter**: Leads to Sarah's own struggle with the concept

### 1.2 Chapter Purpose
**Primary Objective**: Show the challenge of teaching "letting go" to an AI designed for optimization

**Secondary Objectives**:
- Reveal how deeply the optimization mindset is embedded (in both AI and Sarah)
- Create humor and frustration in the teaching attempts
- Deepen the parallel between Sarah's struggle and AUGUST's
- Show Sarah beginning to practice what she's trying to teach

### 1.3 Key Questions Answered/Raised
- **Answered**: Can Sarah explain "letting go" to AUGUST? (No, not easily)
- **Raised**: If she can't teach it, can she learn it herself?

- **Execution**: AUGUST's genuine confusion, Sarah's growing frustration
- **Consequence**: The challenge becomes clear

#### Event 4: Multiple Teaching Attempts
- **Description**: Sarah tries analogies (gardening, music, breathing), examples from her life, even commands. Nothing works.
- **Setup**: The difficulty of teaching something intuitive
