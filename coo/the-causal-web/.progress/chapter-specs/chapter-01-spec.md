### 3.2 Event Descriptions

#### Event 1: Causal Network Presentation
- **Description**: A formal presentation at the Emergence Institute showcasing the Causal Network AI's capabilities
- **Setup**: Marcus attends as part of his sociological research
- **Execution**: Holographic displays show intricate webs of causality - how one action ripples through society
- **Consequence**: Marcus becomes fascinated by the concept of quantifiable causality
- **Connection to Theme**: Introduces the central question: if everything is connected, what does it mean to be disconnected?

#### Event 2: Causal Weight Explanation
- **Description**: The presenter explains how individuals have "causal weight" - a measure of their impact on the causal network
- **Setup**: Part of the presentation, but Marcus tunes in more closely
- **Execution**: Examples shown - high-weight individuals (leaders, artists) vs. low-weight (isolated individuals)
- **Consequence**: Marcus begins to wonder about his own weight
- **Connection to Theme**: Establishes the metric by which Marcus will judge his own existence

#### Event 3: Personal Analysis Request
- **Description**: Marcus approaches Dr. Morrison after the presentation to request his own causal analysis
- **Setup**: Dr. Morrison is an old colleague who respects Marcus's work
- **Execution**: Marcus frames it as "professional curiosity" but his hands tremble slightly
- **Consequence**: Dr. Morrison agrees, sensing there's more to the request
- **Connection to Theme**: The gap between professional detachment and personal need

#### Event 4: Data Processing
- **Description**: Marcus sits in the analysis chamber while the system processes his life data
- **Setup**: He's connected to the system through neural interface
- **Execution**: Memories flash - childhood, education, career, relationships - all being quantified
- **Consequence**: A brief flicker of the childhood near-death experience, quickly suppressed
- **Connection to Theme**: The attempt to quantify a life that may not be quantifiable

#### Event 5: Anomaly Revealed
- **Description**: The system shows Marcus's causal weight: zero
- **Setup**: The technician is confused, checks the system, confirms it's working
- **Execution**: Marcus sees the number, the web visualization shows no threads emanating from him
- **Consequence**: Marcus's world shifts - he is an anomaly, a zero-weight individual
- **Connection to Theme**: The central paradox - how can someone exist without causal impact?

### 6.2 Dialogue Segment Specifications

#### Segment 1: Presentation Q&A

**Participants**: Marcus Chen, Dr. Elena Vasquez (presenter)

**Surface Content**: Questions about the Causal Network's methodology

**Subtext**: Marcus is fishing for information about anomalies

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Marcus: Curiosity masking personal investment
- Elena: Professional enthusiasm, unaware of Marcus's hidden agenda

**Power Dynamics**: Elena has knowledge; Marcus has questions

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Marcus asks about edge cases in the network
2. Elena mentions "anomaly cases" briefly
3. Marcus follows up, trying to seem academically interested
4. Elena deflects, saying anomalies are rare and not well understood
5. Marcus thanks her, mind racing

**Action Beats Required**: Marcus's pen pausing, his slight lean forward

**What Must Be Revealed**: Anomalies exist; they're not well understood

**What Must Be Concealed**: Marcus's personal connection to anomalies

**Forbidden Expressions**:
- [ ] "What do you mean?"
- [ ] "You know what I mean"
- [ ] Direct emotion statements
- [ ] Over-explanation

**Participants**: Marcus Chen, Dr. James Morrison

**Subtext**: Marcus needs to know if he matters, if his existence creates ripples

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Marcus: Desperation masked as professional interest

**Power Dynamics**: Morrison has access; Marcus has need

1. Morrison states the unusual nature of the request

**What Must Be Revealed**: Marcus wants the analysis; Morrison suspects deeper reasons

**Forbidden Expressions**:

- [ ] Perfect responses
