## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.1 Position in Book
- **Overall Location**: Climax
- **Narrative Function**: Discovery that Marcus's causality points "elsewhere" - beyond the network
- **Relationship to Previous Chapter**: Follows the system's failure to calculate Marcus
- **Relationship to Next Chapter**: Sets up scientific validation of the phenomenon

### 1.2 Chapter Purpose
**Primary Objective**: Reveal that Marcus's zero weight isn't absence but redirection - his causality points to the "elsewhere"

**Secondary Objectives**:
- Connect the childhood memory to the present discovery
- Show Priya's worldview being fundamentally challenged
- Introduce the concept of the "elsewhere" as a real phenomenon
- Deepen the philosophical implications

### 1.3 Key Questions Answered/Raised
- **Answered**: What does zero causal weight actually mean? Where does Marcus's causality go?
- **Raised**: What is the "elsewhere"? Can Marcus access it consciously?
