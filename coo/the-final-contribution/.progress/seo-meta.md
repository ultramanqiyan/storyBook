### Meta Title (60 chars max)
The Final Contribution | A Retirement in the Age of Automation

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
When Robert's 401(k) and Social Security collapse as AI displaces millions, he faces an impossible choice: take the last payout or witness the social contract's end.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Intergenerational Justice**: When one generation's security depends on another's inability to contribute
- **The Social Contract**: The promise of "work hard, retire securely" - what happens when work disappears?
- **The Meaning of Contribution**: Is contribution still meaningful when there's no one left to contribute to?
- **The Value of a Promise**: Is a promise still valid when conditions fundamentally change?
- **The Last Generation Problem**: What do we owe to those who come after us?
