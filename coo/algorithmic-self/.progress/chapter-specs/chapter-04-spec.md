## 5. Opening

The number appeared on the screen like a verdict. 78%. Probability of divorce within five years. Maggie stared at it, her mind refusing to process what she was seeing. "That's impossible," she said. "My marriage is fine."

---

## 6. Ending

Maggie left the lab with a new determination. The AI was wrong. It had to be. She knew her marriage better than any algorithm. She'd prove it. She'd prove that self-knowledge was still possible, that she wasn't a mystery to herself. She just needed more data.
