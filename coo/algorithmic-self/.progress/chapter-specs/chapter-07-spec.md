## 4. Key Scene

## 5. Opening

Tom was in the kitchen when Maggie came home, making dinner like he did every Tuesday. She stood in the doorway, watching him, seeing him clearly for the first time in months. "We need to talk," she said. He turned, his expression knowing. "I know."

## 6. Ending

They talked until midnight, the first real conversation in years. Not about divorce - not yet - but about what had happened to them. About the distance that had grown so slowly they hadn't noticed. About the lives they'd been living side by side instead of together. The AI had been right. But that wasn't the point anymore. The point was what they did next.
