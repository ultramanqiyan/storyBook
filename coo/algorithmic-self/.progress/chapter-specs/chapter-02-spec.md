## 5. Opening

The University of Chicago's AI Research Lab was nothing like a courtroom. No wood paneling, no leather chairs, no sense of history. Just white walls, screens, and the hum of servers. Dr. Chen led Maggie to a private terminal. "Everything you input stays here," he said. "The analysis is for your eyes only. Ready?"

## 6. Ending

The initial results appeared on the screen. Maggie scanned them, her lawyer's eye catching patterns immediately. Communication frequency with spouse: declining 12% annually. Shared activities: down 34% over five years. Emotional language in messages: decreasing. She frowned. "This is... interesting." Dr. Chen smiled. "That's just the surface. Do you want to see the prediction?"
