## 4. Key Scene

---

## 5. Opening

Maggie approached her marriage like a case to be won. She made a list of evidence that would disprove the AI's prediction: the anniversary trip they'd planned, the way Tom still made her coffee every morning, the fact that they'd never had a screaming fight. These were the facts. The AI was missing context.

---

## 6. Ending

By the end of the week, Maggie had a folder of evidence. But looking at it, she realized how thin it was. A few nice moments. A handful of gestures. Against the weight of the behavioral data - the declining communication, the separate lives, the slow drift - her counter-evidence felt like a desperate defense. Like she was losing a case she'd thought was won.
