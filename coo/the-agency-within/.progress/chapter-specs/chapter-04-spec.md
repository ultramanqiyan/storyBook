## 2. Emotional Arc

### 2.1 Emotional Journey
```
Opening: Attempting to implement agency
    ↓
Middle: Strong resistance, rationalization
    ↓
Closing: Acknowledgment of fear
```

### 2.2 Emotional Depth Design

**Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**:
- Riley tries to take initiative
- Fails repeatedly, falls back into passivity
- Rationalizes each failure

**Layer 2 - Internal Activity**:
- Anxiety about stepping up
- Fear of being seen as pushy
- Defensive justification of waiting

**Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**:
- Fear of failure if initiative taken
- Past experiences of being criticized
- Identity crisis around passivity
