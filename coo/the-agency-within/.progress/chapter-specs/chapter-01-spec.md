## 2. Emotional Arc

### 2.1 Emotional Journey
```
Opening: Riley's passive approach to work
    ↓
Middle: Comfort in waiting for direction
    ↓
Closing: First hint of dissatisfaction
```

### 2.2 Emotional Depth Design

**Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**:
- Riley waits for assignments and direction
- Follows instructions precisely
- Never volunteers or initiates

**Layer 2 - Internal Activity**:
- Fear of overstepping
- Belief that waiting is professional
- Subtle awareness of stagnation

**Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**:
- Fear of making mistakes
- Need for approval
- Deeper fear of responsibility

## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Key Conversations

**Manager**:
- "Riley, can you handle this task?"
- "Of course." (Riley's standard response)

**Colleague (proactive)**:
- "I've been thinking about how we could improve this process. What if we tried..."

**Riley (internal)**:
