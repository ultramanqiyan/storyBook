# Chapter 02 Specification: The Archive

> **Book**: The Translator's Betrayal
> **Chapter Number**: 2 of 10

---

## 1. Chapter Purpose
Show the "preservation" system and deepen Yuki's unease.

## 2. Key Events
1. Yuki visits the language archive
2. She sees languages being "preserved" (deleted)
3. She remembers her father's words about translation
4. The unease grows

## 3. Opening Paragraph
The archive was in the basement of LEXICON headquarters—a vast, climate-controlled space where the world's languages were being "preserved" for posterity. Yuki walked through the rows of servers, each one containing the words of a language that had been optimized out of existence.
