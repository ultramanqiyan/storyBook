### Meta Description (160 chars max)
When AI writes in David Kim's voice, clients prefer the AI version. A philosophical exploration of authentic expression and self-concept in the age of AI-generated communication.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Self-Concept Blurring**: When AI writes "in your voice," whose voice is it? Is self essentially a behavior pattern?
- **Authentic Expression**: What makes a voice "authentic"? Is it the flaws, hesitations, genuine moments?
- **The Ideal Self**: If AI is a better version of you, is it still you? What is the relationship between authentic self and effective self?
