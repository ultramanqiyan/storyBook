## Character Voice Consistency

David Kim's voice should:
- Start authentic: warm, humorous, genuine
- Become polished but hollow: smooth, formulaic
- End authentic again: imperfect, genuine, real

Key phrases to track:
- "Let me tell you a story" (authentic)
- "Here's the thing about..." (authentic)
- AI-generated phrases (polished but soulless)
