## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Dialogue Segments

| Segment | Speakers | Subtext | Emotional State | Power Dynamic | Forbidden Phrases |
|---------|----------|---------|-----------------|---------------|-------------------|

| 2 | Sarah, phone call with mom | Deflection, hiding | Guarded | Equal | Over-explanation |

### 6.2 Dialogue Segment Specifications

#### Segment 1: Internal Monologue

**Participants**: Sarah alone

**Surface Content**: Rationalizing the research

**Subtext**: Fear disguised as preparation

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Sarah: Desperate need for control, terror of making wrong choice

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. *I need to understand this fully before I decide.*
2. *This is responsible. This is what smart people do.*
3. *What if I make the wrong choice because I didn't research enough?*
4. *Just one more article. Then I'll decide.*
5. *Maybe tomorrow...*

**Action Beats Required**: Scrolling, clicking, note-taking

**What Must Be Revealed**: Her intelligence trapped in loops

**What Must Be Concealed**: The depth of her fear

**Forbidden Expressions**:
- [x] "I feel anxious"
- [x] "I'm scared of making the wrong choice"
- [x] Over-explanation

#### Segment 2: Phone Call with Mom

**Participants**: Sarah, her mother (via phone)

**Surface Content**: Casual catch-up

**Subtext**: Sarah hiding her struggle, mom sensing something

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Sarah: Shame at her paralysis, need to appear fine
- Mom: Concern, intuition that something is wrong

**Power Dynamics**: Parent-child dynamic, Sarah feels like she's failing

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Mom: "How are things? You sound tired."
2. Sarah: "Fine! Just busy with work. You know how it is."
3. Mom: "Are you sure? You don't sound like yourself."
4. Sarah: "I'm good, Mom. Really. How's Dad?"
5. (Deflection successful, but hollow)

**Action Beats Required**: Pacing, rubbing temples, forcing lightness into voice

**What Must Be Revealed**: Sarah's isolation, her hiding

**What Must Be Concealed**: The full extent of her struggle

**Forbidden Expressions**:
- [x] "I'm struggling"
- [x] "I don't know what to do"
- [x] Over-sharing
