# The Silent River

An environmental thriller about one lawyer's fight for justice when AI technology threatens to destroy her community.

## Synopsis

When the river that has sustained River's Bend for generations runs dry, the town turns to environmental lawyer Elena Vasquez. What she discovers is a conspiracy that reaches from corporate boardrooms to the highest levels of government. NeuralCore's AI data centers need water—millions of gallons of it—and the law seems powerless to stop them.

As Elena digs deeper, she uncovers secret deals, ignored warnings, and a community sacrificed on the altar of progress. But River's Bend refuses to give up, and neither does Elena. Together, they take on one of the most powerful corporations in the world.

## Themes

- Environmental justice
- Corporate accountability
- The limits and possibilities of the law
- Community resilience
- Progress vs. preservation
