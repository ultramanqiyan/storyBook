- **POV Character(s)**: Ingrid Lindqvist

- **Overall Tone**: Gentle, reflective prose with Scandinavian warmth
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, sensory and emotional focus

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Death and Meaning**: Does death give life meaning?
2. **Immortality**: Is living forever desirable?
3. **The Dying Process**: What do we lose when death becomes optional?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| Does death give life meaning? | Ingrid's patients choose digital immortality |
