## 3. Opening
Ingrid visited Sarah in the digital environment. It looked like a comfortable living room - Sarah's living room, recreated from her memories. Sarah appeared on the screen, smiling, asking about Ingrid's day. It was exactly like talking to the real Sarah. But it wasn't the real Sarah. Or was it?

---

## 4. Ending
Ingrid left the visit with more questions than answers. Digital Sarah remembered everything, felt everything, seemed to be everything Sarah had been. But she existed only in servers, only in code. Was that living? Or was it something else - a kind of existence that defied all the categories Ingrid had known?
