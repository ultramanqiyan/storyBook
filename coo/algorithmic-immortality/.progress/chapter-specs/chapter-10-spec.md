## 4. Opening
Ingrid sat with her newest patient, a man choosing digital immortality. She listened to his reasons, supported his decision, and helped him prepare. It wasn't the choice she would make. But it was his choice. And that was what mattered.

---

## 5. Ending
The question of immortality would never be fully resolved. Ingrid knew that now. But she also knew something else: her work wasn't about death or immortality. It was about presence. About being there for people at the most important moments of their lives - whatever they chose those moments to be. The mystery remained. And somehow, that was okay.
