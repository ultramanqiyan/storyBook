## 3. Opening
Ingrid realized that digital immortality wasn't the enemy of meaning. It was just another choice - one that some people would make and others wouldn't. The meaning of life wasn't determined by its length, but by its shape. And that shape could be finite or infinite, biological or digital.

---

## 4. Ending
Ingrid returned to work with a new understanding. Her job wasn't to judge whether digital immortality was right or wrong. It was to help people make their own choices - and to accompany them through whatever came next. Death had changed. But her work remained the same: to be present, to care, to honor each person's story.
