## 3. Opening
Ingrid had been present for hundreds of deaths. But Sarah's upload felt different. There was no body to bury, no final breath. Just a procedure, a transfer, and then... what? A copy? A continuation? Something in between?

---

## 4. Ending
The procedure took eight hours. When it was over, Sarah's body was cremated. Her consciousness - or a copy of it - existed now in a digital environment. Her family would visit her there, talk to her through screens, share their lives with her digital presence. It wasn't death. But it wasn't life either. It was something new.
