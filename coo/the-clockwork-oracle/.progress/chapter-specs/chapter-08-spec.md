# Chapter 08 Specification: The Clockwork Oracle

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show impact of ethical work, deepen relationships
- **Emotional Arc**: Progress → Connection → Hope
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate the value of Chronos's legacy

## Core Events
1. Corwin's ethical machines begin helping people (progress)
2. Princess Elara supports Corwin's work (connection)
3. Kingdom begins to understand the value of ethics (hope)
4. Corwin feels Chronos's presence in his work (peace)

## Character States

### Corwin
- **Psychological State**: Hopeful, connected, at peace
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Continue ethical work, honor Chronos
- **Emotional Layers**:

  - Inner: Hope for the future
  - Deep: Connection to Chronos

### Princess Elara

- **Emotional Layers**:

  - Deep: Hope for the kingdom
