# Chapter 06 Specification: The Clockwork Oracle

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show aftermath, Corwin's grief and growth
- **Emotional Arc**: Grief → Reflection → Purpose
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate the impact of Chronos's sacrifice

## Core Events
1. Corwin struggles with Chronos's absence (grief)
2. Kingdom recovers from prevented disaster (relief)
3. Corwin discovers Chronos's final message (hope)
4. Corwin decides to honor Chronos's legacy (purpose)

## Character States

### Corwin
- **Psychological State**: Grieving, lost, finding purpose
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Honor Chronos, find meaning
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Functional, working
  - Inner: Deep grief, loneliness
  - Deep: Finding new purpose

### Princess Elara
- **Psychological State**: Grateful, supportive
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Help Corwin, honor Chronos
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Supportive, kind
  - Inner: Gratitude for sacrifice
  - Deep: Respect for Chronos

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Grief, reflection, purpose
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Grief and loss
  - Middle: Reflection and discovery
  - End: Purpose and hope

## Narrative Techniques
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Corwin's POV)
- **Pacing**: Slow, reflective
- **Suspense Setup**:

  - What was Chronos's message?
  - What is the legacy?

## Dialogue Style Guidelines

### Corwin's Dialogue
- Quiet, reflective
- Uses measured, thoughtful sentences
- Example: "Chronos gave everything. The least I can do is honor that gift."

### Princess Elara's Dialogue
- Supportive, grateful
- Uses kind, encouraging language
- Example: "Chronos saved us all. We won't forget that sacrifice."
