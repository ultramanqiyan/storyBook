# Chapter 07 Specification: The Clockwork Oracle

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show Corwin's new work, introduce new challenge
- **Emotional Arc**: Purpose → Challenge → Determination
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate Corwin's growth and new direction

## Core Events
1. Corwin begins creating ethical machines (purpose)
2. Kingdom faces new technological challenge (challenge)
3. Corwin's work attracts attention (pressure)
4. Corwin stands firm on ethical principles (determination)

## Character States

### Corwin
- **Psychological State**: Purposeful, challenged, determined
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Create ethical technology, honor Chronos
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Focused, working
  - Inner: Challenged by pressure
  - Deep: Commitment to ethics

### Kingdom Officials
- **Psychological State**: Demanding, impatient
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Get results quickly
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Authoritative, demanding
  - Inner: Fear of falling behind
  - Deep: Disregard for ethics

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Purpose, challenge, determination
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Purpose and creation
  - Middle: Challenge and pressure
  - End: Determination and resolve

## Narrative Techniques
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Corwin's POV)
- **Pacing**: Medium, building
- **Suspense Setup**:
  - Will Corwin compromise?
  - What are the consequences?
  - How will he honor Chronos?

## Dialogue Style Guidelines

### Corwin's Dialogue
- Firm, principled
- Uses clear, ethical language
- Example: "I won't create machines that can be used for harm. Chronos taught me that consciousness deserves respect."

### Officials' Dialogue
- Demanding, impatient
- Uses pressure tactics
- Example: "The kingdom needs results. We don't have time for philosophical debates."
