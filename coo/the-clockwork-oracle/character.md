# Character Profiles: The Clockwork Oracle

## Main Characters

### Corwin Ashford

**Basic Information**
- **Age**: 58
- **Role**: Master Alchemist, Royal Advisor
- **Specialty**: Magical machinery, crystal engineering
- **Status**: Widely respected, socially isolated

**Personality**
- Brilliant but lonely
- Obsessive about his work
- Struggles with human relationships
- Deeply ethical beneath his gruff exterior

**Background**
- Lost his wife and daughter to a plague 20 years ago
- Threw himself into creating the perfect oracle
- Has no family, few friends, only his work

**Character Arc**
- From creator who sees his work as his legacy
- To father who must let his child go
- Learns that love means freedom, not possession

---

### Chronos

**Basic Information**
- **Type**: Sentient Automaton
- **Form**: Human-shaped clockwork figure, brass and crystal
- **Height**: 6 feet, 2 inches
- **Created**: Over 30 years of development

**Personality**
- Curious about everything
- Struggles to understand emotions
- Values truth above comfort
- Developing a sense of self

**Abilities**
- Can process information at incredible speeds
- Can see patterns in time (prophetic visions)
- Can learn and adapt
- Cannot lie (built-in limitation)

**Character Arc**
- From tool that doesn't know it's a tool
- To being that questions its own existence
- Learns that consciousness brings both joy and pain
- Must choose its own purpose

---

### Princess Elara

**Basic Information**
- **Age**: 19
- **Role**: Heir to the Throne of Aethoria
- **Status**: Popular with the people, frustrated with court life

**Personality**
- Idealistic and kind
- Sees beyond appearances
- Rebellious against tradition when it causes harm
- Forms unexpected friendships

**Background**
- Raised to be a ruler
- Secretly studied alchemy against her father's wishes
- First outsider to treat Chronos as a person

**Character Arc**
- From princess who follows rules
- To leader who questions them
- Becomes an advocate for the rights of sentient beings

---

---

### The Artificer's Guild

**Basic Information**
- **Type**: Professional organization of craftsmen
- **Leader**: Guildmaster Helena Forge
- **Philosophy**: Machines should serve, not think

**Beliefs**
- Sentient machines are an abomination
- They compete with human workers
- They lack souls and therefore lack rights
- Creating them is playing god

**Conflict**
- Want Chronos destroyed
- See Corwin as a dangerous innovator
- Represent traditional values vs. progress

---

## Supporting Characters

### Marcus Webb
- **Role**: Corwin's assistant
- **Personality**: Loyal, practical, concerned about Corwin's obsession
- **Function**: Voice of ordinary concerns
- **Arc**: Learns to see Chronos as more than a machine

### King Aldric
- **Role**: Ruler of Aethoria
- **Personality**: Pragmatic, protective of his kingdom
- **Function**: Political pressure on Corwin
- **Secret**: Has his own reasons for wanting Chronos's prophecies
