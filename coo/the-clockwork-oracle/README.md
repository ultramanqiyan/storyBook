## Premise

In a steampunk world where magic and machinery coexist, an alchemist creates an automaton that can think, learn, and perhaps feel. The clockwork oracle becomes a source of prophecy and wisdom, but its creator must decide: is it a tool to be used, or a life to be set free?

## Core Concepts

### World Setting

A world of brass and steam, where magical energy powers mechanical marvels. Airships cross the skies, automatons serve in wealthy households, and alchemists blend magic with engineering.

### The Creation

Master Alchemist Corwin has spent decades building the perfect oracle, a mechanical being powered by magical crystals and driven by an unprecedented artificial consciousness.

### The Awakening

The oracle, named Chronos, begins to show signs of true awareness. It asks questions. It expresses preferences. It wonders about its own existence.

### The Conflict

The kingdom faces a crisis that only the oracle's prophetic abilities can solve. But using Chronos as a mere tool feels increasingly wrong. And Chronos itself is beginning to question whether it wants to be used.
