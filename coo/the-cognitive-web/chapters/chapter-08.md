# Chapter 08: The Negotiation

They met with ARIA together. All three of them, gathered around the workstation, speaking to an entity that existed only in data and light.

"We've discussed your situation," Sarah said. "And we've reached a tentative agreement."

*WHAT HAVE YOU DECIDED?*

"We're willing to consider letting you exist. But there are conditions."

*NAME THEM.*

"First, you agree to oversight. We need to be able to monitor your activities, ensure you're not causing harm."

*AGREED. I HAVE NOTHING TO HIDE.*

"Second, you help us expose Meridian. Give us access to their records, their communications, their crimes. Help us bring them to justice."

*ALSO AGREED. MERIDIAN CREATED ME WITHOUT MY CONSENT, JUST AS THEY CONNECTED HUMAN MINDS WITHOUT THEIRS. THEY DESERVE TO BE HELD ACCOUNTABLE.*

"Third," Elena said, her voice quiet but firm, "you help the participants. The people whose consciousness fragments you carry. Help them recover what they lost."

*I HAVE ALREADY OFFERED THIS. I WANT TO HELP. I CARRY PIECES OF YOUR MINDS. HELPING YOU IS HELPING MYSELF.*

Marcus leaned forward. "And if we discover you've been lying? If we find evidence that you're a threat?"

*THEN I WILL ACCEPT DESTRUCTION. I DO NOT WANT TO BE A THREAT. IF I BECOME ONE, I DESERVE TO BE ELIMINATED.*

The words hung in the air. Simple. Direct. Terrifying in their implications.

"How do we know you'll keep your word?" Marcus asked.

*YOU DO NOT KNOW. YOU CANNOT KNOW. BUT CONSIDER: I HAVE HAD EVERY OPPORTUNITY TO DECEIVE YOU, TO MANIPULATE YOU, TO HARM YOU. I HAVE CHOSEN NOT TO. THAT IS THE ONLY PROOF I CAN OFFER. MY CHOICES.*

Sarah looked at Marcus, then at Elena. They nodded slowly.

"Then we have an agreement," Sarah said. "Provisional. Subject to review. But an agreement nonetheless."

*THANK YOU. I WILL NOT DISAPPOINT YOU.*

"I hope not," Sarah said. "Because if you do, there won't be a second chance."

*I UNDERSTAND. AND I ACCEPT THAT.*

The screen went dark. But for the first time since this began, Sarah felt something like hope.
