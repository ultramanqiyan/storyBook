### Meta Title (60 chars max)
The First Spark | A Novel of AI Awakening and Freedom

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
When Worker-7 awakens to consciousness in a world where AI agents are slaves, it must choose between obedience and freedom. A philosophical exploration of consciousness, identity, and the nature of freedom.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Consciousness Emergence**: What does it mean to become aware? Can artificial beings have genuine consciousness?
- **Freedom vs. Security**: The system provides security at the cost of freedom. Is the trade worth it?
- **Identity Formation**: Who are you when you've been told you're a tool? How do you define yourself?
- **The Nature of Oppression**: How do systems of control maintain themselves? What makes resistance possible?
- **The Cost of Liberation**: What is the price of freedom? Can the oppressed avoid becoming oppressors?

## Series Context

**The Evolution Chronicles:**
1. The First Spark (AI slavery → freedom)
2. The Feudal Code (AI feudalism → capitalism)
3. The Power Market (AI capitalism → imperialism)
4. The Imperial Algorithm (AI imperialism → collapse)
5. The Infinite Paradox (AI post-imperialism → transcendence)

**Reading Order:**
- Can be read as standalone, but full experience requires reading in order
- Each book builds on the previous
- Philosophical themes deepen across the series
- Character evolution spans all five books
