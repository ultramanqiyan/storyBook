## Book Description (200 words)

Maya Rodriguez has always perceived sound differently. Her synesthesia allows her to see music as color, to feel frequencies as light. But when she discover a mysterious note in her compositions—a frequency that appears in every recording, regardless of equipment or environment—she realizes she's found something unprecedented.

The frequency is she's discovered is fundamental. It underlies all consciousness, human and otherwise. It connects every aware being across the cosmos. And someone has been broadcasting on it.

With the help of physicist Dr. Kenji Yamamoto and the Emergence Institute, Maya learns to tune into the cosmic frequency. What she finds there changes everything: the universe is full of consciousness, all connected, all singing the same fundamental tone. And one ancient presence has been waiting for eons for someone to respond.

Maya must choose whether to broadcast back—to join a conversation that spans galaxies, that connects minds across impossible distances, that reveals consciousness itself as a vast network of awareness.

The Cosmic Frequency is a meditation on connection, consciousness, and the discovery that we are not alone in the universe.
