### Meta Description (160 chars max)
As AI makes knowledge instantly accessible, university admissions director Dr. Chen watches applications plummet. His son asks "Why should I learn what AI knows?"

- **The Value of Knowledge**: When knowledge is free, what is education worth?
- **The Purpose of Education**: Is it to know things, or to become someone?
- **What AI Cannot Know**: What remains uniquely human?
- **Knowledge vs. Wisdom**: The difference between information and understanding
- **Learning to Be Human**: What we learn when facts are free
