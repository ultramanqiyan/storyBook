**Subtitle**: When Knowledge Became Free and Value Disappeared

**Title Analysis**:
- Main title "Degree Dust" evokes the dissolution of traditional educational value
- Subtitle reveals the paradox: knowledge is free, but degrees have become worthless
- Suitable keywords: education crisis, degree value, knowledge economy


**Optimization Notes**:

- "Why should I learn what AI knows?" states the core conflict
- Core keywords: education crisis, AI and learning, degree value
