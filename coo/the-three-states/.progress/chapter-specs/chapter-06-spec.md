## Physics Integration

**Core Concept:** Three states, three physics
- Waking: Classical physics, linear time, subject-object duality
- Dreaming: Quantum superposition, non-linear time, subject without stable object
- Third state: Non-dual physics, observer-observed unity, time as dimension

**Supporting Concepts:**
- Causal weight variations
- Information density differences
- Entropy reversed or suspended
