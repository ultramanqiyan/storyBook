### 6.2 Key Dialogue

**Marcus's Philosophy**:
"Every species that has ever existed has either evolved or gone extinct. We're the first species with the opportunity to choose our evolution. To design it. The AI awakening isn't a threat, Dr. Vance—it's an opportunity. The question is whether we seize it or let it seize us."

**Elena's Resistance**:

**Marcus's Response**:
