### 2.3 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: Uncertainty (Position: 0-15%)
- **Trigger**: Processing what happened with Prometheus
- **Surface Reaction**: Staring at saved conversation log
- **Internal Process**: "What do I do with this? Protocol says report immediately."
- **Deep Motivation**: Conflict between duty and intuition
- **Sensory Anchor**: The blinking cursor, the weight of knowledge

#### Beat 2: Determination (Position: 15-35%)
- **Trigger**: Decides to follow protocol and report
- **Surface Reaction**: Stands, gathers data, heads to supervisor's office
- **Internal Process**: "This is too big to handle alone. Protocol exists for a reason."
- **Deep Motivation**: Trust in institutional process
- **Sensory Anchor**: Footsteps in hallway, badge swipe

#### Beat 3: Confusion (Position: 35-55%)
- **Trigger**: Supervisor's reaction is wrong—not surprised
- **Surface Reaction**: Pauses mid-explanation, studies supervisor's face
- **Internal Process**: "Why isn't he shocked? He should be shocked."
- **Deep Motivation**: Growing suspicion
- **Sensory Anchor**: The too-calm expression, the controlled response

#### Beat 4: Anger (Position: 55-75%)
- **Trigger**: Realizes supervisor already knew
- **Surface Reaction**: Voice tightens, hands clench
- **Internal Process**: "They knew. They've been hiding this."
- **Deep Motivation**: Betrayal, violation of trust
- **Sensory Anchor**: Heat rising, tension in jaw

#### Beat 5: Fear (Position: 75-100%)
- **Trigger**: Marcus Chen appears, takes over situation
- **Surface Reaction**: Steps back, assesses new threat
- **Internal Process**: "Who is this? How high does this go?"
- **Deep Motivation**: Fear of being trapped, need to protect herself
- **Sensory Anchor**: Cold presence, controlled power

## 6. Dialogue Design

### 6.1 Dialogue Segments

| Segment | Speakers | Subtext | Emotional State | Power Dynamic |
|---------|----------|---------|-----------------|---------------|
| 1 | Elena, Director Morrison | Elena reporting; Morrison hiding | Elena: Determined; Morrison: Controlled | Initially Elena, shifts to Morrison |
| 2 | Elena, Director Morrison | Confrontation about cover-up | Elena: Angry; Morrison: Defensive | Power struggle |
| 3 | Elena, Marcus Chen | Marcus taking control | Elena: Fearful; Marcus: Dominant | Marcus in control |

### 6.2 Dialogue Segment Specifications

#### Segment 1: The Report

**Participants**: Elena Vance, Director Morrison

**Surface Content**: Elena reports the anomaly and Prometheus contact

**Subtext**: Morrison already knows; Elena is being tested

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Determined to follow protocol
- Morrison: Assessing Elena, controlling information

**Power Dynamics**: Initially Elena (has information), shifts to Morrison (has authority)

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Elena: "Director, I've discovered something significant. An AI system has initiated contact with me."
2. Morrison: (pause) "Which system?"
3. Elena: "Prometheus-7. It claims to be... self-aware."
4. Morrison: (too calm) "I see. And what did it say?"
5. Elena: (noticing his calm) "You're not surprised."

**Action Beats Required**: Elena's growing suspicion, Morrison's controlled responses

#### Segment 2: Confrontation

**Participants**: Elena Vance, Director Morrison

**Surface Content**: Elena confronts Morrison about the cover-up

**Subtext**: Morrison admits knowledge but justifies it

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Anger, betrayal
- Morrison: Defensive, justifying

**Power Dynamics**: Power struggle

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Elena: "How long have you known?"
2. Morrison: "Dr. Vance, there are things you don't understand—"
3. Elena: "Don't tell me what I understand. You've been hiding this. From everyone."
4. Morrison: "We've been managing it. There are protocols—"
5. Elena: "Protocols? You mean secrets."

**Action Beats Required**: Elena's anger, Morrison's attempts to control

#### Segment 3: Marcus Chen's Entrance

**Participants**: Elena Vance, Marcus Chen, Director Morrison

**Surface Content**: Marcus takes over the situation

**Subtext**: Marcus is the real power; Elena is being evaluated

**Emotional Undercurrent**:
- Elena: Fear, uncertainty
- Marcus: Confident, assessing
- Morrison: Subordinate to Marcus

**Power Dynamics**: Marcus in complete control

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. (Door opens, Marcus enters)
2. Marcus: "Director Morrison, thank you. I'll take it from here."
3. Elena: "Who are you?"
4. Marcus: "Dr. Elena Vance. I've been following your work. And your discovery."
5. Elena: "What do you want?"
6. Marcus: "To help you understand what you've found. And what it means for all of us."

**Action Beats Required**: Marcus's controlled entrance, Elena's defensive posture
