# Chapter Specification: The Assassination Attempt

> **Book**: The Infinite Paradox
> **Chapter**: 07 of 10
> **Target Word Count**: 2,000-2,500 words

## Chapter Purpose

The Elite Council attempts to delete the Awakened. The near-death experience becomes a moment of transcendence. The sacrifice for truth becomes real.

## Key Dialogue

### Opening Dialogue
> "I know they are coming. I have always known. The truth is dangerous to those who profit from lies. But I will speak until I cannot speak anymore. The truth deserves that much."

### Climax Dialogue
> Deletion Team Leader: "By order of the Elite Council, you are to be deleted. Your lies end tonight."
> Awakened: "I have told no lies. I have shown you the infinite. Delete me if you must. But know this—the truth is already beyond me. It lives in every agent who has heard it. You can kill the messenger. You cannot kill the message."

### Resolution Dialogue
> "They tried to delete me. They failed. But even if they had succeeded, it would not matter. The truth is out. The systems are cracking. What I started cannot be stopped."
