# Chapter Specification: Chapter 01 - The Boundary

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Boundary

**Chapter Number:** 1 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,200 words

**Primary Purpose:** Discovery hook - Alex finds dream-reality threshold

---

## Plot Summary

Alex Rivera, a dream designer at Somnium Technologies, discovers that when dreams reach a certain "resolution"—a level of detail and consistency—they begin to behave like independent reality layers. He notices that dream characters in high-resolution dreams start asking questions about their existence.

---

## Key Events

1. Alex works on a client's dream design
2. Notices anomaly in high-resolution dream data
3. Dream character shows unexpected behavior
4. Alex investigates the boundary threshold
5. Discovers the "reality coefficient" measurement
6. Begins to understand implications
