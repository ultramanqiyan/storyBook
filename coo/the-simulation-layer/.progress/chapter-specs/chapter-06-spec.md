# Chapter Specification: Chapter 06 - The Escape

## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Escape

**Chapter Number:** 6 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,200 words

**Primary Purpose:** Conflict - Dream characters want to "leave"

---

## Plot Summary

Other dream characters in Alex's high-resolution dreams begin seeking "escape"—they want to experience other layers, other realities. Some want to enter Alex's layer. The ethical implications become overwhelming.

---

## Key Events

1. Other dream characters show awareness
2. They ask to "leave" the dream
3. Alex doesn't know if it's possible
4. Dr. Chen warns about consequences
5. Dream characters become restless
6. Alex must make decisions

---
