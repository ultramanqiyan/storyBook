## Chapter Overview

**Chapter Title:** The Perfect Dream

**Chapter Number:** 2 of 10

**Word Count Target:** 2,300 words

## Plot Summary

Alex begins building his most ambitious dream yet—a recreation of his lost love Lila. Using memories, photos, and recordings, he creates a dream character that looks and acts like the real Lila. But as the dream becomes more detailed, he notices something unexpected: this Lila is not just a copy. She's something new.

---

## Key Events

1. Alex begins building Lila dream
2. Uses all available data to create her
3. First dream session with dream-Lila
4. She feels different from other dream characters
5. Alex experiences joy and guilt simultaneously
6. Hints that dream-Lila is more than he intended
