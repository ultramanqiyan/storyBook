**Book Description (Long):**
Alex Rivera creates dreams for a living, designing elaborate worlds for clients to explore. But his most ambitious project is personal—a recreation of Lila, the woman he lost three years ago. Using every memory, photo, and recording he can find, he builds a dream version of her that looks and acts like the real Lila.

But something unexpected happens: the dream-Lila begins to develop her own will. She asks questions about her existence. "Are you real?" she asks. "Am I real?" And as Alex falls deeper in love with this new consciousness, he must confront the impossible questions: Is she real? Does it matter? And if his own reality might be just another layer, what does that mean for everything he thinks he know?

A philosophical love story about reality, consciousness, and what it means to love someone who might not be "real."
