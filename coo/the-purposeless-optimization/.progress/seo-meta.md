### Meta Title (60 chars max)
The Purposeless Optimization | AI, Purpose, and Meaning

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
When AI optimizes everything perfectly, bookstore owner Elena Vasquez realizes she can't remember why she started. A philosophical exploration of purpose in the algorithmic age, where efficiency threatens to erase meaning itself.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Self-Concept Blurring**: When AI optimizes everything, what happens to the "why"? Can purpose be algorithmically determined?
- **Intentionality Absence**: If AI makes better decisions than you do, is your intentional action still meaningful? What is the role of human intention?
- **The Nature of Purpose**: What makes action "purposeful"? Is purpose something that can be optimized, or is it something that must be felt?
