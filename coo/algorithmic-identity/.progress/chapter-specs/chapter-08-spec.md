## 3. Opening
Maria's tests were subtle. She asked about things only Elena would know - not facts, but feelings. How did you feel when Nonna died? What were you thinking when we opened the bakery? Digital Elena's answers were perfect. Too perfect? Or exactly right?

---

## 4. Ending
After weeks of testing, Maria had to admit the truth: there was no test that could determine if digital Elena was "really" Elena. The question was unanswerable. And maybe that meant the question was wrong. Maybe identity wasn't something you could test. Maybe it was something you chose to believe.
