## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: Third-person limited
- **POV Character(s)**: Maria Rossi
- **POV Consistency**: Single POV throughout

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Warm, family-focused prose with philosophical depth
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, sensory and emotional focus
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate - family and food vocabulary mixed with philosophical terms

## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Personal Identity**: What makes you "you"?
2. **Consciousness Upload**: If consciousness is copied, which is the real you?
3. **Twin Identity**: The unique challenge of being one of two

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| What is identity? | Maria's twin sister is uploaded after terminal illness |
| Is the copy the same person? | Maria must decide if the digital version is her sister |
| What makes us unique? | Being a twin already challenges uniqueness; upload complicates it further |
