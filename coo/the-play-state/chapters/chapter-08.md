# Chapter 8: The Entry

Zara entered the play state.

She'd been playing her own game for months, but she'd never fully entered the play state herself. She'd been too serious about it. The game required play.

---

She sat in her studio, the game running, and she made a decision. She would play.

Not seriously.

---

She entered the game and let go. She let go of seriousness, of achievement, the optimization. She let go of everything except play.

---

The play state opened.

It was like a door that vast and bright, inviting her to step through. She stepped.

---

The universe played with her.

Zara felt it presence—vast, ancient, joyful. The cosmos itself was playing, not indifferent, not benevolent, but playful. Like a child with infinite toys, an eternity to play with.

---

**End of Chapter 8**
