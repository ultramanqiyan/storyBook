## 3. Opening
Sarah's story about truth ran on the front page. The AI flagged it too - but this time, the editor overrode the flag. "You were right," he told her. "The AI has its place. But so do human journalists. So do communities. So do the people who were there."

---

## 4. Ending
Sarah had found something important: truth wasn't a single perspective, but a conversation. The AI could contribute to that conversation. But it couldn't replace it. And that was what journalism was really about - not declaring truth, but seeking it together.
