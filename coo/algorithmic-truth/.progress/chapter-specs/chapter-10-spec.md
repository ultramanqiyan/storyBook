## 4. Opening
Sarah Mitchell typed the first paragraph of her next story. The AI would fact-check it, as it fact-checked everything now. But Sarah knew something the AI didn't: truth wasn't just about data. It was about experience, perspective, community. It was about the people who were there.

---

## 5. Ending
The question of truth would never be fully resolved. Sarah knew that now. But she also knew something else: her job wasn't to declare truth. It was to seek it - to listen, to question, to tell stories that helped others seek it too. The mystery remained. And somehow, that was exactly as it should be.
