## 3. Opening
Sarah spent the weekend reading about epistemology - the philosophy of knowledge. She learned about objective truth and subjective experience, about the difference between facts and meaning, about the ways that truth had always been contested. The AI wasn't discovering truth. It was constructing it.

---

## 4. Ending
Sarah realized that truth had never been simple. Every fact was interpreted, every account was partial, every truth was told from a perspective. The AI wasn't wrong because it was artificial. It was wrong because it claimed to have the one true perspective. And that was a claim no one - human or machine - could make.
