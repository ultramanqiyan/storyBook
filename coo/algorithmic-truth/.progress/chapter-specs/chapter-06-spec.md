## 3. Opening
Sarah researched how FactCheck AI worked. The system analyzed patterns in verified data sources - official records, established news outlets, government documents. It looked for consistency with "verified truth." But what counted as verified? Who decided? And what happened when experience contradicted data?

---

## 4. Ending
Sarah discovered that the AI's "verified data" didn't include her small-town newspaper. It didn't include the town clerk's notes. It didn't include the memories of the people who'd been there. The AI was deciding what was true based on what it had been told was true. And that wasn't the same as truth itself.
