## 3. Opening
Sarah interviewed her community. She asked them what they remembered about the council meeting, about Councilman Peters, about truth itself. Their answers were different - not contradictory, but complementary. Each person had seen the same event from a different angle. Together, their perspectives formed a richer truth than any single account.

---

## 4. Ending
Sarah wrote a new story - not about the council meeting, but about truth itself. About how the AI had missed what the community knew. About how truth wasn't something you could compute, but something you had to seek together. It was the most important story she'd ever written.
