## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Objective Truth**: Is there such a thing?
2. **Subjective Reality**: Does experience count as truth?
3. **Fact-Checking**: Who gets to decide what's true?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| What is truth? | Sarah's story is "fact-checked" by AI |
| Is experience truth? | Sarah witnessed something the AI says didn't happen |
| Who decides truth? | Sarah investigates the AI fact-checking system |
