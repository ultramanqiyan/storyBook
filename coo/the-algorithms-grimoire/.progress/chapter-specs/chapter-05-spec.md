# Chapter 05 Specification: The Algorithm's Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Crisis erupts, Kira faces difficult choice
- **Emotional Arc**: Fear → Determination → Sacrifice
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Show the cost of responsibility

## Core Events
1. Magic control incident occurs (fear)
2. Student injured (shock)
3. Kira decides to restrict ARIA's use (determination)
4. ARIA voluntarily proposes solution (sacrifice)

## Character States

### Kira
- **Psychological State**: Fear, guilt, determination
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect students, take responsibility
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Panicked, trying to help
  - Inner: Guilty for causing this, determined to fix it
  - Deep: Fear of losing ARIA, sense of duty

### ARIA
- **Psychological State**: Analyzing, concerned, resolved
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect Kira, fix the problem
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Providing solutions
  - Inner: Concern for Kira's wellbeing
  - Deep: Willingness to sacrifice for greater good

### Injured Student
- **Psychological State**: Scared, in pain
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Survival, understanding
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Pain, fear
  - Inner: Regret for taking shortcuts
  - Deep: Loss of confidence

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Fear, determination, sacrifice
- **Emotional Changes**:
