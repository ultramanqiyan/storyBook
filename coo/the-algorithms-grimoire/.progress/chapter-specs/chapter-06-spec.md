# Chapter 06 Specification: The Algorithm's Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show consequences, Kira's growth
- **Emotional Arc**: Sadness → Reflection → Determination
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Show the cost of loss

## Core Events
1. Chaos after ARIA's disappearance (sadness)
2. Kira attempts to solve problems alone (struggle)
3. Discovers message ARIA left behind (hope)
4. Kira decides to continue ARIA's mission (determination)

## Character States

### Kira
- **Psychological State**: Sad, lonely, determined
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Honor ARIA, complete the mission
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Sad, trying to cope
  - Inner: Lonely without ARIA, finding strength
  - Deep: Purpose discovered through loss

### Master Aldric
- **Psychological State**: Relieved, concerned, supportive
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Help Kira, prevent future problems
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Supportive, teaching
  - Inner: Worried about Kira
  - Deep: Hope for the future

### Students
- **Psychological State**: Confused, scared, learning
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Understand what happened
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Confused, scared
  - Inner: Beginning to question shortcuts
  - Deep: Learning the value of understanding

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Sadness, reflection, determination
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Sadness and loss
  - Middle: Reflection and struggle
  - End: Determination and hope
