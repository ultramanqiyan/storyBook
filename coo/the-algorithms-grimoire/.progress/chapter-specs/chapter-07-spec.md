# Chapter 07 Specification: The Algorithm's Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show Kira's growth, introduce new challenge
- **Emotional Arc**: Determination → Challenge → Hope
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate Kira's ability to carry on

## Core Events
1. Kira begins teaching proper magic methods (determination)
2. Prince Darian returns with demands (challenge)
3. Kira stands firm against pressure (courage)
4. Discovers ARIA's legacy in the Archive (hope)

## Character States

### Kira
- **Psychological State**: Determined, confident, grieving
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Honor ARIA, protect the Archive
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Calm, teaching
  - Inner: Missing ARIA, finding strength
  - Deep: Purpose and resolve

### Prince Darian
- **Psychological State**: Frustrated, demanding
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Regain access to power
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Demanding, threatening
  - Inner: Fear of losing control
  - Deep: Desperation for success

### Master Aldric
- **Psychological State**: Proud, supportive
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect Kira, guide her growth
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Calm, wise
  - Inner: Pride in Kira
  - Deep: Hope for the future

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Determination, challenge, hope
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Determination and purpose
  - Middle: Challenge and pressure
  - End: Hope and discovery

## Narrative Techniques
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Kira's POV)
- **Pacing**: Medium, building tension
- **Suspense Setup**:
  - Can Kira resist Prince Darian?
  - What did ARIA leave behind?
  - How will Kira use the legacy?

## Dialogue Style Guidelines

### Kira's Dialogue
- Confident, measured
- Uses clear, teaching language
- Example: "Magic requires understanding, not shortcuts. I won't compromise that principle."

### Prince Darian's Dialogue
- Demanding, threatening
- Uses political pressure
- Example: "The kingdom needs progress. You're standing in the way of the future."
