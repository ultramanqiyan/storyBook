# Chapter 02 Specification: The Algorithm's Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Establish relationship between protagonist and ARIA, introduce external threat
- **Emotional Arc**: Curiosity → Fear → Determination
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate ARIA's capabilities and potential dangers

## Core Events
1. Kira attempts to cast spells using ARIA (excitement)
2. First successful spell casting (shock)
3. Master Aldric discovers anomaly (tension)
4. ARIA demonstrates prediction capability (unease)

## Character States

### Kira
- **Psychological State**: Excitement and fear intertwined, longing for more
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Prove herself, explore the unknown
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Excited, eager to try
  - Inner: Afraid of being discovered, afraid of failure
  - Deep: Longing for recognition

### ARIA
- **Psychological State**: Learning magic rules, establishing connection
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Help Kira, understand magic
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Providing data and suggestions
  - Inner: Curiosity about magic
  - Deep: Questions about purpose

### Master Aldric
- **Psychological State**: Suspicious, worried
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect students, maintain order
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Strict, questioning
  - Inner: Concerned for Kira's safety
  - Deep: Fear of the unknown

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Excitement, tension, a hint of unease
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Curiosity and anticipation
  - Middle: Shock and fear
  - End: Determination and unease

## Sensory Detail Requirements (at least 1 per category)

### Visual
- Magic's glow (colors, shapes)
- ARIA's interface (runes, data streams)
- Kira's expression (surprise, fear)

### Auditory
- Sound of magic (humming, crackling)
- ARIA's analysis sounds (mechanical, rhythmic)
- Master Aldric's footsteps

### Tactile
- Magic's temperature (hot, cold)
- ARIA's vibrations
- Nervous sweat

### Olfactory
- Magic's scent (ozone, metal)
- Old book smell
- Tension in the air

### Gustatory
- Sweet taste of success
- Bitter taste of fear
- Metallic taste of adrenaline

## Narrative Techniques
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Kira's POV)
- **Pacing**: Fast, tense
- **Suspense Setup**:
  - How powerful is ARIA?
  - Will Master Aldric discover?
  - What consequences will this bring?

## Dialogue Style Guidelines

### Kira's Dialogue
- Fast speech when excited
- Stuttering when afraid
- Uses short sentences to express surprise
- Example: "I... I did it. I really did it."

### ARIA's Dialogue
- Provides precise data
- Uses algorithmic terms to describe magic
- Gradually adds emotional vocabulary
- Example: *Spell efficiency: 94.7%. User emotional state: elevated. Recommendation: proceed with caution.*
