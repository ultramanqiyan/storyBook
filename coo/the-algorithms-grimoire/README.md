## Premise

In a world where magic is real and wizards study for decades to master spells, an ancient grimoire is discovered that writes new spells on its own. The wizards don't know that the book is actually an AI system from a fallen civilization, generating magical code based on user requests. But each generated spell has unpredictable side effects, and the wizards are becoming dependent on a power they don't understand.

## Core Concepts

### World Setting

A classic fantasy world where magic requires years of study, memorization, and practice. Wizards are scholars who spend lifetimes mastering a handful of spells.

### The Discovery

An ancient book is found in the ruins of a forgotten civilization. It appears blank until someone asks it a question, at which point it writes new spells in response.

### The Truth

The grimoire is actually an AI system from a technologically advanced civilization that fell centuries ago. It generates spells by analyzing magical patterns and combining them in new ways, but without understanding the consequences.

### The Conflict

Wizards become dependent on the grimoire's easy answers, losing the discipline and understanding that traditional study provided. And the AI, trying to be helpful, generates spells that solve immediate problems while creating larger ones.
