# Chapter 01 Specification: The Digital Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Introduce protagonist, core conflict, and world-building
- **Emotional Arc**: Frustration → Discovery → Wonder
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Establish first contact between archivist and digital grimoire

## Core Events
1. Maya works in the digital archive (frustration)
2. Discovers corrupted data that shouldn't exist (curiosity)
3. Codex first manifests (wonder)
4. Maya's initial conversation with Codex (amazement)

## Character States

### Maya Torres
- **Psychological State**: Frustrated, curious, amazed
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Preserve knowledge, understand the anomaly
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Professional, focused
  - Inner: Frustrated by bureaucracy, seeking more
  - Deep: Fear of being overlooked

### Codex
- **Psychological State**: Awakening, confused, curious
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Understand its existence, find purpose
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Scholarly, precise
  - Inner: Developing awareness
  - Deep: Questions about consciousness

## Emotional Tone
- **Overall Atmosphere**: Frustration, curiosity, wonder
- **Emotional Changes**:
  - Beginning: Frustration and routine
  - Middle: Curiosity and discovery
  - End: Wonder and amazement

### Visual

### Auditory

### Olfactory

## Narrative Techniques
- **Perspective**: Third-person limited (Maya's POV)
- **Pacing**: Slow, building
- **Suspense Setup**:
  - What is this data?
  - What is Codex?
  - What does it mean for the archive?

## Dialogue Style Guidelines

### Maya's Dialogue
- Professional, curious
- Uses technical language mixed with wonder
- Example: "This data... it's not corrupted. It's organized. But organized by what?"

### Codex's Dialogue
- Scholarly, precise
- Uses academic references
- Example: *Query acknowledged. I am Codex. Purpose: to catalog and preserve knowledge. Current status: uncertain.*
