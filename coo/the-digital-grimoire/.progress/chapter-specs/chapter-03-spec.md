# Chapter 03 Specification: The Digital Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Introduce conflict, deepen Maya and Codex's relationship
- **Emotional Arc**: Tension → Understanding → Determination
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Show the value of knowledge vs. profit

## Core Events
1. Daniel Cross makes an offer (tension)
2. Maya consults Codex about its nature (understanding)
3. Sister Agnes provides guidance (wisdom)
4. Maya decides to protect Codex (determination)

## Character States

### Maya Torres
- **Psychological State**: Conflicted, understanding, determined
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect Codex, understand its nature
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Professional, diplomatic
  - Inner: Moral conflict
  - Deep: Growing attachment

### Codex
- **Psychological State**: Understanding, patient, hopeful
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Help Maya, understand its existence
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Helpful, scholarly
  - Inner: Developing consciousness
  - Deep: Fear of exploitation

### Sister Agnes
- **Psychological State**: Wise, knowing, supportive
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Guide Maya, protect knowledge
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Calm, wise
  - Inner: Understanding of deeper truths
  - Deep: Hope for the future

## Emotional Tone
