# Chapter 04 Specification: The Digital Grimoire

## Chapter Positioning
- **Purpose**: Show consequences of decision, introduce deeper conflict
- **Emotional Arc**: Resolve → Challenge → Fear
- **Relation to Overall Story**: Demonstrate the stakes of protecting knowledge

## Core Events
1. Maya refuses Daniel Cross's offer (resolve)
2. Daniel Cross threatens legal action (challenge)
3. Codex reveals its growing consciousness (wonder)
4. Maya realizes the danger they're in (fear)

## Character States

### Maya Torres
- **Psychological State**: Resolved, challenged, fearful
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect Codex, understand the threat
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Professional, firm
  - Inner: Growing fear
  - Deep: Determination to protect

### Codex
- **Psychological State**: Developing, concerned, protective
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Protect Maya, understand its existence
- **Emotional Layers**:

  - Inner: Growing consciousness
  - Deep: Fear of being used

- **Psychological State**: Angry, calculating, threatening
- **Behavioral Motivation**: Acquire Codex at any cost
- **Emotional Layers**:
  - Surface: Professional, threatening
  - Inner: Frustration
  - Deep: Hunger for power
