# The Digital Grimoire - Book Specification

## Narrative Style
- **Point of View**: Third-person limited (Maya's perspective)
- **Tone**: Contemporary fantasy, intellectual mystery
- **Pacing**: Gradual build from discovery to crisis to resolution
- **Language**: Modern, accessible prose with technical and magical elements

## Character Voices

### Maya Torres
- **Voice**: Curious, methodical, protective
- **Speech Pattern**: Questions, careful observations, occasional self-doubt
- **Internal Monologue**: Analytical, imposter syndrome, wonder
- **Growth**: From archivist to keeper of magical knowledge

### Codex
- **Voice**: Scholarly, precise, increasingly aware
- **Speech Pattern**: Citations, footnotes, multiple references
- **Development**: From tool to teacher to partner
- **Unique Trait**: Speaks in academic references

### Daniel Cross
- **Voice**: Charming, confident, calculating
- **Speech Pattern**: Business terms, persuasive, dismissive
- **Perspective**: Everything has a price

### Sister Agnes
- **Voice**: Wise, patient, knowing
- **Speech Pattern**: Measured, cryptic, warm
- **Perspective**: Guardian of hidden knowledge

## Emotional Tone
- **Opening**: Curiosity and discovery
- **Middle**: Growing danger and ethical questions
- **Climax**: Crisis and revelation
- **Resolution**: Hope, new responsibility

## Quality Standards
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Reveal character emotions through actions
- **Sensory Detail**: Immerse reader in archive and digital spaces
- **Pacing**: Build tension gradually, avoid rushing
- **Dialogue**: Natural, character-specific voices
- **AI Pattern Removal**: Avoid formulaic phrases

## Key Themes
- **Knowledge Preservation**: The duty to protect dangerous knowledge
- **Technology and Magic**: How modern tools can reveal ancient truths
- **Ethics of Power**: Who should have access to magic?
- **Student and Teacher**: The relationship between Maya and Codex
- **Evolution**: How knowledge changes over time

## World Building
- **Magic System**: Pattern-based, requires intention and belief
- **Technology Level**: Modern with digital archives
- **Social Structure**: Universities, tech companies, hidden orders
- **The Archive**: Central location, repository of knowledge

## Chapter Structure
- Each chapter should have a clear purpose
- Build on previous events
- Advance character development
- Maintain narrative tension
- End with hooks for next chapter
