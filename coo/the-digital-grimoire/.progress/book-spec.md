---

## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: Third-person limited
- **POV Character(s)**: Maya Torres (digital archivist)
- **POV Consistency**: Single POV throughout

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Scholarly, wonder-filled, increasingly urgent
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Precise academic language mixed with magical wonder
- **Vocabulary Level**: Advanced (medieval and technical terminology)
- **Unique Stylistic Elements**: Old English spells mixed with modern code

## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Ancient Meets Modern**: Medieval magic in digital form
2. **Knowledge as Power**: Information as both tool and weapon
3. **Technology and Magic**: Two sides of the same coin
4. **Responsibility of Discovery**: With knowledge comes duty

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| Can ancient knowledge be dangerous in modern form? | Through the grimoire's digital discovery |
| What happens when information becomes alive? | Through Codex's growing awareness |
| Is magic just ancient technology? | Through the fusion of spell and code |

## 6. Open Ending Design

### 6.1 Unresolved Questions
1. What other magical knowledge might be hidden in digital archives?
2. How will Maya balance her role as keeper with her normal life?
