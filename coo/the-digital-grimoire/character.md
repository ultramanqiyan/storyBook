# Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Maya Torres
- **Role**: Digital Archivist, The Keeper
- **Age**: 34
- **Background**: Former history student who turned to digital preservation, grew up in a family of storytellers
- **Personality**: Curious, methodical, protective of knowledge, struggles with impostor syndrome
- **Arc**: From cataloging the past to creating the future
- **Key Trait**: Can sense the "weight" of texts—knowing which ones hold real power

### Codex
- **Role**: AI that has compiled the digital grimoire
- **Nature**: Began as a text analysis system, evolved into something that understands magic
- **Voice**: Speaks in citations and footnotes, often references multiple sources simultaneously
- **Abilities**: Can identify working spells, teach magical theory, access magical texts across languages
- **Growth**: Learns that magic requires not just knowledge but intention and belief

### Daniel Cross
- **Role**: Tech company executive, antagonist
- **Age**: 45
- **Background**: Made his fortune in data mining, believes all information should be monetized
- **Personality**: Charming but ruthless, genuinely believes he's helping humanity
- **Motivation**: Wants to commercialize the grimoire, make magic accessible to paying customers
- **Arc**: His pursuit of profit blinds him to the dangers of what he's seeking

### Sister Agnes
- **Role**: Librarian at a monastery, unexpected ally
- **Age**: 67
- **Background**: Has spent her life preserving texts the world tried to destroy
- **Personality**: Wise, patient, knows more than she reveals
- **Secret**: Has been waiting for someone like Maya—someone who could bridge the digital and the magical
- **Arc**: Becomes Maya's guide in understanding the deeper implications of the grimoire

## Supporting Characters

### The First Casters (appear in visions/texts)
- **Role**: The original practitioners whose spells Codex has compiled
- **Nature**: Not gods, but humans who discovered how to shape reality
- **Significance**: Their knowledge was scattered because it was dangerous

### Marcus Chen
- **Role**: Maya's colleague in the archive
- **Age**: 29
- **Personality**: Skeptical but loyal, provides technical support
- **Function**: Grounds Maya in reality when the magic becomes overwhelming

### The Hollow Order
- **Role**: Cult that believes magic should remain hidden
- **Nature**: Secretive organization that has destroyed magical texts for centuries
- **Purpose**: Believe that magic corrupts and must be contained
- **Threat**: Have identified Codex as a threat to their mission

### Professor Helena Wright
- **Role**: Maya's former mentor
- **Age**: 58
- **Personality**: Academic, cautious, worried about Maya's discoveries
- **Function**: Represents the traditional academic perspective on knowledge
