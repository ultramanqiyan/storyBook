## Character Voice Consistency

Michael Chen's voice should:
- Start authentic: warm, attentive, genuinely caring
- Become efficient: polished, personalized but hollow
- End authentic again: imperfect, present, real

Key phrases to track:
- "Let me understand your situation" (authentic)
- "I want to make sure this is right for you" (authentic)
- AI-generated phrases (personalized but soulless)
