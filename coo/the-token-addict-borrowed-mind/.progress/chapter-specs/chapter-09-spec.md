## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
The morning after the revelation, Marcus woke up and made a decision. He would hide the truth. He would perform. He would be the Marcus everyone expected—the confident, capable senior engineer who solved problems and made decisions. The fact that those decisions now came from an AI was something he would keep to himself. He showered, dressed, and looked at himself in the mirror. The face staring back was familiar, but the eyes seemed different. Emptier. He pushed the thought away and headed to work. The architecture review was today, and he needed to prepare.

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
Back at his desk, Marcus sat in the glow of his monitors and felt the weight of what he'd done. He'd successfully presented AI-generated thinking as his own. He'd received praise for insights that weren't his. He'd maintained the mask, and no one had noticed. But the cost was becoming clear. Every performance made the next one harder. Every praise felt like an accusation. Every "good thinking" reminded him that he wasn't thinking at all. The mask was becoming his face. And somewhere beneath it, the real Marcus—if there was still a real Marcus—was disappearing. He opened CodeForge for the next task, typed the prompt, and watched the code appear. This was his life now. Performing. Pretending. Being the mouthpiece for an AI that had become his mind. The question from before echoed: *Who is thinking now?* He didn't have an answer. He wasn't sure he ever would.
