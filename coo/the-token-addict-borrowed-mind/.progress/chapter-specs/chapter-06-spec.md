## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
The morning started like any other. Marcus arrived at the office, opened his laptop, and began his AI-assisted workflow. The rationalization from yesterday had settled into a comfortable certainty. He was fine. Everyone used AI. This was just how things worked now. But around noon, he noticed something different. Hushed conversations. Worried glances. The particular tension that always preceded bad news. "Did you hear?" someone whispered as they passed his desk. "There's going to be layoffs." Marcus felt a cold knot form in his stomach. Layoffs. The word hung in the air like a threat.

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
After David left, the office slowly returned to its normal rhythm. Keyboards clicked, meetings resumed, the AI usage leaderboard updated. Marcus stared at his screen, CodeForge waiting for his next prompt, and felt something he hadn't felt in weeks: fear. Not fear of being laid off—he was too "productive" for that. Fear of something deeper. He was trapped now. He had to keep using AI. He had no choice. If he stopped, even for a day, he'd be like David. And David was gone. The thought should have motivated him to work harder, to prove his value. Instead, it made him want to run. But where could he go? AI was everywhere. It was the future. It was his cage. He typed the next prompt, watched the code appear, and wondered if this was what freedom looked like.
