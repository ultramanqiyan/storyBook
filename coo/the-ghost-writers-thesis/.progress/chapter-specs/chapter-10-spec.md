## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
The library was quiet in the early morning, the stacks stretching into darkness like questions waiting to be asked. Elena sat at her usual table, the worn surface familiar beneath her hands. She had been coming here for weeks, relearning how to wonder. The process was slow, often frustrating, but she was making progress. She could feel it.

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
Elena looked at the campus around her, at the students walking to class, at the buildings full of research and questions. The system was still powerful. The fight wasn't over. But for the first time in a long time, Elena Martinez had a question that was truly hers. And that was everything. She walked forward, into the morning light, ready to find out what came next.
