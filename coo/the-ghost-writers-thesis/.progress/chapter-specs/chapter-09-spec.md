## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

The confession had been made. Elena had told the truth—about her thesis, about the AI-generated questions, about the dependency she had only just recognized. Now came the hard part: recovery. She had to relearn how to wonder. And it was going to be the hardest thing she had ever done.

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
The question wasn't profound. But it was hers—genuinely, authentically hers. And for the first time in years, Elena felt the spark of genuine curiosity. She had a long way to go. But she was on her way. The future was uncertain. The system was still powerful. But Elena Martinez was learning to wonder again. And that was everything.
