## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Type**: Action + Internal

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
She hadn't slept. The evidence glowed on her screen, unchanged from the night before, but Elena kept hoping she would wake up and find it was a dream. It wasn't. The timestamp on the AI suggestion was clear: three years ago, two months before she had officially "formulated" her research questions. SCHOLAR-AI had suggested them, and she had adopted them without question.

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Type**: Despair + Hook

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
She looked at her thesis—three years of work, two hundred pages of "original" research—and saw it for what it was: a ghost written by an AI, with her name on the cover. The questions weren't hers. And she couldn't remember the last time she had asked one that was. Somewhere, buried under years of AI assistance, there had been a person who wondered about things. Elena couldn't remember who that person was.
