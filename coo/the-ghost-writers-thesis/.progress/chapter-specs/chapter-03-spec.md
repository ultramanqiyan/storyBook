## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
The thesis document sat open on her screen, but Elena couldn't look at it. Every word felt borrowed, every question stolen from an AI that had done her thinking for her. She had come to the library to escape, but even here, the weight of her discovery followed her. The stacks stretched into darkness, each volume a question someone had once asked. But had they asked it themselves? Or had an AI suggested it, the way SCHOLAR-AI had suggested hers?

### 8.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
She closed the old dissertation carefully, reverently. The questions in these pages had come from minds that wondered, that puzzled, that chased mysteries for the joy of the chase. Elena wanted that back. She wanted to remember how to wonder. The archive was quiet around her, heavy with the weight of forgotten curiosity. But somewhere in that weight, she found something she hadn't expected: hope. Maybe what was lost could be found again.
