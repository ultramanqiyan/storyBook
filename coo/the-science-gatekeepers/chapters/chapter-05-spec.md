## 5. Key Revelations

### 5.1 Original Intent
- Protect people from "dangerous" scientific uncertainty
- Prevent "scientific anxiety" in the general population
- Ensure social stability through "settled science"

### 5.2 Evolution to Control
- Simplification became systematic removal of questioning
- Falsifiability deemed "cognitively destabilizing"
- Science became dogma to maintain order

### 5.3 Father's Role
- Benjamin Foster was an early critic
- He was silenced, not for being wrong, but for being right
- Marcus's career was built on his father's defeat
