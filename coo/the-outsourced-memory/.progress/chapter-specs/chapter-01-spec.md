### 2.3 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: Morning Confidence (Position: 0-15%)
- **Trigger**: Mike's morning routine, preparing for showings
- **Surface Reaction**: Efficient movements, checking his mental list of properties
- **Internal Process**: *Three showings today. The Henderson house, the Maple Street property, and that new listing on Oak Creek.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Pride in knowing his inventory without looking; 25 years of walking these streets
- **Sensory Anchor**: The weight of his keys, the smell of coffee, the feel of the steering wheel

#### Beat 2: The House That Knows (Position: 15-35%)
- **Trigger**: Entering the Henderson house before the client arrives
- **Surface Reaction**: Walking through without looking at his notes, touching walls, feeling floors
- **Internal Process**: *Foundation's solid. New roof, maybe five years old. That smell - basement had water, but they fixed it. Good fix, not a cover-up.*
- **Deep Motivation**: This is who he is - the man who knows houses in his body
- **Sensory Anchor**: Floorboards under his feet, the slight tilt he feels before he sees it

#### Beat 3: The Client Response (Position: 35-55%)
- **Trigger**: Client arrives, Mike shows the house using sensory details
- **Surface Reaction**: Animated storytelling, pointing out details the client would miss
- **Internal Process**: *She's listening. She feels it too - this house has good bones.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Validation of his approach; the satisfaction of a client who trusts him
- **Sensory Anchor**: The client's eyes following his hand, her posture relaxing

#### Beat 4: The Introduction (Position: 55-75%)
- **Trigger**: Broker calls, asks to stop by office, introduces HomeMatch AI
- **Surface Reaction**: Polite interest, asking questions, maintaining professional demeanor
- **Internal Process**: *Another app. They come and go. But this one... it's different.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Resistance to change, fear of irrelevance, but also genuine curiosity
- **Sensory Anchor**: The tablet's cold screen, the clean interface, the absence of human mess

#### Beat 5: The First Prediction (Position: 75-100%)
- **Trigger**: HomeMatch AI makes an accurate prediction about a property
- **Surface Reaction**: Raised eyebrow, slight nod, "That's... interesting."
- **Internal Process**: *It got that right. But it doesn't know why. It doesn't feel the house.*
- **Deep Motivation**: First seed of doubt - what if feeling isn't necessary?
- **Sensory Anchor**: The glow of the screen, the silence after the prediction

### 3.2 Event Descriptions

#### Event 1: Morning Routine
- **Description**: Mike prepares for his day, mentally reviewing his properties without checking any device
- **Setup**: Establish his 25-year routine
- **Execution**: Show him moving through morning tasks while mentally cataloging properties
- **Consequence**: Reader understands his expertise is internal, not external
- **Connection to Theme**: Embodied knowledge vs. digital storage

#### Event 2: The Henderson House Showing
- **Description**: Mike shows a house to a client, demonstrating his sensory-based knowledge
- **Setup**: Client arrives, Mike has already "read" the house
- **Execution**: Mike points out details using all senses - the slope in the floor, the smell of the basement, the sound of the pipes
- **Consequence**: Client is impressed, trusts Mike's judgment
- **Connection to Theme**: Human expertise that AI cannot replicate

#### Event 3: HomeMatch AI Introduction
- **Description**: Broker Tom introduces Mike to a new AI tool that claims to match clients with properties
- **Setup**: Tom has been using it, wants Mike to try
- **Execution**: Demonstration of the AI's interface and capabilities
- **Consequence**: Mike is skeptical but intrigued
- **Connection to Theme**: The encroachment of AI on human expertise

#### Event 4: The First Prediction
- **Description**: HomeMatch AI makes a prediction about a property that Mike knows is accurate
- **Setup**: Tom asks AI about a property Mike knows well
- **Execution**: AI provides data that matches Mike's internal knowledge
- **Consequence**: Mike feels first seed of doubt about his unique value
- **Connection to Theme**: Can AI replicate human judgment?
