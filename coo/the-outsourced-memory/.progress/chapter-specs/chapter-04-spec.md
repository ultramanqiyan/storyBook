The morning had started like any other. Mike checked his HomeMatch notifications over coffee, reviewed the day's AI-matched showings, and headed to the office with the particular confidence of a man whose career was finally running on autopilot. ### 7.2 Ending (Last 200 Words)

**Ending Type**: Internal - Suppressed unease

**Ending Paragraph Draft**:
```
Driving home that evening, Mike caught himself humming along to the radio. The Patterson meeting had gone fine - the AI had provided everything he needed, and Mr. Patterson had left satisfied. But something nagged at the edge of his mind, a faint static he couldn't quite tune out. He turned up the radio. Tomorrow would be another day of matches and closings, another day of success. He didn't need to remember everything. That's what the app was for.
```
