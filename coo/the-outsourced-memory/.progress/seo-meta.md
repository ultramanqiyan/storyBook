### Meta Title (60 chars max)
The Outsourced Memory | A Novel About Agency and Identity

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
A philosophical exploration of cognitive outsourcing. When a real estate agent outsources his memory to AI, he must question: what makes professional intuition human?

### Philosophical Themes
- **Cognitive Outsourcing**: What happens when we outsource not just tasks but cognitive processes?
- **Memory and Identity**: If memory is outsourced, does experience still belong to us?
- **Professional Intuition**: Is human "gut feeling" fundamentally different from AI pattern recognition?
- **Embodied Knowledge**: Can tacit, body-based knowledge be digitized?
- **Agency Crisis**: What does it mean to "choose" when AI makes better choices?
