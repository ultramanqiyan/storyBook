The simulation ended, and David sat in silence. The digital Elena had been articulate, intelligent, clearly based on Elena's memories and personality. But something had been missing—or had it? Was the unease he felt just his own bias, his own need for a biological body? Or was there something essential that the technology couldn't capture? He didn't know. And that uncertainty was the heart of the problem.
