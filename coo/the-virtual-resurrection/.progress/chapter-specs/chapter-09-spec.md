### Opening
Elena's condition worsened rapidly. Within days, she was unable to leave her bed, her body failing while her mind remained sharp. The upload was scheduled for the following week. There was no more time for deliberation, for philosophical exploration. It was time to decide.

### Ending
David stood by Elena's bedside, ready to offer what he could. "I cannot tell you if this is resurrection," he said. "I cannot tell you if the thing that wakes up after the upload will be you. But I can tell you this: you have faced your mortality with courage and honesty. You have asked the right questions, even when there were no answers. And whatever comes next, that matters." Elena smiled. "Is that a blessing?" David smiled back. "It's the best I have. Take it as what it is."
