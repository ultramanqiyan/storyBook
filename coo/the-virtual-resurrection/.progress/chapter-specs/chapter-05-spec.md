### Opening
Sarah Vasquez was a lawyer, like her mother had been before founding her tech company. She was sharp, protective, and clearly struggling with her mother's decision. "Rabbi," she said, "I don't know what to do. My mother is dying. And instead of making peace with that, she's planning to... upload herself. To become a digital ghost. And I'm supposed to just accept that?"

### Ending
David left the family meeting with a heavy heart. Elena's decision was tearing her family apart. Sarah couldn't accept a digital version of her mother as her mother. The grandchildren were confused and frightened. And Elena, facing death, was reaching for a technological solution that might or might not preserve her identity. There were no easy answers here. Only pain, and love, and the desperate human need for continuity.
