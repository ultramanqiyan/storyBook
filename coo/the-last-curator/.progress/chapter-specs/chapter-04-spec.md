**Narrative Function**
- **Purpose**: Test Marcus's method under controlled conditions
- **Information Revealed**: He can't reliably distinguish AI art

## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening
```
The blind test was Helen Marsh's idea. "We need to verify the Method before the exhibition," she'd said, as if it were routine. As if it weren't a test of everything Marcus had built his career on. He'd agreed, because refusing would have been worse. Now, sitting in the conference room with ten images in front of him, he wondered if that was true.
```

### 8.2 Ending
```
He walked home that night, ignoring his phone, ignoring the messages that were surely piling up. Elena was wrong. The test was flawed. His method was real. He just needed to prove it. Tomorrow, he would look at Jimmy's work again, and he would see - really see - the humanity in it. He hoped.
```
