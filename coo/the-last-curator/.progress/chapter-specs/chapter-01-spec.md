## 1. Chapter Positioning

### 1.2 Chapter Purpose
**Primary Objective**: Introduce Marcus Webb and his "Webb Method" while establishing the satirical tone and the world of AI art certification

**Secondary Objectives**:
- Demonstrate Marcus's expertise (and hint at its possible emptiness)
- Establish the art world's obsession with "authenticity"

### 2.3 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: The Preparation (Position: 0-15%)
- **Trigger**: Marcus prepares for his lecture in the green room
- **Surface Reaction**: Checking his appearance, reviewing notes, calm demeanor
- **Internal Process**: Running through his greatest hits, mentally rehearsing key phrases
- **Deep Motivation**: Need for validation, fear of being exposed as fraud
- **Sensory Anchor**: The smell of the museum (floor wax, old paper) - familiar and comforting

#### Beat 2: The Performance (Position: 15-40%)
- **Trigger**: Marcus delivers his signature lecture
- **Surface Reaction**: Confident gestures, dramatic pauses, engaging the audience
- **Internal Process**: Riding the high of recognition, feeling "seen"
- **Deep Motivation**: This is his identity - without this, who is he?
- **Sensory Anchor**: The spotlight warmth, the rustle of the audience

#### Beat 3: The Demonstration (Position: 40-55%)
- **Trigger**: Marcus demonstrates the Webb Method on a new piece
- **Surface Reaction**: Confident pronouncements, dramatic reveals
- **Internal Process**: A moment of hesitation - did he actually see what he claimed?
- **Deep Motivation**: The fear that he's been fooling himself all along
- **Sensory Anchor**: The texture of the canvas under his fingers

#### Beat 4: The Commission (Position: 55-80%)
- **Trigger**: Museum director offers Marcus the "Humanity's Last Breath" exhibition
- **Surface Reaction**: Gracious acceptance, professional enthusiasm
- **Internal Process**: Excitement mixed with vague anxiety about the scale
- **Deep Motivation**: This could be his legacy - or his undoing
- **Sensory Anchor**: The weight of the portfolio in his hands

#### Beat 5: The Aftermath (Position: 80-100%)
- **Trigger**: Marcus alone after the event, processing the day
- **Surface Reaction**: Satisfied smile, checking messages
- **Internal Process**: A flicker of doubt he quickly suppresses
- **Deep Motivation**: The exhaustion of maintaining certainty
- **Sensory Anchor**: The silence of his apartment, the taste of scotch

### 3.2 Event Descriptions

#### Event 1: Pre-lecture Preparation
- **Description**: Marcus prepares in the green room, checking his appearance and reviewing notes
- **Setup**: Establish his routine, his care for presentation
- **Execution**: Show his practiced confidence, but also a moment of looking too long in the mirror
- **Consequence**: He's ready to perform, but we sense the performance
- **Connection to Theme**: The curated self vs. authentic self

#### Event 2: The Webb Method Lecture
- **Description**: Marcus delivers his signature lecture on detecting "human soul" in art
- **Setup**: His reputation precedes him; the audience is rapt
- **Execution**: Show his dramatic style, the pseudo-profound language, the audience's reaction
- **Consequence**: Establishes his authority and the world's values
- **Connection to Theme**: The commodification of "soul"

#### Event 3: Live Demonstration
- **Description**: Marcus demonstrates his method on a new, unfamiliar piece
- **Setup**: The audience expects him to succeed
- **Execution**: He makes confident pronouncements, but there's a beat of hesitation
- **Consequence**: He's right (or seems to be), but we see the uncertainty
- **Connection to Theme**: Is expertise real or performed?

#### Event 4: Commission Offer

- **Setup**: The most important show of his career
- **Execution**: Professional exchange with undercurrent of ambition
- **Consequence**: Marcus commits to finding the most "authentic" human artists

#### Event 5: Post-event Reflection

- **Setup**: The contrast between public success and private doubt
- **Execution**: A moment of genuine reflection, quickly suppressed
- **Consequence**: We see the crack in his certainty
