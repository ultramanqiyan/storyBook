Helen Marsh didn't fire him. That would have been too clean. Instead, she asked him to "step aside" for the good of the museum. The phrasing was careful, corporate, designed to let everyone save face. But they both knew what it meant. Marcus Webb was no longer the curator of "Humanity's Last Breath."
