### Meta Title (60 chars max)

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
A darkly comic novel about an art curator who claims to see "human soul" in art—until AI makes him question everything. For readers who enjoyed The Circle.

### Philosophical Themes
- **The Death of Authorship**: What does it mean to create when machines can generate art?
- **Authenticity as Commodity**: How the art world's obsession with "human touch" became its own form of commodification
- **Expertise and Identity**: What happens when your professional identity is built on an ability that may not exist?
- **Meaning Without Intention**: Can art have meaning without human intention?
- **The Uncanny Valley of Creativity**: When human and machine creation become indistinguishable
