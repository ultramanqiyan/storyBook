# Chapter 08: The Choice

"Can I have more control," she said slowly, "but with limits? Something between the two options?"

Dr. Vasquez raised an eyebrow. "What do you mean?"

Monitoring. And if your patterns become dangerous again, I would have the authority to restore restrictions."
