## 5. Thematic Depth

### 5.1 Core Themes
1. **The Three Gunas**: All decisions fall into three tendencies—Sattva (balance), Rajas (activity), Tamas (inertia)
2. **Randomness ≠ Freedom**: Random choices feel "out of control," not free
3. **Conscious Balance**: Freedom is knowing which guna is needed and choosing it

### 5.2 Sanskrit Concepts
| Term | Meaning | AI Mapping |
|------|---------|-----------|
| **Sattva** | Balance, purity, light | Balanced optimization, careful analysis |
| **Rajas** | Activity, passion, desire | Aggressive optimization, rapid action |
| **Tamas** | Inertia, darkness, heaviness | Conservative repetition, minimal change |
