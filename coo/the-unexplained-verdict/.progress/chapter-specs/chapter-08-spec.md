## 8. Key Paragraph Specifications

### 8.1 Opening (First 200 Words)

**Opening Type**: Object with character state

**Opening Paragraph Draft**:
```
The files sat on her desk like accusations. Jennifer picked up the first one - a claim from three months ago, her signature at the bottom, her name attached to a decision she couldn't explain. She opened it and began to read, really read, for the first time in months. The details were there: the claimant's story, the damage assessment, the AI's verdict. But beneath all of it was her signature, her name, her pretense of understanding.
```
