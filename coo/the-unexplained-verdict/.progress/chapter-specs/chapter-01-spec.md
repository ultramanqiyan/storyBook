### 2.3 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: Morning Routine (Position: 0-15%)
- **Trigger**: Beginning of workday, familiar patterns
- **Surface Reaction**: Efficient movements, checking files, coffee at desk
- **Internal Process**: *Another day. Another set of claims. Twelve years and I still feel the weight of each one.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Pride in her work, genuine care for clients, sense of responsibility
- **Sensory Anchor**: The weight of her pen, the smell of Cleveland lake wind through the window

#### Beat 2: The Flagged Claim (Position: 15-35%)
- **Trigger**: ClaimSmart highlights a claim with 73% fraud probability
- **Surface Reaction**: Leans forward, studies the data, cross-references with her experience
- **Internal Process**: *Interesting. The system caught something I might have missed initially. Let me verify.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Professional thoroughness, respect for the tool, but also her own expertise
- **Sensory Anchor**: The blue glow of the screen, the click of her mouse

#### Beat 3: Verification (Position: 35-55%)
- **Trigger**: Her own analysis confirms the AI's assessment
- **Surface Reaction**: Nods, makes notes, feels the familiar satisfaction of a correct judgment
- **Internal Process**: *Good. The system works. And I still have my instincts.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Validation of both the tool and her own competence
- **Sensory Anchor**: The tightness in her stomach that comes with fraud detection (still present)

#### Beat 4: The Unexplained Factor (Position: 55-75%)
- **Trigger**: ClaimSmart shows additional factors she can't immediately identify
- **Surface Reaction**: Pauses, frowns slightly, tries to trace the reasoning
- **Internal Process**: *How did it weight these factors? The probability seems right, but I can't see the full chain.*
- **Deep Motivation**: First subtle erosion of her ability to explain her own judgments
- **Sensory Anchor**: A slight hollowness in her chest, quickly dismissed

#### Beat 5: Resolution and Departure (Position: 75-100%)
- **Trigger**: Day ends, claim resolved, client notified
- **Surface Reaction**: Signs off, packs up, heads home
- **Internal Process**: *Good day. Productive. The system is making things easier.*
- **Deep Motivation**: Growing comfort with efficiency, unaware of what's being lost
- **Sensory Anchor**: The quiet of the empty office, the fading light through industrial windows
