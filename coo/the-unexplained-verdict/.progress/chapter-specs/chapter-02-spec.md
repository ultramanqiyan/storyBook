### 2.3 Emotional Beats Breakdown

#### Beat 1: Morning Claims (Position: 0-20%)
- **Trigger**: New batch of claims, routine work

- **Internal Process**: *Another day. The system works.*

- **Trigger**: Multiple claims processed, all AI verdicts correct
- **Surface Reaction**: Smooth workflow, less pausing to verify
- **Internal Process**: *Why second-guess? The system is faster than me.*

- **Internal Process**: *He's right. I'm more efficient now.*

- **Sensory Anchor**: The weight of Marcus's hand on her shoulder
