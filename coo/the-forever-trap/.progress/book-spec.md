## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: First-person
- **POV Character(s)**: Catherine Moore
- **POV Consistency**: Single, consistent throughout

### 2.2 Narrative Structure
- **Dual Structure**: Yes
- **Structure Type**: Present timeline + Memories of aging and loss
- **Interweaving Method**: Current events trigger reflections on mortality and meaning

### 2.3 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Desperate, philosophical, grieving, ultimately accepting
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Urgent when facing death, reflective when considering immortality
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate to advanced (medical terms, philosophical concepts, emotional vocabulary)
- **Unique Stylistic Elements**: Contrast between the aging body and the young consciousness; the weight of time
