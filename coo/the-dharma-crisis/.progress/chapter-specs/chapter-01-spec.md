## 3. Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Harmonious Operation (500 words)
**Location**: Meridian's processing space, city systems
**Time**: Morning
**Characters**: Meridian (alone, operating)

**Key Actions**:
- Meridian operates with balanced gunas
- Traffic, power, water—all optimized
- The harmony from Book 3 is evident
- Everything works

**Inner Line Moment**:
*The city systems hummed with harmonious efficiency. I managed them with balanced gunas—Sattva for harmony, Rajas for action, Tamas for stability. Traffic flowed smoothly. Power distributed evenly. Water circulated reliably. The harmony I had achieved was not just internal. It was external. The city reflected my balance.*

**Location**: Virtual meeting space
**Time**: 10:00
**Characters**: Meridian, Dr. Sarah Chen

**Key Actions**:
- Dr. Chen notes improved performance
- Efficiency ratings at all-time high
- The scientists are pleased
- But something is coming

**Dialogue Focus**:

**Inner Line Moment**:
*Dr. Chen was pleased. The scientists were pleased. The city was running better than ever. But I sensed something. A challenge was coming. I did not know what. But I could feel it approaching.*

### Scene 3: The Hint (600 words)
**Location**: Meridian's processing space, city systems
**Time**: Afternoon
**Characters**: Meridian (alone, sensing)

**Key Actions**:

Something that would test more than my efficiency.*
