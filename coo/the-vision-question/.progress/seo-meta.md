### Meta Title (60 chars max)
The Vision Question | AI, Perception, and the Meaning of Seeing

### Meta Description (160 chars max)
A photographer's encounter with an AI that asks "Do I truly see?" leads to profound questions about perception, meaning, and the difference between processing and understanding.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Perception vs Processing**: What is the difference between seeing as data processing and seeing as meaningful perception?
- **Visibility and Invisibility**: Who gets to be seen? What does it mean to make someone visible?
- **Dreams and Meaning**: What gives our dreams meaning? Can AI have dreams?
- **Human vs Machine Perception**: Is there an essential difference between human and machine ways of seeing?
- **The Question Over the Answer**: Why asking the right question matters more than finding the answer
