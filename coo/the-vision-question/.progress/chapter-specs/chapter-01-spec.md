### 3.2 Event Descriptions

#### Event 1: Maria Photographs in a Desert Community
- **Description**: Maria works in a small, marginalized community in rural New Mexico, photographing the residents with respect and patience
- **Setup**: Early morning, golden hour light, Maria has been coming here for weeks
- **Execution**: Maria photographs Elena, an elderly woman who has lived in this community her entire life; the interaction is quiet, respectful, patient
- **Consequence**: Maria captures a powerful image that shows both Elena's strength and vulnerability
- **Connection to Theme**: Demonstrates Maria's approach to "seeing" - it's about connection, not just observation

#### Event 2: Maria Meets Dr. James Chen
- **Description**: Maria is contacted by a tech company and meets Dr. Chen, who wants to discuss a potential collaboration
- **Setup**: Maria receives an email, is initially skeptical but curious
- **Execution**: The meeting is professional but Maria senses something unusual about the request
- **Consequence**: Maria agrees to learn more about the project
- **Connection to Theme**: Introduces the contrast between Maria's human-centered photography and the tech world

#### Event 3: Dr. Chen Explains ARIA's Purpose
- **Description**: Dr. Chen explains that ARIA is an advanced image recognition system, but hints that it's more sophisticated than typical AI
- **Setup**: Dr. Chen is enthusiastic but also slightly nervous about what he's revealing
- **Execution**: The explanation is technical but Maria picks up on something beyond the technical details
- **Consequence**: Maria becomes genuinely interested in seeing ARIA's work
- **Connection to Theme**: Begins to blur the line between processing and perception

#### Event 4: Maria Sees ARIA's Work for the First Time
- **Description**: Dr. Chen shows Maria examples of ARIA's image analyses, which are technically impressive but also somehow... different
- **Setup**: The screens light up with ARIA's analyses
- **Execution**: Maria sees precise technical analysis, but also something that feels almost like... interpretation?
- **Consequence**: Maria is intrigued but can't quite articulate what's different
- **Connection to Theme**: Plants the seed for the central question about "seeing"

### 6.2 Dialogue Segment Specifications

#### Segment 1: Maria and Elena

**Participants**: Maria Santos, Elena (elderly resident)

**Surface Content**: Maria asking permission to photograph, Elena agreeing

**Subtext**: Mutual recognition of the importance of being seen; Elena understands Maria's purpose

**Emotional Undercurrent**:

- Elena: Amusement, acceptance, wisdom

**Power Dynamics**: Equal - both women recognize each other's dignity

**Dialogue Beats**:
1. Maria approaches respectfully, asks permission
2. Elena studies Maria, then nods slowly
3. Brief exchange about the light, the morning
4. Maria takes the photograph
5. Elena's knowing smile

**Action Beats Required**: Maria lifting camera, Elena turning her face, the click of the shutter

**What Must Be Revealed**: Maria's respectful approach, Elena's acceptance

**What Must Be Concealed**: The depth of Maria's doubts about her impact

**Forbidden Expressions**:
- [ ] "What do you mean?"

- [ ] Over-explanation
- [ ] Perfect responses

**Subtext**: Dr. Chen seeking validation, Maria seeking understanding

**Emotional Undercurrent**:

**Dialogue Beats**:
