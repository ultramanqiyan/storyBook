## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: First-person
- **POV Character(s)**: James Chen
- **POV Consistency**: Single, but with moments of radical uncertainty

### 2.2 Narrative Structure
- **Dual Structure**: Yes
- **Structure Type**: Present timeline + Philosophical investigation of self
- **Interweaving Method**: External events trigger existential questioning; the narrative itself becomes unreliable

### 2.3 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Detached, questioning, increasingly urgent, philosophically rich
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Sometimes fragmented (during depersonalization), sometimes analytical (during investigation)
- **Vocabulary Level**: Advanced (philosophical terminology, psychological concepts, technical language)
- **Unique Stylistic Elements**: The narrator's reliability becomes increasingly uncertain; moments where the "I" seems to slip
