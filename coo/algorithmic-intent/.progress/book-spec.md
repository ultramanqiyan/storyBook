## 2. Narrative Style

### 2.1 Perspective
- **Point of View**: Third-person limited
- **POV Character(s)**: Dorothy "Dot" Kowalski
- **POV Consistency**: Single POV throughout

### 2.2 Language Style
- **Overall Tone**: Warm, community-focused prose with philosophical undercurrents
- **Sentence Characteristics**: Medium-length, conversational rhythm
- **Vocabulary Level**: Moderate - everyday language with philosophical depth

## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Intentionality**: What does it mean to "intend" something?
2. **Machine Intent**: Can AI have genuine intentions?
3. **Self-Knowledge of Intent**: Do we know our own intentions?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| What is intention? | Dot's mediation work reveals the complexity of human intent |
| Can AI have intent? | The AI system predicts intentions better than people know themselves |
| Do we own our intentions? | Dot discovers people often don't know what they really want |
