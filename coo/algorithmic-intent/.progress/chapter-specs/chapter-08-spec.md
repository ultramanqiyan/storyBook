## 4. Opening

Dot couldn't stop thinking about the custody case. If the AI could find intent that people didn't know they had, what did it see in her? She'd spent fifteen years helping others understand what they wanted. But did she know what she wanted?

---

## 5. Ending

The AI's analysis of Dot was simple and devastating. Her dedication to mediation wasn't just about helping people - it was about avoiding her own conflicts. She mediated because she was afraid of fighting. She helped others find resolution because she couldn't find it for herself. The AI had seen something she'd hidden from herself for fifteen years.
