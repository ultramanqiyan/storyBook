## 4. Opening

The Martinez family dispute was supposed to be about inheritance. But when Dot ran the AI analysis, it identified something else entirely. The eldest son didn't want the money - he wanted his father's approval. The father didn't want to keep the property - he wanted to feel needed. The AI had found the real intent beneath the stated positions.

---

## 5. Ending

Dot drove home that night with a question she couldn't shake. If the AI could see what people really wanted better than they could articulate it themselves, what did that mean for the concept of intent? Did people know what they wanted? Or was intent something that had to be discovered - by others, by algorithms, by anyone except the person themselves?
