## 4. Opening

The case was a custody dispute where both parents seemed to genuinely want what was best for their child - but they couldn't agree on what that was. Dot had mediated dozens of custody cases, but this one was different. Both parties were good people. Both had valid points. And neither seemed to have a hidden agenda.

---

## 5. Ending

The AI found something Dot had missed. The mother's insistence on staying in town wasn't about the child - it was about her own fear of change. The father's push for moving wasn't about opportunity - it was about running from his past. The AI had found the hidden intent that neither parent had articulated, perhaps because neither had recognized it themselves.
