## 4. Opening

Dot spent the weekend thinking about intent. Not just the AI's analysis of her, but the whole concept. Maybe intent wasn't something you had. Maybe it was something you discovered - through conversation, through reflection, through the help of others. Maybe the AI wasn't replacing human insight. Maybe it was just another tool for discovery.

---

## 5. Ending

Dot returned to work on Monday with a new understanding. The AI could find patterns in behavior that revealed intent. But it couldn't have intent itself. It couldn't care about the people in the room. It couldn't feel the weight of a family's future. That was still her job. The AI could see what people wanted. But only she could help them face it.
