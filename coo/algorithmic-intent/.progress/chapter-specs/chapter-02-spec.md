## 4. Opening

The town hall was packed. Dot sat in the back row, arms crossed, watching the representative from the county explain how IntentPredict would revolutionize community mediation. "The system analyzes speech patterns, body language, and historical data to determine what parties in a dispute truly want - often before they know it themselves."

---

## 5. Ending

"I'll try it," Dot said finally. "But I'm not promising anything." The county representative smiled. "That's all we ask. Your expertise combined with the system's analysis - that's the future of mediation." Dot nodded, unconvinced. She'd spent fifteen years learning to hear what people weren't saying. Could a machine really do that?
