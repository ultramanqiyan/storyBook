## 4. Opening

The Petersons had been fighting about their driveway for six months. Dot had mediated dozens of property disputes, and she knew the pattern: the issue was never really about the fence or the tree or the boundary line. It was about something deeper. But as she listened to the AI's analysis, she realized it had found the deeper issue faster than she ever had.

---

## 5. Ending

The Petersons left with a handshake and a plan. Dot sat alone in the community center, looking at the AI's analysis on her screen. It had identified that Mrs. Peterson didn't really care about the driveway - she cared about being heard. Mr. Peterson didn't really want to win - he wanted respect. The AI had seen it in minutes. It had taken Dot years to develop that instinct.
