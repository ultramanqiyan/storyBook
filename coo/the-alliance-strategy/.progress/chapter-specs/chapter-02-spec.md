## 2. Emotional Arc

### 2.1 Emotional Journey
```
Opening: Continued confidence despite doubts
    ↓
Middle: Multiple missed opportunities
    ↓
Closing: Confrontation with real limits
```

### 2.2 Emotional Depth Design

**Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**:
- Morgan continues solo approach
- More opportunities arise and are declined
- Business remains stable but stagnant

**Layer 2 - Internal Activity**:
- Growing frustration with limitations
- Comparison to competitors who collaborate
- Defensive justification of approach

**Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**:
- Fear of being dependent on others
- Past trauma from failed partnership
- Identity crisis if independence questioned
