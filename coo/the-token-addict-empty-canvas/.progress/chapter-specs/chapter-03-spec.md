## 4. Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Routine (500 words)
**Location**: Nexus Technologies, Marcus's office
**Time**: Morning

**Key Events**:
- Marcus's established routine: brief → CreateFlow → select → present
- The smooth efficiency of the process
- No struggle, no creative blocks, just selection
- The productivity metrics are excellent
- The comfort of the routine

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: CreateFlow interface, generated designs
- Auditory: Keyboard sounds, smooth workflow
- Internal: Comfort, efficiency, the smooth routine

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "This is how design works now. Efficient. Effective."
- CreateFlow (output): "Design options generated. Confidence: 92%."
- Marcus (internal): "No creative blocks. No dead ends. Just results."

### Scene 2: The Team Dynamic (600 words)
**Location**: Design studio
**Time**: Mid-morning

**Key Events**:
- The team has adapted to Marcus's AI-assisted style
- They bring him briefs, he returns designs quickly
- The expectation: fast turnaround, AI-polished results
- A junior designer asks about creative process
- Marcus's response: "The AI does the heavy lifting"
- The normalization of AI-first creativity
- The junior's confusion: "But what about your own ideas?"

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Studio, team interactions
- Auditory: Discussion, Marcus's confident responses
- Internal: Comfort, slight defensiveness

**Character Voices**:
- Junior designer: "Marcus, how do you approach a new design challenge?"
- Marcus: "I start with CreateFlow. Generate options. Select the strongest."
- Junior: "But what about your own ideas? Your creative vision?"
- Marcus: "My vision is in the selection. The curation."
- Junior: "But... that's not really creating, is it?"
- Marcus: "It's how design works now. You'll understand when you're leading projects."
- Marcus (internal): "Why does that question bother me?"

### Scene 3: The Forgotten Skill (600 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: Afternoon

**Key Events**:
- A brief comes in, Marcus opens CreateFlow automatically
- For a moment, he considers designing it himself
- He picks up a pencil, stares at the blank paper
- Nothing comes - no ideas, no inspiration
- The realization: he can't remember how to start
- The panic: "Have I lost the ability to create?"
- He returns to CreateFlow, the panic subsides

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Blank paper, pencil, CreateFlow interface
- Tactile: Pencil in hand, the blank page
- Internal: Panic, the creative void

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "Let me try to sketch something first. Just to see if I can."
- Marcus (staring at blank paper): ...
- Marcus (internal): "Nothing. There's nothing there."
- Marcus (internal): "Where did my ideas go? Where did my creativity go?"
- Marcus (returning to CreateFlow): "Forget it. This is faster anyway."
- Marcus (internal): "But why can't I think of anything on my own?"

### Scene 4: The Sarah Observation (500 words)
**Location**: Home, dinner
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Sarah notices something different in Marcus
- "You seem... less engaged with your work lately"
- Marcus denies it: "I'm more productive than ever"
- Sarah: "But do you enjoy it? Do you feel creative?"
- Marcus's honest moment: "I don't know what creative feels like anymore"
- The weight of the admission
- Sarah's concern

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Dinner table, Sarah's face
- Auditory: Sarah's voice, dinner sounds
- Internal: The weight of the admission

**Character Voices**:
- Sarah: "You seem different lately. Less... engaged with your work."
- Marcus: "I'm more productive than ever. The numbers are great."
- Sarah: "But do you enjoy it? Do you feel creative?"
- Marcus: "I feel... efficient. Productive."
- Sarah: "That's not what I asked."
- Marcus: "I don't know what creative feels like anymore. I don't know if I ever did."
- Sarah: "That's... sad, Marcus. That's really sad."
- Marcus (internal): "Is it? Or is it just the new reality?"

### Scene 5: The Night Reflection (400 words)
**Location**: Home, bedroom
**Time**: Night

**Key Events**:
- Marcus lies awake, thinking about the day
- The junior's question: "That's not really creating, is it?"
- Sarah's concern: "That's really sad"
- The blank paper: the creative void
- The question: "What have I become?"
- The rationalization: "I'm productive. I'm successful. Isn't that enough?"
- The doubt: "Or have I lost something essential?"

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Dark room, ceiling
- Auditory: Silence, Sarah's breathing
- Internal: Doubt, the creative question

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "The junior asked if I'm really creating. Am I?"
- Marcus (internal): "Sarah said it's sad. Is she right?"
- Marcus (internal): "I couldn't think of a single idea on my own today."
- Marcus (internal): "When did I lose that? When did I stop being able to create?"
- Marcus (internal): "Or maybe I never could. Maybe the AI just revealed the truth."
- Marcus (internal): "Tomorrow, I'll try again. I'll try to create something on my own."
