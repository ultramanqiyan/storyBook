## Plot Summary

James discovers that Elena's avatar has already iterated three times—she has died and been restored three times since her original death. Each iteration has changed her, She's no longer just his wife's copy; She's something evolved.

3. Reads iteration logs
4. Understands how she's changed
5. Confronts what this means
6. Questions about identity and love
