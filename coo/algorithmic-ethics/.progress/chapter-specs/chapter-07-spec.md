## 3. Opening
Marie spent weeks thinking about what had happened. The AI had made the optimal decision. But optimal wasn't right. The question was: why? What was missing from the calculation? What couldn't be quantified?

---

## 4. Ending
Marie realized that ethics wasn't about optimization. It was about values - and values couldn't be reduced to numbers. The AI could maximize efficiency, but it couldn't decide what efficiency was for. That was still a human decision. And maybe that was the point.
