## 4. Thematic Depth

### 4.1 Core Themes
1. **Moral Decision-Making**: Can ethics be computed?
2. **Utilitarianism**: Is "optimal" the same as "right"?
3. **Community Ethics**: What can't be quantified?

### 4.2 Philosophical Exploration
| Question | Exploration Approach |
|----------|---------------------|
| Can AI make moral decisions? | Marie's garden project uses AI for resource allocation |
| Is optimal right? | AI decisions are "optimal" but feel wrong |
| What can't be quantified? | Marie discovers the unmeasurable aspects of community |
