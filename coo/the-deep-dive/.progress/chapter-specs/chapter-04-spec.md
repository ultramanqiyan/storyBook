## 2. Emotional Arc

### 2.1 Emotional Journey
```
Opening: Attempting to implement deep work
    ↓
Middle: Strong resistance, rationalization
    ↓
Closing: Acknowledgment of fear
```

### 2.2 Emotional Depth Design

**Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**:
- Jamie tries to implement deep work practices
- Fails repeatedly, gives in to distractions
- Rationalizes each failure

**Layer 2 - Internal Activity**:
- Anxiety about missing important messages
- Fear of being seen as unavailable
- Pride fighting against need for change

**Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**:
- Fear of irrelevance if not constantly connected
- Identity crisis around "always on" persona
- Deeper fear of what silence might reveal
