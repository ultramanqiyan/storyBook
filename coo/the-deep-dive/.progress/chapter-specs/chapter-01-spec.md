## 2. Emotional Arc

### 2.1 Emotional Journey
```
Opening: Frenetic energy, false productivity
    ↓
Middle: Growing awareness of fragmentation
    ↓
Closing: First hint of dissatisfaction
```

### 2.2 Emotional Depth Design

**Layer 1 - Surface Reaction**:
- Jamie checks phone constantly
- Feels busy but unproductive
- Responds to every notification immediately

**Layer 2 - Internal Activity**:
- Racing thoughts about missing something
- Anxiety about falling behind
- Pride in being "always available"

**Layer 3 - Deep Motivation**:
- Fear of being forgotten
- Need for validation through responsiveness
- Deeper fear of missing opportunities

### 3.1 Event Sequence
