## Premise

Alex Chen thought landing a job at Meridian Tech - Silicon Valley's most prestigious company - was the beginning of their dream career. But beneath the polished surface of innovation and "company family" culture lies a sophisticated web of psychological manipulation. As Alex rises through the ranks, they begin to question everything: their abilities, their worth, and ultimately, their sanity. When they discover they're not the only one being broken down and rebuilt, they must decide whether to play the game or burn it all down.

## Core Concepts

### World Setting

**Meridian Tech** is a fictional Silicon Valley giant - a company that prides itself on "disrupting industries" and "changing the world." With its sprawling campus, gourmet cafeterias, and legendary perks, it's the dream destination for ambitious young professionals. But behind the free kombucha and meditation rooms, a darker reality exists.

### The PUA Playbook

Corporate PUA (Pick-Up Artist) tactics in the workplace:

| Tactic | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Love Bombing** | Excessive praise and attention during recruitment and onboarding |
| **Negging** | Subtle criticism designed to undermine confidence |
| **Gaslighting** | Denying reality to make employees question their perception |
| **Isolation** | Cutting employees off from support networks |
| **Intermittent Reinforcement** | Unpredictable rewards to create dependency |
| **Moving Goalposts** | Constantly changing expectations to ensure failure |

### The Truth

Meridian Tech doesn't just hire employees - it breaks them down and rebuilds them as "Meridian People." The company's success depends on a workforce that's too insecure to leave, too exhausted to question, and too invested to see the manipulation.
