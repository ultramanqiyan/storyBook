### Scene 2: The Team Meeting (700 words)
**Location**: Team meeting room
**Time**: Mid-morning

**Key Events**:
- Marcus leads a team meeting differently
- He deflects technical questions more openly
- "Let me research that and get back to you" - more honest
- He credits AI tools for some suggestions
- The team notices the shift
- Alex's confusion: "Is Marcus okay?"
- The tension between honesty and leadership perception

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Meeting room, team faces
- Auditory: Discussion, Marcus's different tone
- Internal: The mix of relief and anxiety

**Character Voices**:
- Team member: "Marcus, what's the best approach for this API?"
- Marcus: "I don't have an immediate answer. Let me look into it."
- Alex: "That's... unusual. You usually have an answer right away."
- Marcus: "I'm trying a different approach. Being more thoughtful."
- Alex (expression): Confusion, slight concern
- Team member: "Okay... let us know what you find."

### Scene 3: The One-on-One (600 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: Afternoon

**Key Events**:
- Alex asks to speak with Marcus privately
- Alex expresses concern: "You seem different lately"
- Marcus considers being honest
- He deflects: "Just trying a new management style"
- The tension of almost telling the truth
- Alex's lingering doubt

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Office, Alex's face
- Auditory: Alex's concerned voice
- Internal: The temptation to be honest, the fear

**Character Voices**:
- Alex: "Marcus, can I talk to you for a minute?"
- Marcus: "Of course. What's on your mind?"
- Alex: "You seem... different lately. Less certain. Is everything okay?"
- Marcus: "I'm fine. Just trying a different approach to leadership."
- Alex: "It's just... you used to have all the answers. Now you're asking us more."
- Marcus: "That's intentional. I want to empower the team."
- Alex: "Okay. If you're sure."
- Marcus (internal): "I almost told him. I almost told him the truth."

**Location**: Jennifer's office
**Time**: Late afternoon

**Key Events**:
- Jennifer asks about Marcus's performance
- She notices the change in his approach
- She seems cautiously approving: "You're delegating more"
- But also: "I still need you to be the technical authority"
- The tension: he can't be what she needs
- He nods, the lie continues

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Jennifer's face, office
- Auditory: Jennifer's voice
- Internal: The weight of the expectation

**Character Voices**:

- Marcus: "I'm trying to empower the team."

- Marcus: "I understand."
- Marcus (internal): "But I can't be that anymore. I can't be what you need."
