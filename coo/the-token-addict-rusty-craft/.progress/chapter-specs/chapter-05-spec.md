## 4. Scene Breakdown

### Scene 1: The Preparation (500 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: Morning, before the interview

**Key Events**:
- Marcus prepares interview questions using AI
- He reviews the candidate's resume
- He tries to anticipate technical questions
- The anxiety of not knowing what he doesn't know
- He tells himself he's prepared

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Resume on screen, AI-generated questions
- Auditory: Office sounds, his own breathing
- Internal: Anxiety, false confidence, underlying fear

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I've done hundreds of interviews"
- ThinkTank (output): "Suggested questions for senior engineer role..."
- Marcus (internal): "These look good. I'll be fine."

### Scene 2: The Interview (1000 words)
**Location**: Interview room
**Time**: Mid-morning

**Key Events**:
- Marcus meets the candidate, David Chen (no relation to Alex)
- David is technically sharp, asks deep questions
- Marcus asks his prepared questions but can't evaluate answers
- David gives a technically sophisticated answer
- Marcus nods, pretending to understand
- David asks a follow-up that Marcus can't answer
- The awkward moment of deflection
- David's subtle look of confusion

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: David's face, interview room, whiteboard
- Auditory: David's voice, Marcus's uncertain responses
- Tactile: Sweaty palms, tension in chair
- Internal: Panic, shame, the performance

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus: "Can you describe your approach to distributed systems?"
- David: "I prefer event sourcing with CQRS for scalability. The key is handling eventual consistency at the application layer..."
- Marcus (internal): "Is that... right? I can't tell anymore."
- Marcus: "Interesting approach. Can you elaborate on..."
- David: "Sure. The main challenge is idempotency in message handlers. How do you handle that in your current system?"
- Marcus: "We... use a standard pattern. Let's move to the next question."
- David (expression): Confusion, slight concern

### Scene 3: The Decision (400 words)
**Location**: Marcus's office
**Time**: After the interview

**Key Events**:
- Marcus must make a hire/no-hire decision
- He can't evaluate David's technical depth
- He uses AI to analyze the interview notes
- The AI suggests "hire" but Marcus isn't sure
- He makes a decision based on gut, not knowledge
- The hollow feeling of not knowing

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Interview notes, AI analysis
- Auditory: Silence, keyboard
- Internal: Uncertainty, shame, the weight of responsibility

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "Was he good? I can't tell."
- AI analysis: "Candidate shows strong technical depth. Recommendation: Hire."
- Marcus (internal): "But how would I know if that's wrong?"

### Scene 4: The Questioning (500 words)
**Location**: Jennifer's office
**Time**: Afternoon

**Key Events**:
- Jennifer asks about the interview
- She questions Marcus's evaluation
- "You seemed uncertain about his technical depth"
- Marcus deflects, but Jennifer presses
- The fear of being exposed
- Jennifer's subtle concern about Marcus's technical leadership

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Jennifer's face, office setting
- Auditory: Jennifer's voice, Marcus's careful responses
- Internal: Fear, defensiveness, the close call

**Character Voices**:
- Jennifer: "How did the interview go?"
- Marcus: "Good. Strong candidate."
- Jennifer: "You seemed... hesitant on some of his technical answers."
- Marcus: "Just thinking through the fit for our team."
- Jennifer: "Marcus, you're our technical lead. I need you to be confident in these evaluations."
- Marcus: "I am. I'll send my recommendation."

### Scene 5: The Aftermath (300 words)
**Location**: Marcus's car, then home
**Time**: Evening

**Key Events**:
- Marcus drives home, replaying the interview
- The shame of not being able to do his job
- He tells Sarah about the "successful" interview
- The lie grows: he's now lying to himself and others
- The fear: what if the next interview is worse?

**Sensory Requirements**:
- Visual: Road, home lights
- Auditory: Car sounds, Sarah's voice
- Internal: Shame, fear, the weight of deception

**Character Voices**:
- Marcus (internal): "I can't keep doing this. But what choice do I have?"
- Sarah: "How was your day?"
- Marcus: "Good. Interviewed a strong candidate."
- Marcus (internal): "I don't even know if he was strong."
