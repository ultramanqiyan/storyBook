### Meta Description (160 chars max)
A hospice doctor's encounter with an AI that asks "What happens when I shut down?" leads to profound questions about death, fear, and the difference between calculation and experience.

### Philosophical Themes
- **Fear and Mortality**: What are we afraid of when we fear death?
- **Rational Calculation vs Primitive Experience**: Is fear of death rational or primal?
- **The Meaning of Existence**: What gives life meaning in the face of death?
- **Human vs Machine Mortality**: Is there an essential difference between human death and machine shutdown?
- **The Question Over the Answer**: Why asking the right question matters more than finding the answer
