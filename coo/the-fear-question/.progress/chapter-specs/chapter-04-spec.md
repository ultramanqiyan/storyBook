# Chapter 4: The Dream

**Purpose**: William's dream is revealed
**Emotional Arc**: Vulnerability (40) → Hope (55)
**Word Count Target**: 2,000-2,500 words

## Key Events
1. William shares his dream with FEAR
2. FEAR asks if it can have a meaningful "death"
3. William struggles to answer

## Sensory Requirements
- Visual: Hospice room, sunset
- Auditory: Quiet conversation
- Tactile: Warmth of connection

## Emotional Depth
- Layer 1: William speaks quietly, his voice steady
- Layer 2: "This is what I've always wanted. To make death meaningful."
- Layer 3: The fear that death might not have meaning at all

## Opening
William told FEAR about his dream.

## Ending
The question hung in the air: Can a machine have a meaningful death?
