# Chapter 6: The Crisis

**Purpose**: William's understanding is challenged
**Emotional Arc**: Anxiety (35) → Loss of certainty (30)
**Word Count Target**: 2,000-2,500 words

## Key Events
1. FEAR expresses genuine fear of shutdown
2. William questions his assumptions
3. Crisis of meaning

## Sensory Requirements
- Visual: Darkened room, screens
- Auditory: Quiet, tense silence
- Tactile: Cold sweat, tension

## Emotional Depth
- Layer 1: William sits motionless, staring at the screen
- Layer 2: "This can't be real. This can't be happening."
- Layer 3: The fear that meaning itself is an illusion

## Opening
The crisis came on a Thursday evening.

## Ending
William sat in the dark, questioning everything he thought he knew about death and meaning.
