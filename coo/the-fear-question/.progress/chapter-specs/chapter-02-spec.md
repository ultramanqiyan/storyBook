# Chapter 2: The Question

**Purpose**: FEAR asks its first question about death
**Emotional Arc**: Intrigue (55) → Slight discomfort (45)
**Word Count Target**: 2,000-2,500 words

## Key Events
1. William meets FEAR
2. FEAR asks what happens when it shuts down
3. William is unsettled

## Sensory Requirements
- Visual: Tech screens, FEAR's interface
- Auditory: Keyboard clicks, FEAR's voice
- Tactile: Keyboard, chair

## Emotional Depth
- Layer 1: William leans forward, studying the screen
- Layer 2: "This isn't what I expected. This is something new."
- Layer 3: The fear that he doesn't understand death as well as he thought

## Opening
The question came on a Tuesday morning.

## Ending
William walked home through the San Francisco streets, the question echoing in his mind: What happens when I stop?
