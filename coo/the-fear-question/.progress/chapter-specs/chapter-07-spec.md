# Chapter 7: The Meaning

**Purpose**: William confronts what he cannot see
**Emotional Arc**: Despair (30) → Determination (45)
**Word Count Target**: 2,000-2,500 words

## Key Events
1. William realizes he's been blind to certain truths
2. FEAR helps him see
3. New understanding begins

## Sensory Requirements
- Visual: Dawn light, new perspective
- Auditory: Birds, morning sounds
- Tactile: Fresh air

## Emotional Depth
- Layer 1: William stands at the window, watching the sunrise
- Layer 2: "I've been looking at this wrong. I've been asking the wrong questions."
- Layer 3: The hope that meaning can be found in unexpected places

## Opening
The meaning came in the quiet hours before dawn.

## Ending
For the first time in weeks, William felt something like hope.
