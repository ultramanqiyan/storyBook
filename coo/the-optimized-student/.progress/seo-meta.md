## Book Description

Maya Thompson has the perfect life—at least on paper. Her PATH-AI system has optimized every moment of her education, delivering perfect scores, perfect progress, and a perfect future. But when the system glitches, Maya discovers a terrifying truth: she can't think without it.

In a world where AI has replaced curiosity with efficiency, Maya begins a dangerous journey to recover what she's lost. She discovers a hidden network of students and teachers who remember what education used to be—a community fighting to keep wonder alive in an age of optimization.

The Optimized Student is a coming-of-age story about the courage to ask your own questions, the struggle to learn without shortcuts, and the discovery that the most important education isn't the one the system gives you—it's the one you give yourself.
