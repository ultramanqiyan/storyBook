Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to StoryBook. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered interactive storytelling platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our service:

  • Account Information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Story Content: Stories, chapters, and characters you create using our platform
  • Preferences: Language and theme preferences stored locally on your device

How We Store Information

Your data is stored securely using the following methods:

  • Account Data: Your email, username, and story content are stored on our secure servers
  • Local Storage: Language preference, theme preference, and login status are stored in your browser's localStorage

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our storytelling services
  • Process your requests and respond to your inquiries
  • Send you technical notices and support messages

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and the safety of users

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
